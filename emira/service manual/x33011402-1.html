Parking Brake Caliper - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 33.01.14-02

    Removal

  1. Remove parking brake pads. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect electrical connector.
  3. Remove M10x95 bolts (x2) securing parking brake caliper to hub. Torque 50 Nm.
  4. Remove parking brake calliper.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Clean permabond A130 of bolt threads and hub carrier threads.
  3. Apply permabond A130 to bolt threads.
