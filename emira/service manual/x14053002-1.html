Steering Column Shroud - Lower

Print

Operation Code: 14.05.30-02

    Removal

  1. Remove steering column shroud - upper. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove trim covers (x2) from lower steering column shroud.
  3. Remove 4x16 self tapping screws (x3) securing the lower steering column shroud to steering column.
  4. Lower adjustable steering column lever.
  5. Disengage lower steering column shroud from steering column gaiter.
  6. Refer to illustration for trim clip type and location.
  7. Remove lower steering column shroud from vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
