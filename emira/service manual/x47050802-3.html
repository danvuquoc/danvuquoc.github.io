Crank Assembly - Shift And Select - V6

Print

Operation Code: 47.05.08-02

    Removal

  1. Remove airbox assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disengage clip securing shift cable to selector assembly.
  3. Remove shift cable from selector assembly.
  4. Remove M5 nut and washer securing cable to selector assembly.

    5. NOTE: Record position and quantity of washers.

  5. Remove shift cable from selector assembly.
  6. Remove bolts (x2) securing crank assembly to selector assembly.
  7. Remove crank assembly with plastic bushing.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
