Hub Carrier - Front - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 31.01.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove brake disc - front. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove wheel speed sensor - front. Refer to procedure.
  3. Loosen but do not fully remove nut securing track rod end to hub carrier.

    4. NOTE: Loosen the ball joint nut until the locking threads are clear of the ball joint pin threads.

  4. Use ball joint splitter to separate track rod end from hub carrier.
  5. Remove and discard nut securing track rod end from hub carrier. Torque 86 Nm.
  6. Loosen but do not fully remove nut securing upper wishbone ball joint to hub carrier.

    7. NOTE: Loosen the ball joint nut until the locking threads are clear of the ball joint pin threads.

  7. Use ball joint splitter to separate upper wishbone ball joint from hub carrier.
  8. Remove and discard nut securing upper wishbone ball joint from hub carrier. Torque 86 Nm.
  9. Loosen but do not fully remove nut securing lower wishbone ball joint to hub carrier.

    10. NOTE: Loosen the ball joint nut until the locking threads are clear of the ball joint pin threads

  10. Use ball joint splitter to separate lower wishbone ball joint from hub carrier.
  11. Remove and discard nut securing lower wishbone ball joint from hub carrier. Torque 86 Nm.
  12. Remove hub carrier assembly from vehicle.

    13. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  13. Remove M6x16 bolt securing the wheel speed sensor harness bracket to hub carrier and swap to replacement component. Torque 8 Nm.
  14. Remove M10x30 bolts and washers (x4) securing wheel bearing assembly to hub carrier. Torque 70 Nm.

    15. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew all lock nut fasteners that have been removed during removal procedure.
