Carpet - Luggage Compartment - Front

Print

Operation Code: 15.24.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove luggage compartment floor carpet. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove battery cover. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove M6 nut and trim clips (x4) securing luggage compartment front carpet to bootbox.
  4. Remove luggage compartment front carpet.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
