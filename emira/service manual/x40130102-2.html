Primary Coolant Pump - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.13.01-02

Important information

WARNING: Risk of scalding. Allow coolant to cool completely before proceeding.

WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    Removal

  1. Remove primary coolant pump housing assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove screws (11) (x2) securing primary coolant pump (12) to engine. Torque 9 Nm.
  3. Remove primary coolant pump.

    4. NOTE: Remove and discard seal.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.

    2. NOTE: Renew discarded seal.
