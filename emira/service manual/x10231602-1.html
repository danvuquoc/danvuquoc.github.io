Bumper - Front

Print

Operation Code: 10.23.16-02

    Removal

  1. Open access hatch - front. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove wheelarch liner - front - left side. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove wheelarch liner - front - right side.

    4. NOTE: Procedure is the same for left side component.

  4. Remove undertray - front. Refer to procedure.
  5. Apply tape to protect paintwork of adjoining panels before removing the bumper.
  6. Remove M6x16 bolts (x8) securing bumper to upper bumper support. Torque 6 Nm.
  7. Remove M6x16 bolt with washer securing bumper to left side reinforcement bracket.
  8. Remove M6x20 bolts with washers (x3) securing bumper to left side reinforcement bracket.
  9. Remove screw securing bumper to reinforcement left side.
  10. Remove M6x16 bolt with washer securing bumper to right side reinforcement bracket.
  11. Remove M6x20 bolts with washers (x3) securing bumper to right side reinforcement bracket.
  12. Remove screw securing bumper to reinforcement right side.
  13. Remove M6x16 bolts (x4) securing bumper to air duct bracket.
  14. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from left and right side front parking sensors.
  15. Disengage clips (x4) below headlamp area left and right sides.
  16. Using assistance, pull bumper forward from mounts and away from vehicle.

    17. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  17. Remove screws (x2) securing licence plate to licence plate bracket and remove licence plate.
  18. Remove M6x16 bolts with washers (x4) securing licence plate bracket to plinth.
  19. Remove licence plate bracket from vehicle.
  20. Remove screws (x5) securing plinth to bumper.
  21. Remove plinth from vehicle.
  22. Remove fasteners (x12) securing wind deflector to front bumper and remove wind deflector from front bumper.
  23. Disengage clips (x12) securing centre grille to bumper and remove centre grille.
  24. Disengage clips (x14) securing centre grille frame to bumper and remove centre grille frame.
  25. Remove screws (x6) securing LH lower grille to bumper and remove left side lower grille.
  26. Remove screws (x2) and clips (x6) securing LH lower grille frame to bumper and remove left side lower grille frame.
  27. Repeat steps 26 and 27 for right side of bumper.
  28. Remove bolts (x4) securing lower bumper to bumper.
  29. Disengage lower bumper from bumper and remove lower bumper.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. If you install plinth to a new bumper do the following procedure:
  3. With masking tape to protect front bumper, measure and mark the centre of the front bumper as shown.
  4. Position plinth on bumper so centre of plinth aligns with centre mark of bumper and profile of plinth engages with edge of bumper.
  5. Use the plinth as a template to mark the fixing hole positions on the bumper.
  6. Remove plinth from bumper and pre-drill fixing hole positions.
  7. Continue to install plinth to bumper as reverse of removal procedure.
  8. Check fender gap and flush tolerances - see Alignment Specifications for Body Panels and Closures section.
