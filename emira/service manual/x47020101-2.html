Transmission Assembly - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 47.02.01-01

Important Information

WARNING: Transmission oil is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested. Wear protective gloves, protective clothing and safety glasses.

    Removal

  1. Remove engine assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove starter motor. Refer to procedure.
  3. Install lifting device (7) to transmission (1).
  4. Attach a suitable hoist to lifting device and slightly raise transmission.
  5. Remove bolts (x2) securing transmission to engine. Torque 39 Nm.
  6. Remove bolts (x3) securing transmission to engine. Torque 39 Nm.
  7. Remove bolts (x2) securing transmission to engine. Torque 39 Nm.
  8. Remove bolts (x3) securing transmission to engine. Torque 39 Nm.
  9. Remove stud securing transmission to engine. Torque 39 Nm.
  10. Using assistance remove transmission assembly.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Inspect dowel pins for damage, renew if necessary.
  3. Apply grease to teeth of driveshaft.
