Auxiliary Radiator Assembly

Print

Operation Code: 46.01.41-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front bumper. Refer to procedure.
  2. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove scrivets (x6) and remove front air duct.
  4. Disengage hose clamp and remove hose secured to radiator.

    5. WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain coolant into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  5. Remove M6x16 bolts (x6) and remove bracket. Torque 10 Nm.
  6. Remove M6x16 bolts (x9) and remove bracket from radiator. Torque 10 Nm.
  7. Disengage hose clamp and remove hose secured to radiator.
  8. Remove auxiliary radiator.

    9. Do not carry out further disassembly is removing for access only.

  9. Remove bushes (x4) from radiator.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
