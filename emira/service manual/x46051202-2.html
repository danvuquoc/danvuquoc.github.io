Hose - Inlet - Fan - Engine Bay Cooling - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 46.05.12-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Release clamp securing fan inlet hose to intake vent.
  3. Release clamp securing fan inlet hose to fan assembly.
  4. Remove fan inlet hose.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
