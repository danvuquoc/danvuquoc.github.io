Heater / Air Conditioning Housing Assembly - LHD - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 18.01.01-02

    Removal

  1. Depressurise air conditioning system. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  3. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove front fender left side. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove front fender right side.

    6. NOTE: Procedure is the same as for left side component.

  6. Remove pollen filter. Refer to procedure.
  7. Remove cooling fan. Refer to procedure.
  8. Remove PAS pump motor. Refer to procedure.
  9. If fitted, remove water shield from HVAC unit. Refer to TSB-131-24007.
  10. Remove scrivets (x6) securing centre radiator duct to front subframe.
  11. Remove centre radiator duct.
  12. Release clips (x2) securing pipes (x2) to PAS fluid reservoir.
  13. Disconnect pipes (x2) from PAS fluid reservoir.
  14. Release PAS fluid reservoir from clip on bracket.
  15. Remove PAS fluid reservoir.
  16. Remove M5x13 self tapping screws (x2) securing external filter housing to HVAC unit.
  17. Release clips (x4) securing external filter housing to HVAC unit.

    18. CAUTION: Tabs are fragile, take care when releasing external filter housing.

  18. Remove external filter housing.
  19. Remove M6x16 bolt securing air conditioning pipe clamp plate to expansion valve. Torque 9 Nm.
  20. Remove air conditioning clamp plate.
  21. Disconnect air conditioning pipe from expansion valve.

    22. NOTE: Cap pipe and expansion valve port to prevent ingress of dirt and moisture.

    NOTE: Discard O ring.

  22. Remove M6x20 bolt from air conditioning pipe. Torque 10 Nm.
  23. Disconnect air conditioning pipe from fitting.

    24. NOTE: Cap pipe and fitting to prevent ingress of dirt and moisture.

    NOTE: Discard O ring.

  24. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing air conditioning pipe to receiver dryer and front subframe. Torque 10 Nm.
  25. Release clip from air conditioning pipe.
  26. Disconnect air conditioning pipe from receiver dryer.

    27. NOTE: Cap pipe and receiver dryer port to prevent ingress of dirt and moisture.

    NOTE: Discard O ring.

  27. Disconnect air conditioning pipe from expansion valve.

    28. NOTE: Cap pipe and expansion valve port to prevent ingress of dirt and moisture.

    NOTE: Discard O ring.

  28. Remove M6x20 bolts (x3) securing brackets (x2) to front subframe. Torque 8 Nm.
  29. Remove M6 nut securing bracket to front subframe. Torque 8 Nm.
  30. Remove brackets (x2).
  31. Rotate disconnected air conditioning pipe and move aside.
  32. Remove fusebox cover.
  33. Remove M8 nut securing battery cable to fusebox. Torque 24 Nm.
  34. Remove battery cables and move aside.
  35. Release clips (x4) securing front end harness to fusebox and front subframe.
  36. Remove M6x16 bolts (x2) securing fusebox and bracket to front subframe.
  37. Move fusebox and harnesses aside.
  38. Disconnect harness connector from HVAC unit.
  39. Release clips (x2) securing harness to window wiper mounting and HVAC unit bracket.
  40. Remove harness from mounting and move aside.
  41. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing recirculation pump to intermediate crossmember. Torque 10 Nm.
  42. Move recirculation pump aside.
  43. Remove M6x20 bolts (x3) and M6x30 bolt securing wiper motor support bracket/bar to vehicle.
  44. Remove wiper motor support bracket/bar.
  45. Remove M8x20 bolts (x2) and washers securing HVAC unit to intermediate crossmember. Torque 9 Nm.

    46. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  46. Release clips (x2) securing harness to front subframe.
  47. Remove M6x20 bolt securing power steering pipe to front crossmember. Torque 9 Nm.
  48. Remove M6x20 bolt securing air conditioning pipe to front subframe. Torque 10 Nm.
  49. Slightly loosen power steering pipe union and rotate pipe to allow pipe to pass through intermediate crossmember. Torque 45 Nm.

    50. NOTE: Do not break into the power steering system.

  50. Remove M8x25 bolts (x2) securing fender bracket to front subframe.
  51. Remove fender bracket.
  52. Remove M6x19 Torx bolts (x2) securing intermediate crossmember to front subframe.
  53. Remove M8x25 bolts (x2) securing intermediate crossmember to front subframe. Torque 24 Nm.
  54. Remove M6 nut securing earth point to front subframe. Torque 10 Nm.
  55. Release clip and remove earth point from front subframe.
  56. Remove M6x16 bolt securing coolant pipe to front subframe. Torque 10 Nm.
  57. Remove M6x30 bolt securing coolant pipes. Torque 10 Nm.
  58. Release clamp and disconnect coolant hose.
  59. Remove M6x16 bolt securing coolant pipe to bracket. Torque 10 Nm.
  60. Remove M6x16 bolt securing coolant pipe to intermediate crossmember. Torque 10 Nm.
  61. Remove M8x25 bolts (x2) securing fender bracket to front subframe.
  62. Remove fender bracket.
  63. Remove M6x19 Torx bolts (x2) securing intermediate crossmember to front subframe.
  64. Remove M8x25 bolts (x2) securing intermediate crossmember to front subframe. Torque 24 Nm.
  65. Remove M6x20 bolts (x6) securing intermediate crossmember to front subframe. Torque 8 Nm.
  66. Remove M6x20 bolts (x5) securing intermediate crossmember to front subframe. Torque 8 Nm.
  67. Lift intermediate crossmember over earth studs on left and right side of front subframe.
  68. Move intermediate crossmember forward approximately 144 mm to gain clearance for HVAC unit.
  69. Remove M10x30 bolt securing HVAC unit to front subframe. Torque 45 Nm.
  70. Release clip securing instrument panel air duct hose to HVAC unit.
  71. Disconnect instrument panel air duct hose.
  72. Remove M8x16 bolt securing HVAC unit to bulkhead. Torque 9 Nm.
  73. Release hose clamps (x2) securing heater feed and heater return hoses to HVAC unit.

    74. NOTE: Record positions of feed and return hoses.

  74. Disconnect heater feed and return hoses and install clamps to minimise coolant loss.

    75. NOTE: Use a suitable container to catch coolant.

  75. Release clips (x4) securing transition duct to HVAC unit.
  76. Remove transition duct.
  77. Using assistance remove HVAC unit.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded O rings.
  3. With AC compressor fitted, perform the following running in procedure:

    4. WARNING: The running in procedure described below must be performed so that refrigerant oil does not exit the compressor sump and flow directly into the air conditioning hoses/pipes before first circulating around the compressor, this ensures the compressors internal components are fully lubricated.

  4. Start engine.
  5. Turn on air conditioning to maximum cold setting.
  6. Allow engine to idle for 2 minutes while monitoring performance.
  7. Check for any leaks of AC refrigerant or oil.
  8. Fill power steering system. Refer to technical data.
  9. Bleed power steering system. Refer to procedure.
