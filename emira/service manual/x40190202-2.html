Alternator - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.19.02-02

    Removal

  1. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  2. Loosen auxiliary drive belt and remove from alternator pulley. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove rear bulkhead access panel. Refer to procedure.
  4. Disconnect harness connector (1) from alternator (G2).
  5. Remove cover from alternator battery cable (3).
  6. Remove nut (2) securing securing alternator battery cable to alternator. Torque 16 Nm.
  7. Disconnect battery cable from alternator and move aside.
  8. Remove bolts (4) (x2) from alternator. Torque 20 Nm.
  9. Remove alternator.

    10. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  10. Remove bolts (x3) securing belt tensioner to alternator. Torque 20 Nm.
  11. Remove belt tensioner.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
