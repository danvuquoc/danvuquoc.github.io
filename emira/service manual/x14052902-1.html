Steering Column Shroud - Upper

Print

Operation Code: 14.05.29-02

    Removal

  1. Carefully disengage clips (x4) securing steering column upper shroud to steering column lower shroud.
  2. Refer to illustration for clip type and location.
  3. Remove steering column upper shroud with steering column gaiter from vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
