Wiper Blade - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 17.21.21-02

    Removal

  1. Lift wiper arm to access wiper blade.

    2. CAUTION: Do not release wiper arm suddenly or damage can occur to windscreen.

  2. Release wiper blade clip.
  3. Remove wiper blade from wiper arm in direction shown.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. CAUTION: Carefully lower wiper arm to prevent damage to windscreen.
