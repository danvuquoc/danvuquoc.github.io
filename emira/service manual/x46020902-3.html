Water Outlet - V6

Print

Operation Code: 46.02.09-02

    Removal

  1. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disengage clip securing coolant hose to water outlet.
  3. Remove coolant hose.
  4. Remove the 2 bolts, 4 nuts and water outlet. Torque 10 Nm.
  5. Remove the 2 gaskets and O-ring.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
