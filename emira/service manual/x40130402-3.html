Thermostat Housing - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.13.04-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove thermostat. Refer to procedure.
  3. Disengage hose clamp securing thermostat housing to coolant pipe.
  4. Remove bolts (x2) securing thermostat housing to engine block. Torque 10 Nm.
  5. Remove nut securing thermostat housing to engine block. Torque 10 Nm.
  6. Remove thermostat housing.
  7. Remove thermostat housing gasket and O ring.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
