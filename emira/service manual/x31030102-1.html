Hub Carrier - Rear - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 31.03.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove brake disc rear left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove wheel speed sensor. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove and discard M24 nut securing driveshaft to wheel bearing. Torque 300Nm.
  4. Remove M14x100 bolt securing upper wishbone to hub carrier. Torque 190 Nm.
  5. Remove M14x190 bolt securing toe arm to hub carrier. Torque 135 Nm.
  6. Remove M14x100 bolt securing lower wishbone to hub carrier. Torque 190 Nm.
  7. Release and remove hub carrier from driveshaft.

    8. NOTE: Take care not to release driveshaft from gearbox.

    Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  8. Remove M10x30 bolts and washers (x4) securing wheel bearing assembly to hub carrier. Torque 70 Nm.

    9. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  9. Remove wheel bearing assembly from hub carrier.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew all discarded fixings that have been removed during removal procedure.
