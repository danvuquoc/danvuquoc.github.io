Brake Disc Caliper - Rear - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 33.01.06-02

    Removal

  1. Remove brake pads - rear - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove brake hose banjo bolt from brake caliper. Torque 30 Nm.
  3. Remove brake hose sealing washers (x2).

    4. CAUTION: Brake fluid will discharge from brake hose when removing banjo bolt. Use a container to collect brake fluid.

    CAUTION: Do not allow brake fluid to contact lower arm ball joint.

    NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  4. Plug brake hose connections.

    5. CAUTION: Plug pipe connections to prevent fluid loss and ingress of moisture or dirt.

    CAUTION: If brake fluid is spilt on the paintwork wash off immediately with clean water.

  5. Remove and discard M12x75 bolts (x2) securing brake caliper to hub carrier. Torque 86 Nm.
  6. Remove brake caliper from vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except the following:

    2. NOTE: Make sure the brake hose is free of kinks during installation.

  2. Renew brake caliper bolts.
  3. Renew banjo bolt sealing washers.
  4. Bleed brakes using Lotus Insight tool.
  5. Operate brake pedal at least 5 times to seat pads against brake disc.

    6. WARNING: Always check brake pads are seated correctly before driving the vehicle.
