Valve Assembly - Cam Timing Control - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.07.23-02

    Removal

  1. Remove supercharger. Refer to procedure.

    2. NOTE: Supercharger removal not required if only replacing RH bank 1 lower oil control valve.

  2. Remove and discard cable ties (x2) securing engine harness to oil control valve.
  3. Disconnect harness connectors (x4) secured to oil control valves.
  4. Remove bolts (x4) securing oil control valves to engine and remove locating tab. Torque 9 Nm.
  5. Remove oil control valves (x4).

    6. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  6. Remove O-rings (x4) from oil control valves.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew O-rings if they are cracked or pinched.
  3. Apply light coat of oil to new oil control valve O-rings (x4).
  4. Replace discarded cable ties.
