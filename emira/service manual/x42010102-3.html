Airbox Assembly - V6

Print

Operation Code: 42.01.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect Mass Air Flow (MAF) sensor connector.
  3. Loosen hose clamps (x2) securing intake tube to throttle body and airbox assembly.
  4. Release hose clamp securing hose to intake tube.
  5. Remove vacuum hose from intake tube.
  6. Remove intake tube.
  7. Release engine harness and clip from air box assembly.
  8. Release coolant hoses (x3) and harness from lower airbox assembly.
  9. Remove M8x20 bolts (x2) securing airbox to vehicle. Torque 24 Nm.
  10. Release airbox from locating tabs.
  11. Remove airbox from vehicle.

    installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
