Oil Dipstick Tube - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.15.07-02

    Removal

  1. Remove dipstick inspection cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove dipstick.
  3. Remove rear bulkhead access panel. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove bolt securing top of dipstick tube to cylinder head. Torque 21 Nm.
  5. Remove bolt securing bottom of dipstick tube to engine block. Torque 21 Nm.
  6. Remove dipstick tube.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
