Undertray - Front

Print

Operation Code: 10.25.07-02

    Removal

  1. Raise and support vehicle on 2 post lift. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove scrivets (x4) securing front undertray to wheelarch liners.
  3. Remove M6x30 bolts and rubbing blocks (x2) securing undertray to undertray support bracket. Torque 9 Nm.
  4. Remove M6x16 bolts and washers (x19) securing undertray to front bumper panel, bumper mounting bracket, radiator support bracket and centre air duct. Torque 6 Nm.
  5. With assistance support weight of undertray.
  6. Remove M8x20 bolts (x12) securing undertray to front lower crossmember and lower dash panel crossmember. Torque 20 Nm.

    7. NOTE: When final bolts are removed support the diffuser so it does not drop unexpectedly.

  7. With assistance remove undertray from vehicle.

    8. NOTE: When lowering undertray make sure it is released from wheelarch liners.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
