Recirculation Pump - LHD

Print

Operation Code: 18.05.16-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front wheelarch liner left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Install clamps to inlet and outlet pipes to limit coolant loss.

    3. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  3. Disconnect harness connector from recirculation pump.
  4. Disengage hose clamps (x2) securing inlet and outlet pipes to recirculation pump.
  5. Remove inlet and outlet hoses.
  6. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing recirculation pump bracket to radiator mounting bracket assembly. Torque 10 Nm.
  7. Remove recirculation pump.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Check and top up coolant if necessary.
