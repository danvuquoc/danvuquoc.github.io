Timing Chain Cover Gasket - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.08.10-02

    Removal

  1. Remove timing chain cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Clean mating surfaces.

    Installation

  1. Renew oil pump gasket Refer to procedure.
  2. Apply seal packing as shown in illustration.

    3. NOTE: The timing chain cover must be installed within 3 minutes of applying the seal packing.

    NOTE: The timing chain cover bolts must be tightened within 15 minutes of applying the seal packing.

    NOTE: Do not start the engine within 2 hours of applying seal packing.

    NOTE: For oil related sections use Toyota Genuine Seal Packing Black, Three Bond 1207B or equivalent.

    NOTE: For water related sections use Toyota Genuine Seal Packing 1282B, Three Bond 1282B or equivalent.
