Centre Console Assembly

Print

Operation Code: 15.19.14-02

    Removal

  1. Remove drivers seat. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove passenger seat.

    3. NOTE: Passenger seat removal is the same as driver seat removal.

  3. Remove switch panel assembly. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove glove box assembly. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove engine start switch. Refer to procedure.
  6. Remove M4x16 self tapping screws (x3) securing switch pack to centre console and remove switch pack. Torque 2 Nm.
  7. Disengage harness connectors (x3) from switch pack.
  8. Remove auxiliary power socket. Refer to procedure.
  9. Remove M5x25 bolts (x2) securing centre console armrest to centre console. Torque 4 Nm.
  10. Remove centre console armrest.
  11. Release clips (x4) securing armrest latch trim to centre console and remove armrest latch.
  12. Remove rubber pads (x2) from cup holders.
  13. Remove M4x16 self tapping screws (x4) securing cup holders to centre console. Torque 2 Nm.
  14. Remove M4x16 self tapping screws (x2) securing centre console upper section to lower section. Torque 2 Nm.
  15. Remove M4x16 self tapping screws (x2) securing centre console upper section to instrument panel. Torque 2 Nm.
  16. Carefully disconnect harness connectors (x3) from instrument panel harness.
  17. Disengage fir tree clip securing tunnel harness to underbody assembly.
  18. Remove centre console upper assembly to access tunnel harness.
  19. Pull HVAC harness through aperture.
  20. Remove M5x16 bolt securing centre console lower section to crossmember. Torque 4 Nm.
  21. Remove M5x10 bolts and washers (x2) securing centre console lower section to underbody assembly.
  22. Remove rubber mats (x2) from centre console lower section.
  23. Remove M6x20 bolts (x3) securing centre console to floor crossmember. Torque 2 Nm.

    24. NOTE: For automatic transmission, remove bolts (x5).

  24. Remove M6x20 bolts (x4) securing centre console to reinforcement plate. Torque 2 Nm.
  25. Pull back carpet flaps (x6) to reveal centre console bolts (x6).
  26. Remove M6x20 bolts (x6) securing centre console to vehicle. Torque 2 Nm.

    27. CAUTION: Plastic tabs on centre console are brittle.

  27. Lift centre console assembly to access keyless antenna and USB port.
  28. Disconnect harness connector from USB port.
  29. Carefully disengage clips (x3) securing keyless antenna to centre console assembly.
  30. Disconnect harness connector from keyless antenna.
  31. Remove keyless antenna from centre console assembly.
  32. Lift centre console over armrest bracket and release clips (x6) securing centre console to vehicle.
  33. Remove centre console lower assembly.

    34. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  34. Disengage clips (x3) securing USB port to centre console assembly.
  35. Remove USB port.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
