Camshaft Position Sensors - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 17.06.16-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from camshaft position sensors.
  3. Remove M6 bolts (x2) securing camshaft position sensors to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  4. Remove camshaft position sensors.
