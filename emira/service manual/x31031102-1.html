Lower Wishbone - Rear - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 31.03.11-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear wheel. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove M12 nut securing anti-roll bar link to lower wishbone. Discard nut. Torque 36 Nm.

    4. NOTE: Insert 5mm hex bit into ball pin stud to prevent anti roll bar drop link ball joint from spinning during removal / installation.

  4. Remove anti-roll bar link from lower wishbone.
  5. Remove M6x12 bolt securing cable bracket to lower wishbone. Torque 10 Nm.
  6. Remove cable bracket from lower wishbone.
  7. Remove M14x105 bolt and securing damper to lower wishbone. Discard nut. Torque 190 Nm.
  8. Remove M14x125 bolt securing hub to lower wishbone. Discard nut. Torque 190 Nm.
  9. Remove M12x85 bolt securing front of lower wishbone to vehicle. Discard nut. Torque 105 Nm.
  10. Remove M12x95 bolt securing rear of lower wishbone to vehicle. Discard nut. Torque 105 Nm.

    11. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  11. Remove lower wishbone.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew all torque nut fixings that have been removed during removal procedure.
  3. Only torque tighten wishbone bolts once vehicle is in the normal ride height position.
  4. Perform a rear suspension geometry inspection and adjustment. Refer to procedure.
