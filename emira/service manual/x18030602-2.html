Pipe/Hose Assembly - Compressor Suction - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 18.03.06-02

    Removal

  1. Recover Air Conditioning (AC) refrigerant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear right side wheel arch liner.

    3. NOTE: Procedure is the same as left side component.

  3. Remove rear bulkhead access panel. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove bolt securing AC compressor suction pipe to AC compressor. Torque 20 Nm.
  6. Disconnect AC compressor suction pipe from AC compressor
  7. Remove M6x25 bolt securing AC compressor suction pipe to body pipe. Torque 10 Nm.
  8. Disconnect AC compressor suction pipe from body pipe.
  9. Remove AC compressor suction pipe.
  10. Remove and discard O-rings (x2).

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew O-rings (x2) with new refrigerant oil.
  3. Recharge AC refrigerant.
