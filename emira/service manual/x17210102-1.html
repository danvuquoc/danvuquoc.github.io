Wiper Motor & Linkage Assembly - LHD

Print

Operation Code: 17.21.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front wheelarch liner left side. Refer to procedure.

    2. NOTE: Remove left side wheelarch liner for LHD vehicles or right side wheelarch liner for RHD vehicles.

  2. Remove radiator outlet duct left side. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove radiator outlet duct right side.

    4. NOTE: Procedure is the same for left side component.

  4. Place wiper motor in parked position.
  5. Remove scrivets (x3) securing HVAC air inlet to vehicle.
  6. Remove M5x12 bolts (x2) and washers (x2) securing HVAC air inlet to vehicle.
  7. Remove HVAC air inlet.
  8. Manually move wiper arms to the vertical position.
  9. Remove wiper arm left side. Refer to procedure.
  10. Remove wiper arm right side.
  11. Remove front access panel grille frame. Refer to procedure.
  12. Remove M6x25 bolts and washers (x2) securing cowl to vehicle. Torque 5 Nm.
  13. Remove cowl from vehicle
  14. Disconnect harness connector from wiper motor.
  15. Remove M6x30 bolts (x2) and M6x40 bolt and washers securing wiper motor and linkage assembly to to wiper motor mid mounting bracket and upper dash panel bracket. Torque 10 Nm.
  16. Remove wiper motor and linkage assembly.

    17. NOTE: Wiper motor must be in the service position.

    Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  17. Remove rubber bushings (x3) from wiper linkage assembly.
  18. Remove bolts (x2) securing wiper motor to bracket.
  19. Remove bracket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. NOTE: When harness connector is connected the wiper motor will move to the parked position after 2-5 seconds.

  2. Switch on ignition to allow the wiper motor to automatically calibrate the home position.
