CV Joint and Shaft Assembly - Left Side - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 47.15.09-02

    Removal

  1. Remove drive shaft assembly - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Using a suitable tool, release clamps (9a and 9b) and slide gaiter (9) along driveshaft (7).
  3. Match mark CV joint (10), joint hub (10a) and driveshaft (7).
  4. Remove CV joint and joint hub from driveshaft using a suitable drift.
  5. Remove gaiter and clamps from driveshaft.

    6. NOTE: Discard clamps.

  6. Remove and discard snap ring (7b).

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Pack gaiter and joint hub with long-life grease (NLGI grade 2).
  3. Renew discarded clamps.

    4. NOTE: Dimension "X" must be ≤ 2 mm otherwise leaks may occur at gaiter.

  4. Renew discarded snap ring.
