Glass - Rear Quarterlight - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 11.01.13-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear fender assembly left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear quarter trim left side. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove M6x12 bolts (x2) and washers (x2) securing rear quarterlight window to vehicle. Torque 13 Nm.
  4. Remove M5 nyloc nut and washer securing rear quarterlight window to vehicle. Torque 10 Nm.

    5. NOTE: Using assistance support glass.

  5. Remove rear quarterlight glass.

    6. NOTE: Place glass on suitable surface to prevent damage.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
