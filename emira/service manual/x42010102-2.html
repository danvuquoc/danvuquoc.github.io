Airbox Assembly - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 42.01.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove tailgate trim - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove trim cover rear quarter - left side. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove Electronic Control Module (ECM). Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove 5x20 screws (x3) securing airbox lid to airbox assembly. Torque 1.2 Nm.
  5. Move ECM harness to one side.
  6. Remove airbox lid from airbox assembly.
  7. Release hose clamps (x2) securing air inlet hose to air filter element. Torque 3 Nm.
  8. Remove air inlet hose.
  9. Remove rear wheelarch liner - left side. Refer to procedure.
  10. Remove air intake duct from bodyside vent and airbox.
  11. Remove M8x25 bolt securing airbox to bracket. Torque 25 Nm.
  12. Remove M8x25 bolt securing airbox to bulkhead bracket. Torque 25 Nm.
  13. Remove M8x25 bolt securing airbox to transmission. Torque 25 Nm.
  14. Release clip securing engine harness to airbox.
  15. Lift airbox to release from lower clips.
  16. Release clips (x2) securing starter motor harness and coolant hose to airbox.

    17. NOTE: Airbox shown removed for clarity.

  17. Remove airbox.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
