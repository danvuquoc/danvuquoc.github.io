Rear Fog - Reverse Lamp

Print

Operation Code: 17.11.35-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear diffuser. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove 5x12 self tapping screws securing rear bumper grille frame to rear bumper.
  3. Release clips (x2) securing lower rear bumper grille frame to gain access to rear fog lamp.
  4. Disconnect harness connector from rear fog lamp.
  5. Remove M4x16 bolts (x2) securing rear fog lamp to rear bumper. Torque 2.25 Nm.
  6. Remove rear fog lamp.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
