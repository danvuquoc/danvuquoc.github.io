Reservoir - Master Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 33.03.20-02

    Removal

  1. Remove radiator duct outlet left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove brake fluid reservoir cap.
  3. Drain brake fluid from reservoir.
  4. Disconnect harness connector from brake fluid reservoir.
  5. Use hose clamp on clutch master cylinder hose to minimise fluid loss.
  6. Disengage hose clamp and remove hose from brake fluid reservoir.
  7. Remove bolt securing brake fluid reservoir to brake master cylinder. Torque 4 Nm.
  8. Remove brake fluid reservoir.

    9. CAUTION: If brake fluid is spilt on the paintwork wash off immediately with clean water.

    CAUTION: Plug pipe connections to prevent fluid loss and ingress of moisture or dirt.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Check and top up brake fluid level.
  3. Bleed brakes using Lotus Insight tool.
