Torque Rod - Upper Engine Mount - Right Side - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.01.09-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove right side rear wheelarch liner.
  3. Using a suitable tool, support engine.
  4. Remove M12x90 bolt securing torque rod to engine mount bracket.
  5. Remove M12x70 bolt securing torque rod to rear subframe.
  6. Remove torque rod.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
