Turbocharger with Actuator - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.21.12-02

    Removal

  1. Remove coolant feed line. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove oil feed line. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove boot box access panel. Refer to procedure.
  4. Loosen clamp securing air inlet pipe to turbocharger.
  5. Disconnect air inlet pipe from turbocharger.
  6. Release clamp securing air manifold to turbocharger. Torque 4 Nm.
  7. Disconnect air manifold from turbocharger.
  8. Remove bolts (x3) and nut securing bracket to engine and turbocharger.
  9. Remove bracket.
  10. Remove bolts (5 and 6) securing coolant return line (8) to turbocharger (1) and oil return line (10). Torque 8 Nm.
  11. Remove bolt (7) securing coolant return line to turbocharger. Torque 8 Nm.
  12. Disconnect coolant return line from turbocharger.

    13. NOTE: Discard seal.

  13. Remove bolt (9) securing oil return line to turbocharger. Torque 8 Nm.
  14. Disconnect oil return line from turbocharger.

    15. NOTE: Discard seal.

  15. Remove and discard clamp (2) securing turbocharger to catalytic converter. Torque 21 Nm.

    16. NOTE: Discard seal (11).

  16. Remove and discard clamp (3) securing turbocharger to exhaust manifold. Torque 21 Nm.

    17. NOTE: Discard seal.

  17. Remove turbocharger.

    18. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  18. Remove bolts (1) (x3) securing heatshield (2) to turbocharger (6). Torque 8 Nm.
  19. Remove heatshield.
  20. Remove retaining washer (4) securing linkage (5) to turbocharger.
  21. Release linkage from turbocharger.
  22. Remove bolts (7) (x3) securing actuator motor to turbocharger. Torque 9 Nm.
  23. Remove actuator motor.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded seals and clamps.
