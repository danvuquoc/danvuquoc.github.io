Oil Pan - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 47.02.09-02

Important Information

WARNING: Transmission oil is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested. Wear protective gloves, protective clothing and safety glasses.

    Removal

  1. Drain transmission oil. Refer to procedure.
  2. Install clamps to coolant hoses (7 and 8).
  3. Release spring clips (x2) securing coolant hoses to oil cooler (9).
  4. Disconnect coolant hoses (x2) from oil cooler.

    5. NOTE: Plug exposed connections to prevent ingress of dirt.

  5. Remove bolts (5) (x10) securing oil pan (1) to transmission. Torque 10 Nm.
  6. Remove oil pan.

    7. NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  7. Remove and discard oil pan gasket (10).
  8. Remove and discard molded seals (11) (x2).
  9. Remove and discard plain couplers (12) (x2) with sealing rings.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Clean component mating faces, prior to installation.

    3. WARNING: Only use cleaning agents and solvents in a well ventilated area.

  3. Renew all discarded seals and gasket.
  4. Install bolts in a diagonal sequence.
  5. Check and top up coolant level.
