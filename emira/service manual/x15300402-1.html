Carpet Mat Retaining Lug

Print

Operation Code: 15.30.04-02

    Removal

  1. Open door.
  2. Remove upper carpet mat retaining lugs (x2) securing carpet mat to carpet.
  3. Remove carpet mat.
  4. Remove carpet - left side. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove lower carpet mat retaining lugs (x2).

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
