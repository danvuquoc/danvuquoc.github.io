Fascia Panel - Lower

Print

Operation Code: 14.05.37-02

    Removal

  1. Remove finisher fascia end - left. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove 4x16 self tapping screw securing lower dash panel to fascia.
  3. Remove 4x16 self tapping screws (x2) securing lower fascia panel to fascia.
  4. Push diagnostic connector through to rear of lower fascia panel and move aside.
  5. Carefully disengage clips (x11) securing lower fascia panel to fascia.

    6. CAUTION: The clips are delicate, take care not to break plastic tabs.

  6. Refer to illustration for clip type and location.
  7. Partially remove lower fascia panel to access harness connectors.
  8. Release clips (x2) securing diagnostic connector to lower fascia panel.
  9. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from electronic handbrake switch and tailgate release switch.
  10. Disconnect harness connector from interior lamp.
  11. Remove lower fascia panel from vehicle.

    12. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  12. Release clips (x4) securing interior lamp to lower fascia panel.
  13. Remove interior lamp from lower fascia panel.
  14. Remove tailgate and illumination control switch. Refer to procedure.
  15. Remove parking brake switch. Refer to procedure.
  16. Remove self tapping screw securing electronic handbrake switch surround to lower fascia panel.
  17. Disengage clip securing electronic handbrake switch surround to lower fascia panel.
  18. Remove electronic handbrake switch surround from lower fascia panel.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
