Washer Pump

Print

Operation Code: 17.21.06-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear wheelarch liner left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect hose from washer pump.
  3. Release washer bottle pump from washer bottle.
  4. Disconnect harness connector from washer bottle pump.
  5. Remove washer pump.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
