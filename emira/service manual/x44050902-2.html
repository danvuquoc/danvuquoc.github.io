Pipe - Purge Valve to Charcoal Canister - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 44.05.09-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Release quick connector securing evaporative pipe to purge valve.

    3. NOTE: Pinch the two buttons on the quick connector and pull to remove.

  3. Disconnect evaporative pipe from purge valve.
  4. Release quick connector securing evaporative pipe to charcoal canister.
  5. Disconnect evaporative pipe from charcoal canister.
  6. Remove evaporative pipe.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal.

    2. NOTE: Push quick connector until and audible click is heard.
