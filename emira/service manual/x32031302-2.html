Pipe - Steering Rack to Cooler - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 32.03.13-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front undertray. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove banjo bolt securing steering rack/cooler pipe to steering rack. Torque 32 Nm.

    3. Oil will discharge from steering rack to cooler pipe when removing banjo bolt. Use a container to collect oil.

    Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  3. Release clips (x2) securing steering rack/cooler pipe to cooler/pump pipe.
  4. Release clip securing steering rack/cooler pipe to cooler/pump pipe.
  5. Remove M6x20 bolt securing steering rack/cooler pipe to front subframe. Torque 9 Nm.
  6. Open hood.
  7. Loosen union nut securing steering rack/cooler pipe to cooler pipe.
  8. Disconnect steering rack/cooler pipe from cooler pipe and discard O-ring.
  9. Remove steering rack/cooler pipe.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew banjo bolt sealing washers.
  3. Renew discarded O-ring.
  4. Fill power steering system. Refer to technical data.
  5. Bleed power steering system. Refer to procedure.
