Switch - Seat - Driver

Print

Operation Code: 17.05.44-02

    Removal

  1. Open door.
  2. Release clips (x5) securing seat outer trim to seat.
  3. Disconnect harness connector from seat switch.
  4. Slide off cable ties (x4) from seat outer trim.

    5. NOTE: Do not cut cable ties.

  5. Remove seat outer trim.
  6. Remove M5x13 screws (x5) securing seat switch to seat outer trim.
  7. Remove seat switch.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
