Cam Cover - Left Side - Bank 2 - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.07.25-02

    Removal

  1. Remove ignition coils left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove oil control valves. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove camshaft position sensors. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove union bolts (x2) securing oil control valve pipe to engine. Torque 65 Nm.
  5. Remove oil control valve pipe and discard gaskets (x2).
  6. Remove bolts (x12) securing cylinder head cover to cylinder head. Bolt A.
    Torque 21 Nm. Rest of bolts.
    Torque 10 Nm.
  7. Remove cylinder head cover.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded gaskets (x2).
