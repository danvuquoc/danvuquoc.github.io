Oil Seal - Differential To Driveshaft - Left Side - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 47.15.05-02

Important Information

WARNING: Transmission oil is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested. Wear protective gloves, protective clothing and safety glasses.

    Removal

  1. Remove driveshaft assembly - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Clean area around oil seal.

    3. WARNING: Only use cleaning agents and solvents in a well ventilated area.

  3. Using a suitable tool, remove oil seal.

    4. CAUTION: Do not damage mating surface of oil seal on transmission otherwise leaks may occur.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Inspect mating surface of oil seal on transmission for damage.
  3. Apply transmission oil to sealing lip of oil seal.
  4. Install oil seal to transmission using drift.

    5. NOTE: Insert oil seal far enough into transmission until drift makes contact with transmission housing.
