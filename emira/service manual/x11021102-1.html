Door Water Deflector Panel - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 11.02.11-02

    Removal

  1. Remove motor - window regulator - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector from door speaker.
  3. Disconnect harness connector from door pressure sensor.
  4. Disconnect harness connector from door mirror harness.
  5. Disengage clips (x2) securing harness to water deflector panel.
  6. Disengage grommets (x3) from water deflector panel.

    7. NOTE: Push grommets through the panel into the door.

  7. Remove M6x16 bolts (x15) securing water deflector panel to door panel. Torque 5 Nm.
  8. Pull through harness and door release cable as you remove the water deflector panel from the door.
  9. Remove water deflector panel from vehicle.

    10. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only

    .
  10. Remove 4x16 screws (x3) securing door speaker to water deflector panel.
  11. Remove speaker from water deflector panel.
  12. Remove 5x20 screws (x2) securing door pressure sensor to door water deflector panel. Torque 6 Nm.
  13. Remove door pressure sensor from door water deflector panel.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Reset window one touch facility:
  3. Fully raise each window, once raised, hold the switch for 2 seconds until a click is heard.

    4. NOTE: Perform with door open.

  4. Then fully lower each window, once lowered, hold the switch for 2 seconds until a click is heard.
  5. Close door, raise and lower window to ensure door glass drop is working correctly.
