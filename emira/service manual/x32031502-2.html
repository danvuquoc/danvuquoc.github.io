Pipe - Cooler to Pump - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 32.03.15-02

    Removal

  1. Remove radiator duct outlet left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove radiator duct outlet right side.

    3. NOTE: Procedure is the same as left side component.

  3. Remove scrivets (x3) securing HVAC air inlet to vehicle.
  4. Remove M5x12 screws and washers (x2) securing HVAC air inlet to filter lid.
  5. Remove HVAC air inlet.
  6. Remove M8x25 bolt securing cooler/pump pipe to pump. Torque 18 Nm.
  7. Disconnect cooler/pump pipe from pump and discard O-ring.
  8. Loosen union nut securing cooler/pump pipe to cooler pipe.
  9. Disconnect steering rack/cooler pipe from cooler pipe and discard O-ring.
  10. Remove front undertray. Refer to procedure.
  11. Remove M6x20 bolt securing cooler/pump pipe to front subframe. Torque 9 Nm.
  12. Release clips (x2) securing cooler/pump pipe to steering rack/cooler pipe.
  13. Remove cooler/pump pipe.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded O-rings.
  3. Fill power steering system. Refer to technical data.
  4. Bleed power steering system. Refer to procedure.
