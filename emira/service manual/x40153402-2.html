Hose - Coolant - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.15.34-02

Important Information

WARNING: Risk of scalding. Allow coolant to cool completely before proceeding.

WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    Removal

  1. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove catalytic converter. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove M6 bolts (x11) securing heat shields (x2) to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  4. Remove heat shields.
  5. Release spring clamp securing coolant hose to coolant pipe.
  6. Disconnect coolant hose from coolant pipe.

    7. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  7. Remove bolt (1) securing coolant hose (3) to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  8. Remove bolt (2) securing coolant hose to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  9. Disconnect coolant hose from engine.

    10. NOTE: Remove and discard O-ring.

  10. Remove coolant hose.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded O-ring.
