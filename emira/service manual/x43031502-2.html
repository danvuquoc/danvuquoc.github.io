Purge Line - Intermediate To Bleed Line - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 43.03.15-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Release connector and disconnect purge line from bleed line.

    3. NOTE: Plug purge line to prevent ingress of dirt.

  3. Release clips (x2) securing purge line to coolant return pipe and bleed line.
  4. Release purge line from engine harness.
  5. Release connector and disconnect purge valve line from purge line.

    6. NOTE: Plug purge line to prevent ingress of dirt.

  6. Release connector and disconnect purge line from charge air distributor.
  7. Remove purge line.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
