Pipe - Cooler Assembly

Print

Operation Code: 32.03.14-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front bumper. Refer to procedure.
  2. Loosen union nuts (x2) securing cooler pipe to power steering fluid feed and return hoses.
  3. Disconnect cooler pipe from power steering fluid feed and return hoses and discard O-rings.

    4. WARNING: Power steering fluid is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  4. Remove bolt securing cooler pipe to condenser.
  5. Release clips (x2) securing cooler pipe to condenser.
  6. Remove cooler pipe and drain oil.

    7. NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded O-rings.
  3. Fill power steering system. Refer to technical data.
  4. Bleed power steering system. Refer to procedure.
