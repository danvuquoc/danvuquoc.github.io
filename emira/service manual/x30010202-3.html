Rear Subframe Assembly - V6

Print

Operation Code: 30.01.02-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove fuel filler bowl assembly. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove rear hub carrier left side. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove rear upper wishbone assembly left side. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove rear hub carrier right side.

    6. NOTE: Procedure is the same for left side component.

  6. Remove rear upper wishbone assembly right side.

    7. NOTE: Procedure is the same for left side component.

  7. Remove rear lower wishbone left side. Refer to procedure.
  8. Remove rear lower wishbone right side.

    9. NOTE: Procedure is the same for left side component.

  9. Remove rear anti roll bar. Refer to procedure.
  10. Disengage clips (x6) securing harness to rear subframe assembly.
  11. Remove harness and set aside to clear rear subframe assembly.
  12. Remove M6x20 bolts (x5) securing power steering fluid lines to rear subframe. Torque 9 Nm.
  13. Remove power steering fluid lines and power steering fluid reservoir.
  14. Remove M6x25 bolts (x2) securing alternator battery cable to rear subframe assembly. Torque 10 Nm.
  15. Disengage clips (x6) securing alternator battery cable to rear subframe assembly.
  16. Remove alternator battery harness.
  17. Remove M6x16 bolts (x4) securing oil cooler hoses to rear subframe assembly.
  18. Remove M8x20 bolts (x3) securing oil cooler to rear subframe assembly.
  19. Remove oil cooler assembly and hoses.
  20. Disengage clip securing harness to rear subframe assembly.
  21. Remove harness and set aside.
  22. Install suitable lifting table under rear subframe assembly.
  23. Lower vehicle until rear subframe assembly is supported on lifting table.
  24. Disengage rear left side brake line from bracket.
  25. Remove M10x30 bolts (x5) securing left side rear subframe assembly to bulkhead. Torque 47 Nm.
  26. Remove M10x30 bolts (x2) and nuts securing left side rear subframe assembly to underbody assembly. Torque 47 Nm.
  27. Disengage rear right side brake line from bracket.
  28. Remove M10x30 bolts (x5) securing right side rear subframe assembly to bulkhead. Torque 47 Nm.
  29. Remove M10x30 bolts (x2) and nuts securing right side rear subframe assembly to underbody assembly. Torque 47 Nm.
  30. Remove M10x80 bolts (x3) and washers (x3) securing rear subframe assembly to underbody assembly. Torque 47 Nm.

    31. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  31. Remove rear subframe assembly.
  32. Remove any shim plates from bottom of rear subframe side rails.

    33. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of shim plates.

  33. Remove rear subframe adapter shim plates (x2).

    34. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  34. Remove M6x30 bolts (x4) securing heatshield to rear subframe assembly. Torque 10 Nm.
  35. Remove heatshield.
  36. Remove M6x30 bolts (x5) securing heatshield to rear subframe assembly. Torque 10 Nm.
  37. Remove M6x25 bolts (x2) securing heatshield to rear subframe assembly. Torque 10 Nm.
  38. Remove heatshield.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Trial fit rear subframe assembly using no shim plates.
  3. Temporarily fit M10x30 bolts and nuts (x4) during trial installation.
  4. Measure gap between bottom of chassis side rails and subframe horizontal surface.
  5. Apply duralac anti-corrosion compound to subframe and shim mating surfaces.
  6. Install shim plates as required.
  7. Perform a rear suspension geometry inspection and adjustment. Refer to procedure.
