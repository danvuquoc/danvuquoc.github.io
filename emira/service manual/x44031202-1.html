Fuel Pressure Sensor

Print

Operation Code: 44.03.12-02

    Removal

  1. Depressurise fuel system. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector from fuel pressure sensor.
  3. Disengage hose connectors (x2) securing fuel pressure sensor to fuel pipe.

    4. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  4. Remove fuel pressure sensor.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
