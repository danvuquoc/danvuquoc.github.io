Switch - Infotainment

Print

Operation Code: 17.05.36-02

    Removal

  1. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rubber pad from centre console.
  3. Release clips (x11) securing centre console finisher to centre console.

    4. NOTE: Release clips working from front to rear.

  4. Remove centre console finisher.
  5. Remove 4x13 self tapping screws (x2) securing infotainment switch to centre console.
  6. Remove infotainment switch to access harness connector.
  7. Disengage harness connector from infotainment switch.
  8. Remove infotainment switch.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
