Brake Disc - Front - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 33.01.17-02

    Removal

  1. Remove road wheel - front - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove and discard M12x75 bolts (x2) securing brake caliper to hub carrier. Torque 86 Nm.
  3. Release brake caliper and move aside.

    4. NOTE: Secure brake caliper on vehicle. Do not disconnect hydraulic brake hose.

  4. Remove M8x12 bolt securing brake disc to hub. Torque 10 Nm.
  5. Remove brake disc from hub assembly.

    Installation

  1. Installation procedure is the reverse of removal except for the following:
  2. Renew brake caliper bolts.
  3. Clean mating face of hub and disc.
  4. Clean new component to remove preservation coating before installing.
  5. Operate brake pedal at least 5 times to seat pads against brake disc.

    6. WARNING: Always check brake pads are seated correctly before driving the vehicle.
