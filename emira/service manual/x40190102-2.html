Starter Motor - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.19.01-02

    Removal

  1. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove cover from battery cable.
  4. Remove M8 nut securing battery cable to starter motor. Torque 16 Nm.
  5. Disconnect battery cable from starter motor.
  6. Disconnect harness connector from starter motor.
  7. Remove bolts (x2) securing starter motor to transmission. Torque 39 Nm.
  8. Remove starter motor.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Inspect pinion of starter motor for damage.
