Armrest Pad

Print

Operation Code: 15.19.10-02

    Removal

  1. Remove auxiliary power socket. Refer to procedure.
  2. Open armrest pad.
  3. Remove M5x25 bolts (x2) securing armrest pad to centre console. Torque 4 Nm.
  4. Remove armrest pad.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
