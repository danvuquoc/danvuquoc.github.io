Crossmember - Rear Subframe - Lower - Left Side - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 30.01.06-02

    Removal

  1. Remove lower wishbone - rear left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove lower wishbone - rear right side.

    3. NOTE: Procedure is the same as left side component.

  3. Remove toe arm assemblies - left and right side. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove rear diffuser. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove engine mount - rear right side lower. Refer to procedure.
  6. Install suitable lifting table under vehicle.
  7. Lower vehicle until vehicle is supported on lifting table.
  8. Release clips (x3) securing ground harness to lower subframe.
  9. Remove M5x30 bolts (x3) securing lower rear subframe to rear subframe. Torque 45 Nm.
  10. Remove M5x30 bolts (x5) securing lower rear subframe to underbody assembly. Torque 45 Nm.
  11. Remove M5x30 bolts (x3) securing lower rear subframe to rear subframe. Torque 45 Nm.
  12. Remove M5x30 bolts (x5) securing lower rear subframe to underbody assembly. Torque 45 Nm.
  13. Remove bolts (x4) securing lower rear subframe to rear subframe.
  14. Remove bolts (x6) securing lower rear subframe to rear subframe.
  15. Remove lower rear subframe.
  16. Drill out rivets (x8) securing lower crossmember to lower rear subframe.
  17. Drill out rivet securing lower crossmember to lower rear subframe.
  18. Remove M8x20 bolts (x2) securing lower crossmember to lower rear subframe.
  19. Remove lower crossmember.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Perform a rear suspension geometry inspection and adjustment. Refer to procedure.
