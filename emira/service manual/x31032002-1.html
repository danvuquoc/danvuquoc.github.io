Toe Arm Assembly - Rear

Print

Operation Code: 31.03.20-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear wheel. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove M14x90 bolt and M14 nut securing toe arm to hub. Discard nut. Torque 135 Nm.
  4. Remove M14x75 bolt and M14 nut securing toe arm to vehicle. Discard nut. Torque 135 Nm.
  5. Remove toe arm.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded nuts.
  3. Ensure left handed threaded link of rear toe arm is is fitted inboard of rear subframe.
  4. Refit inner and outer link joint bolts in correct orientation.

    5. NOTE: The bolt head should face the rear of the vehicle.

  5. Only torque tighten toe arm bolts once vehicle is in the normal ride height position.
  6. If renewing toe arm assembly, perform a rear suspension geometry inspection and adjustment. Refer to procedure.
