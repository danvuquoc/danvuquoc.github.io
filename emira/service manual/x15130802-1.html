Trim - Switch Panel - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 15.13.08-02

    Removal

  1. Remove interior door pull trim - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M4x13 screws (x4) securing switch panel trim to interior door pull trim.
  3. Remove switch panel trim.

    4. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  4. Release clips (x6) securing switch pack to switch panel trim.
  5. Remove switch pack.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
