Oil Sump - Lower - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.03.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove downpipe. Refer to procedure.

    2. WARNING: Allow the exhaust to cool down before proceeding to prevent injury.

  2. Remove sump plug and drain engine oil.

    3. NOTE: Discard sump washer.

  3. Remove nuts (x2) securing sump to engine. Torque 10 Nm.
  4. Remove bolts (x16) securing sump to engine. Torque 10 Nm.

    5. NOTE: Torque sump fasteners evenly to prevent warping of the sump during installation.

  5. Use sump removal tool to release sealant.
  6. Remove sump.
  7. Apply silicon remover to sump flange and let sit for 10 minutes.
  8. Apply silicon remover to engine mating surface and let sit for 10 minutes.
  9. Remove silicon from sump flange and engine mating surface.
  10. Clean sump flange.

    11. NOTE: Use Betaclean 3900 to clean sump flange.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Apply 5910 sealant around sump flange.

    3. NOTE: Apply 3-4 mm wide bead of sealant to sump flange.

  3. Replace sump plug washer.
