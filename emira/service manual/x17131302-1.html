Headlamp Module - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 17.13.13-02

    Removal

  1. Remove headlamp assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove screws (x3) securing control module to headlamp.
  3. Remove control module from headlamp to gain access to electrical connectors.
  4. Disconnect electrical connectors (x4) connecting headlamp harness to control module.
  5. Remove control module from headlamp.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
