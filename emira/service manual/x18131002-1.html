Face Level Vent - In Dash

Print

Operation Code: 18.13.10-02

    Removal

  1. Remove fascia finisher panel left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove fascia finisher panel right side.

    3. NOTE: Procedure is the same for left side component.

  3. Disengage clips (x4) securing right side face level vent to instrument panel.
  4. Remove left side face level vent.
  5. Disengage clips (x4) securing right side face level vent to instrument panel.
  6. Remove right side face level vent.
  7. Disengage clips (x8) securing center vent to instrument panel.
  8. Remove centre vent.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
