Actuator - Recirculation Flaps - LHD

Print

Operation Code: 18.01.09-02

    Removal

  1. Remove radiator outlet duct left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove radiator duct outlet right side.

    3. NOTE: Procedure is the same as for left side component.

  3. Remove scrivets (x3) securing HVAC air inlet to vehicle.
  4. Remove HVAC air inlet.
  5. If fitted, remove water shield from HVAC unit. Refer to TSB-131-24007.
  6. Disconnect harness connector from left side actuator.
  7. Remove self tapping screws (x3) securing left side actuator to HVAC unit.
  8. Remove left side actuator.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. After installation perform a diagnostic read and clear error memory using Lotus Insight tool.
