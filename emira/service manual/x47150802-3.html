CV Joint Assembly - Inboard Gaiter - Right Side - V6

Print

Operation Code: 47.15.08-02

    Removal

  1. Remove driveshaft assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove clips (x2) securing inboard constant velocity (CV) joint gaiter to driveshaft and pull away gaiter.
  3. Match mark driveshaft and CV joint outer body.
  4. Using a press, remove dust shield from inboard end of driveshaft.
  5. Remove circlip from outboard face of bearing housing.
  6. Remove CV joint outer body.
  7. Match mark driveshaft and CV joint spider.
  8. Remove snap ring from end of driveshaft and remove CV joint spider.
  9. Remove CV joint gaiter.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. NOTE: Use special press tool T000T1439F to press bearing dust shield onto driveshaft.

  2. Renew CV joint grease for inboard and outboard CV joints.

    3. CAUTION: The supplied grease must be used in order to prevent damage or premature wear to the CV joints.

    NOTE: Inboard joint requires 200g of NKG302 grease.

    NOTE: Outboard joint requires 180g of NTG2218-M grease.
