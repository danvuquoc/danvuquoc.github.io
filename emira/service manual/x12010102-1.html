Wheelarch Liner - Front - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 12.01.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front road wheel left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M6x16 bolts (x2) and washers (x2) securing wheelarch liner to front undertray. Torque 9 Nm.
  3. Remove wheel arch liner front section scrivets (x10).
  4. Remove M6x35 pan head bolt and washer securing wheelarch liner to chassis. Torque 6 Nm.
  5. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) and washers (x2) securing wheelarch liner to chassis.
  6. Remove wheel arch liner rear section scrivets (x8).
  7. Remove wheelarch liner from the vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
