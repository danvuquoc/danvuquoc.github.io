Radiator

Print

Operation Code: 46.01.03-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front access hatch. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove front bumper. Refer to procedure.
  3. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove trinary switch Refer to procedure.
  5. Drain power steering fluid.
  6. Remove M6x16 bolts (x2) securing horn bracket right side to front bumper air duct.
  7. Remove M6x16 bolts (x2) securing horn bracket left side to front bumper air duct.
  8. Remove M6x16 bolts (x3) and washers securing front bumper air duct to front bumper beam.

    9. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  9. Remove front bumper air duct.
  10. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing condenser inlet pipes (x2).
  11. Remove condenser inlet pipes (x2) from air conditioning condenser.
  12. Disengage hose clamp securing radiator hose to radiator.
  13. Disconnect radiator hose from radiator.

    14. WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain coolant into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  14. Remove M6x12 bolts (x2) securing coolant bypass pipe to radiator.
  15. Remove nuts (x2) securing power steering fluid return pipe to power steering pipes (x2).
  16. Disconnect power steering return pipe from power steering pipes.
  17. Disconnect harness connector from radiator fan assembly.
  18. Disengage fir tree clips (x3) securing front end harness to radiator assembly and set harness aside.
  19. Disengage fir tree clips (x2) securing radiator outlet hose to radiator brackets (x2).
  20. Disengage hose clamp securing radiator inlet hose to radiator.
  21. Remove radiator hoses (x2) from radiator.
  22. Remove M6x16 bolts (x6) securing radiator support brackets (x2) to vehicle. Torque 10 Nm.
  23. Remove radiator support brackets (x2).
  24. Remove radiator assembly.

    25. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  25. Remove bolts (x8) securing radiator fan shroud to radiator.
  26. Remove radiator fan shroud.
  27. Remove bolts (x6) securing condenser and power steering fluid return pipe to radiator.
  28. Remove condenser and power steering fluid return pipe assembly.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
