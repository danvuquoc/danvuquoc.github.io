Fuel Filler Flap

Print

Operation Code: 44.01.09-02

    Remove

  1. Remove fuel filler bowl assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disengage and remove fuel flap release solenoid from fuel filler bowl assembly.
  3. Remove spring securing fuel filler flap to filler bowl assembly.
  4. Remove arm from fuel filler bowl assembly.
  5. Remove fuel flap from fuel filler bowl assembly.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
