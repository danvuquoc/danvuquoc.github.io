CV Joint Assembly - Inboard Gaiter - Left Side - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 47.15.07-02

    Removal

  1. Remove drive shaft assembly - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Using a suitable tool, release clamps (2 and 4) and slide gaiter (3) along driveshaft (1).

    3. CAUTION: Take care not to damage the gaiter if it is to be reused.

  3. Release gaiter seal (5) from joint hub (10).
  4. Match mark joint hub, ball cage (8), constant velocity(CV) joint hub (9) and driveshaft.
  5. Remove joint hub.
  6. Remove balls from ball cage.
  7. Remove CV joint hub from driveshaft using a suitable drift.

    8. NOTE: Remove and discard sealing ring (6).

  8. Remove ball cage from CV joint hub.
  9. Remove gaiter seal, gaiter and clamps from driveshaft.

    10. NOTE: Discard clamps.

  10. Remove and discard snap ring (7).

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Insert balls into ball cage and grease with long-life grease (NLGI grade 2).
  3. Renew sealing ring of joint hub.

    4. NOTE: Do not apply grease or oil to sealing ring otherwise gaiter seal may turn on joint hub.

  4. Pack gaiter and joint hub with remaining long-life grease (NLGI grade 2).
  5. Renew discarded clamps.

    6. NOTE: Dimension "X" must be ≤ 2 mm otherwise leaks may occur at gaiter.

  6. Renew discarded snap ring.
