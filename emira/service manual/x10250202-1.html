Undertray - Rear

Print

Operation Code: 10.25.02-02

    Removal

  1. Raise and support vehicle on 2 post lift. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M5x16 bolts and washers (x4) securing floor protection covers to diffuser. Torque 4 Nm.
  3. Remove M5x16 bolts (x7) and washers securing undertray to diffuser. Torque 4 Nm.
  4. Remove M5x16 bolts (x5) securing undertray to rear subframe crossmember. Torque 4 Nm.
  5. Remove M8x20 bolts (x14) securing undertray to left and right side subframe crossmembers and rear tank bay crossmember. Torque 24 Nm.
  6. Release rear of undertray from diffuser and remove undertray.

    7. NOTE: When the last bolts are removed, support the undertray so it does not drop unexpectedly.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. CAUTION: When installing, ensure gearshift cable is orientated left of engine bay away from downpipe. Replace gear cable insulation if damaged to prevent risk of heat exposure to cable assembly.
