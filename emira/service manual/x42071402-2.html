Charge Air Distributor - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 42.07.14-02

    Removal

  1. Remove purge line - purge valve to purge line intermediate. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove purge line - intermediate to bleed line. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove bleed line - air intake pipe. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove electronic throttle actuator. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove screws (x3) securing engine harness to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  6. Disconnect harness connector from pressure sensor.
  7. Release clip securing engine harness to charge air distributor.
  8. Disconnect harness connectors (x4) from injectors.
  9. Release clips (x3) securing engine harness to charge air distributor.
  10. Remove M6 bolts (x2) securing bleed line to engine. Torque 9 Nm.
  11. Remove bleed line from engine and charge air distributor.

    12. NOTE: Discard seals.

  12. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from purge valve.
  13. Remove M6 bolt securing purge valve to charge air distributor. Torque 9 Nm.
  14. Remove M6 bolt securing charge air distributor to engine. Torque 11 Nm.
  15. Disconnect purge lines (x2) from charge air distributor.

    16. NOTE: Plug purge lines to prevent ingress of dirt.

  16. Remove M6 bolts (x7) securing charge air distributor to engine.
  17. Carefully remove charge air distributor.

    18. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  18. Remove screw securing pressure sensor to charge air distributor.
  19. Remove pressure sensor from charge air distributor.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Tighten M6 bolts (x7) in sequence shown. Torque 11 Nm.
  3. Renew discarded seals.
