Bracket and Idler Assembly - V6

Print

Operation Code: 41.03.09-02

    Removal

  1. Remove auxiliary drive belt. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove airbox assembly. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove M12x85 bolt securing left side transmission mount to transmission. Torque 86 Nm.
  4. Remove M8x25 bolts (x3) securing idler support bracket to idler assembly.
  5. Remove idler support bracket.
  6. Remove M8x35 bolts (x2) securing supercharger nose bracket to idler assembly. Torque 24 Nm.
  7. Remove M6x20 bolts (x3) securing power steering pump reservoir bracket to rear subframe stay. Torque 9 Nm.
  8. Remove power steering pump reservior bracket and pump assembly aside.
  9. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing engine bay fan bracket to rear subframe stay. Torque 10 Nm.
  10. Remove M6x16 bolts (x2) securing tailgate support bracket to rear subframe stay. Torque 10 Nm.
  11. Remove M10x30 and M10x25 bolts securing rear subframe stay to vehicle. Torque 45 Nm.
  12. Remove rear subframe stay.
  13. Remove M12x90 bolt securing idler assembly to subframe. Torque 86 Nm.
  14. Using a suitable jack, raise engine.
  15. Remove bolts (x6) securing idler assembly to engine.

    16. NOTE: Record position of bolts (x6) for installation purposes.

  16. Remove idler assembly.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Install idler assembly.
  3. Apply Loctite 5900 - 5910 silicone sealant or equivalent to bolts (x6).
  4. Install bolts (x6) securing idler assembly to engine. Hand tight only.
  5. Install idler support bracket.
  6. Install M8x25 bolts (x3) securing idler support bracket to idler assembly. Hand tight only.
  7. Install M8x35 bolts (x2) securing supercharger nose bracket to idler assembly. Torque 24 Nm.
  8. Tighten bolts (x6) securing idler assembly to engine in sequence shown. Torque 52 Nm.
  9. Tighten bolts (x3) securing idler support bracket to idler assembly. Torque 24 Nm.
