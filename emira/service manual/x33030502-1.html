Hose - Rear - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 33.03.05-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear wheelarch liner left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove brake hose banjo bolt from brake caliper. Torque 24 Nm.
  3. Remove brake hose sealing washers (x2).

    4. CAUTION: Brake fluid will discharge from brake hose when removing banjo bolt. Use a container to collect brake fluid.

    CAUTION: Do not allow brake fluid to contact lower arm.

    NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  4. Remove M10 brake union nut securing brake hose to brake line. Torque 16 Nm.
  5. Remove spring clip securing brake hose to bracket.
  6. Remove brake hose.

    7. CAUTION: Plug pipe connections to prevent fluid loss and ingress of moisture or dirt.

    CAUTION: If brake fluid is spilt on the paintwork wash off immediately with clean water.

  7. Plug brake hose connections.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. NOTE: Make sure the brake hose is free of kinks during installation.

  2. Renew brake hose sealing washers.
  3. Bleed brakes using Lotus Insight tool.
