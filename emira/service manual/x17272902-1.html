Tweeter Speaker - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 17.27.29-02

    Removal

  1. Carefully pull A pillar trim to disengage clips (x2) securing A pillar trim to vehicle.

    2. NOTE: Windscreen glass removed for image clarity.

  2. Remove A pillar trim to access harness connector.
  3. Disconnect harness connector from tweeter speaker.
  4. Remove tweeter speaker from A pillar trim.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
