Connecting Shaft - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 47.15.12-02

Important Information

WARNING: Transmission oil is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested. Wear protective gloves, protective clothing and safety glasses.

    Removal

  1. Remove driveshaft assembly - right side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M8x30 bolts (x2) securing connecting shaft to driveshaft bearing bracket. Torque 20 Nm.
  3. Release connecting shaft from transmission.

    4. CAUTION: Take care not to damage the transmission oil seal to prevent leaks.

    NOTE: Use a suitable container to collect any transmission oil.

  4. Remove connecting shaft.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Apply grease to inboard driveshaft splines before installation.

    3. CAUTION: Take care when installing driveshaft into gearbox to not damage the snap ring.

    NOTE: Applying grease will keep the snap ring in place, aiding in installation.

  3. Check transmission oil level. Refer to procedure.
