Alternator - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.19.02-02

    Removal

  1. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  2. Loosen auxiliary drive belt and remove from alternator pulley. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove rear bulkhead access panel. Refer to procedure.
  4. Disengage fir tree clip securing harness to alternator.
  5. Disconnect harness connector from alternator
  6. Remove cover from alternator battery cable.
  7. Remove M8 nut securing alternator battery cable to alternator. Torque 24 Nm.
  8. Remove battery cable from alternator and move aside.
  9. Remove harness clamp bolt from cylinder block. Torque 20 Nm.
  10. Remove bolts (x2) from alternator. Torque 43 Nm.
  11. Remove alternator.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
