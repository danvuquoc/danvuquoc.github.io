Actuator Motor - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.21.19-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove bolts (1) (x3) securing heatshield (2) to turbocharger (6). Torque 8 Nm.
  3. Remove heatshield.
  4. Disconnect harness connector (3) from actuator motor.
  5. Remove retaining washer (4) securing linkage (5) to turbocharger.
  6. Release linkage from turbocharger.
  7. Remove bolts (7) (x3) securing actuator motor to turbocharger. Torque 9 Nm.
  8. Remove actuator motor.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. After installation perform a diagnostic read and clear error memory using Lotus Insight tool.
