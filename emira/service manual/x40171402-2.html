Spark Plug - Single - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.17.14-02

    Removal

  1. Remove ignition coil. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove bolts (x2) securing ignition coil to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  3. Disconnect ignition coil from spark plug and move aside.
  4. Remove spark plug (1) using spark plug wrench (01). Torque 15 Nm.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Apply spark plug connector grease to spark plug connector (3) of ignition coil (2).
