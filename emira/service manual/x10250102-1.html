Diffuser - Rear

Print

Operation Code: 10.25.01-02

    Removal

  1. Raise and support vehicle on 2 post lift. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M5x16 bolts and washers (x4) securing floor protection covers to diffuser. Torque 7 Nm.
  3. Remove M5x16 bolts and washers (x6) securing diffuser to rear undertray. Torque 10 Nm.
  4. Remove M6x20 bolts (x6) securing diffuser to rear bumper lower. Torque 10 Nm
  5. Remove M6x20 bolts (x6) securing diffuser to rear diffuser bracket. Torque 10 Nm

    6. NOTE: When the final bolts are removed support the diffuser so it does not drop unexpectedly.

  6. Remove diffuser from vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
