Back Stay Frame

Print

Operation Code: 30.02.02-02

    Removal

  1. Remove C pillar left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disengage clips (x2) securing harness to back stay frame.
  3. Remove M6x16 bolts (x2) securing tailgate support bracket to back stay frame. Torque 10 Nm.
  4. Remove tailgate support bracket.
  5. Remove M6x12 bolt securing electronic control module bracket to back stay frame. Torque 10 Nm.
  6. Remove M8x20 bolt securing airbox assembly to back stay frame. Torque 24 Nm.
  7. Remove M8 nuts (x2) securing back stay frame to rear subframe. Torque 18 Nm.
  8. Remove M10x60 bolt securing back stay frame to rear bulkhead.
  9. Remove back stay frame.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
