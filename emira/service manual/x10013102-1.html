C Pillar - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 10.01.31-02

    Removal

  1. Remove tailgate strut. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove trim cover rear quarter left side. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove rear fender left side. Refer to procedure.
  4. Disengage harness clips (x2) securing tailgate manual release cable to C pillar.
  5. Remove M6x16 bolts (x2) securing C pillar to boot box upper. Torque 10 Nm.
  6. Remove M6x16 bolts (x3) and washers (x2) securing C pillar to rear frame and expansion tank mounting. Torque 10 Nm.

    7. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  7. Remove C pillar.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
