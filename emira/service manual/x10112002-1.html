Tailgate Latch Cover

Print

Operation Code: 10.11.20-02

    Removal

  1. Carefully pull to disengage trim clips (x2) on lower edge of latch cover trim.
  2. Refer to illustration for trim clip locations.
  3. Disengage upper edge of latch cover trim from trunk inner trim and remove from vehicle.

    Refitment

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
