Oil Filter - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.15.03-02

    Removal

  1. Drain engine oil. Refer to procedure.
  2. Connect hose to pipe.
  3. Remove oil filter drain plug and O ring. Torque 12.5 Nm.
  4. Insert pipe into oil filter cap.

    5. NOTE: Be sure to turn the pipe in the direction of the arrow to remove it.

  5. Drain oil.

    6. WARNING: Engine oil is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  6. Use SST T000T1441F to remove oil filter cap and oil filter element. Torque 25 Nm.
  7. Remove oil filter element and O rings (x2) from oil filter cap.

    8. CAUTION: Be sure to remove the O-ring (for the cap) by hand, without using any tools, to prevent damage to the groove for the O-ring on the cap.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
