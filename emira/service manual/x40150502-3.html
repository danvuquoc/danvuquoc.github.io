Oil Pick-up and Strainer - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.15.05-02

    Removal

  1. Remove lower oil sump. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove bolts (x2) securing oil strainer to oil pan. Torque 10 Nm.
  3. Remove nut securing oil strainer to oil pan. Torque 10 Nm.
  4. Remove oil strainer.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
