Fender Rear - Right Side

Print

Operation Code: 10.01.28-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear bumper. Refer to procedure.
  2. Open door.
  3. Remove 4x8 self tapping screws (x2) securing side vent grill to rear fender.
  4. Disengage clips (x4) and remove side vent grille from rear fender.
  5. Remove emblem secured to rear fender. Refer to procedure.
  6. Disengage trim clips (x6) and remove fender panel secured to rear fender.
  7. Disengage trim clips (x8) and remove air outlet trim secured to rear fender.
  8. Disengage trim clips (x6) and remove luggage side cover secured to vehicle.
  9. Remove M6 nuts (x4) securing fuel filler bowl to rear fender. Torque 6 Nm.
  10. Remove M6x20 bolt securing rear fender to chassis. Torque 10 Nm.
  11. Remove M6x20 bolts (x4) securing rear fender to body side panel. Torque 10 Nm.
  12. Remove M6x20 bolt securing rear fender to body side panel. Torque 10 Nm.
  13. Remove M6x20 bolts (x3) securing fender to C pillar panel. Torque 10 Nm.
  14. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing fender to boot box. Torque 10 Nm.
  15. Remove M6x20 bolts (x4) securing fender to boot box bracket and front boot box panel. Torque 10 Nm.
  16. Disconnect harness connector from rear lamp assembly.
  17. Using assistance release and remove rear fender.

    18. CAUTION: Place on secure and suitable surface to prevent damage.

    Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  18. Remove M8x16 bolt and washer securing rear lamp assembly to rear fender. Torque 10 Nm.
  19. Remove M6x12 bolt securing rear lamp assembly to rear fender. Torque 6 Nm.
  20. Remove M5 nut securing rear lamp assembly to rear fender. Torque 4 Nm.
  21. Remove rear lamp assmebly.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Clean off excess adhesive from fender emblem area and renew adhesive.
  3. Check fender gap and flush tolerances - see Alignment Specifications for Body Panels and Closures section.
