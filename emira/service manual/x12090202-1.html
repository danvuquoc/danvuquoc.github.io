Door Mirror - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 12.09.02-02

    Removal

  1. Remove door water deflector panel - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove clips (x3) securing door mirror harness to door panel.
  3. Partially remove door corner trim access panel to access door mirror fixings.
  4. Remove M6x20 bolts (x3) securing door mirror to door panel. Torque 10 Nm.

    5. NOTE: When the final bolt is removed support the door mirror so it does not drop unexpectedly.

  5. Carefully pull harness through door panel to remove door mirror from door panel.
  6. Remove door mirror from vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
