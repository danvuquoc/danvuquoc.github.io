Door Check Strap - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 10.15.13-02

    Removal

  1. Partially remove door water deflector panel - left side.
  2. Remove M8x30 torx bolt securing door check strap to A pillar. Torque 18 Nm.
  3. Move door to gain access to check strap.

    4. CAUTION: Do not force door beyond door hinge stop position with check strap removed or damage may occur.

  4. Remove M6 nuts (x2) securing door check strap to door panel. Torque 7 Nm.
  5. Remove door check strap from door panel.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
