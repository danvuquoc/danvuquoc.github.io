Door Release Handle - Exterior - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 10.17.15-02

    Removal

  1. Partially remove water deflector panel left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disengage clip securing pull rod to lock assembly.

    3. NOTE: Inner door skin not illustrated for clarity.

  3. Disengage release cable from bracket.
  4. Disengage release cable from exterior door handle latch release lever.
  5. Remove M6 nuts (x3) securing exterior door handle to door panel. Torque 10 Nm.
  6. Remove exterior door handle from vehicle.

    7. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  7. Remove M5x10 bolt securing emergency lock barrel to exterior door handle. Torque 6 Nm.
  8. Remove 5x16 screws (x2) securing emergency lock barrel to exterior door handle.
  9. Remove emergency lock barrel from exterior door handle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
