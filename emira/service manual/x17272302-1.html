Infotainment Screen Assembly

Print

Operation Code: 17.27.23-02

    Removal

  1. Disengage clips (x8) securing center vent to instrument panel.
  2. Remove centre vent.
  3. Remove lower fascia panel. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove M4x16 self tapping screws (x4) securing infotainment screen to instrument panel.
  5. Remove M4x16 self tapping screws (x3) securing bracket to infotainment screen.
  6. Remove bracket from infotainment screen.
  7. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from infotainment screen.
  8. Remove infotainment screen.
