Pipe - Fuel Feed To Engine - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 44.03.07-02

    Removal

  1. Depressurise fuel system. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove fuel tank. Refer to procedure.
  3. Release bushings (x3) securing fuel feed pipe to underbody assembly.
  4. Remove bushings (x3) from fuel feed pipe.
  5. Release clips (x2) securing fuel feed pipe to evap line.
  6. Remove rear wheelarch liner left side. Refer to procedure.
  7. Release clip securing fuel feed pipe to evap line.
  8. Remove airbox assembly. Refer to procedure.
  9. Remove M6x12 bolt securing fuel feed pipe to rear subframe stay. Torque 10 Nm.
  10. Release clip securing fuel feed pipe to bracket.
  11. Release clips (x2) securing fuel feed pipe to bracket.
  12. Release quick connector and disconnect fuel feed pipe from engine.

    13. NOTE: Pinch the two buttons on quick connector and pull to remove.

    NOTE: Plug quick connector to prevent ingress of dirt.

  13. Carefully pull fuel feed pipe through aperture in underbody assembly.
  14. Remove fuel feed pipe.

    15. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
