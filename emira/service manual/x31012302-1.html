Wishbone Assembly Upper - Front - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 31.01.23-02

    Remove

  1. Remove wheelarch liner - front - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove and discard M12 nut securing anti-roll bar drop link to upper wishbone. Torque 36 Nm.

    3. NOTE: Insert 5mm hex bit into ball pin stud to prevent anti roll bar drop link ball joint from spinning during removal / installation.

  3. Remove anti-roll bar drop link from upper wishbone.
  4. Remove M6x12 bolt securing brake hose bracket to upper wishbone.
  5. Loosen but do not fully remove nut securing upper wishbone ball joint to hub carrier.

    6. NOTE: Loosen the ball joint nut until the locking threads are clear of the ball joint pin threads.

  6. Use ball joint splitter to separate upper wishbone ball joint from hub carrier.
  7. Remove and discard nut securing upper wishbone ball joint from hub carrier. Torque 86 Nm.
  8. Remove ball joint from hub carrier.
  9. Gain access to upper wishbone fixings through access hole in front structure.
  10. Remove M12x85 bolt securing front upper wishbone to front structure. Torque 86 Nm.

    11. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  11. Remove M12x85 bolt securing rear upper wishbone to front structure. Torque 86 Nm.

    12. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  12. Remove upper wishbone from vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew lock nut fasteners that have been removed during removal procedure.
  3. Only torque tighten wishbone bolts once vehicle is in the normal ride height position.
  4. Perform a front steering and suspension geometry inspection and adjustment. Refer to procedure.
