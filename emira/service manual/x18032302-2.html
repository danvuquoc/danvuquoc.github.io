O-Ring - Compressor - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 18.03.23-02

    Removal

  1. Recover Air Conditioning (AC) refrigerant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear bulkhead access panel. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove bolts (x2) securing AC compressor pipes to AC compressor. Torque 20 Nm.
  4. Disconnect AC compressor pipes (x2) from AC compressor.
  5. Remove and discard O-rings (x2).

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew O-rings (x2) with new refrigerant oil.
  3. Recharge AC refrigerant.
