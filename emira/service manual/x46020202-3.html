Hose - Engine Inlet To Body Pipe - Right Side - V6

Print

Operation Code: 46.02.02-02

    Removal

  1. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove front engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove rear wheelarch liner - right side.

    4. NOTE: Procedure is the same as for left side component.

  4. Release quick connector from coolant hose.
  5. Disconnect coolant hose from body pipe.
  6. Release clamp securing coolant hose to engine inlet.
  7. Disconnect coolant hose from engine inlet.
  8. Remove inlet hose.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
