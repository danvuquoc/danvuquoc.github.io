Pipe - Tank to Canister - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 44.05.08-02

    Removal

  1. Remove charcoal canister. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M5x12 bolts (x2) securing luggage hooks to vehicle.
  3. Remove luggage hooks (x2).
  4. Remove fuel tank bay trim.
  5. Remove grommet.
  6. Disconnect evaporative pipe from fuel tank.

    7. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  7. Release clip securing evaporative pipe to fuel line.
  8. Release clip securing evaporative pipe to coolant pipe.
  9. Release clips (x2) securing evaporative pipe to fuel line.

    10. NOTE: Coolant hoses shown removed for clarity.

  10. Remove evaporative pipe to canister.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal.
