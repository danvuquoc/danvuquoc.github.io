Receiver Dryer Assembly

Print

Operation Code: 18.03.08-02

    Removal

  1. Depressurise air conditioning system. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing air conditioning inlet and evaporator pipes to receiver dryer assembly. Torque 10 Nm.
  3. Disconnect air conditioning inlet and evaporator pipes.
  4. Loosen but do not fully remove clamps securing receiver dryer assembly to bracket.
  5. Remove receiver dryer assembly.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. NOTE: The word ‘IN’ is etched next to the inlet port on the top of the receiver dryer, the condenser outlet pipe must be connected to this port.

  2. Install new 'O' rings coated in an approved refrigerant oil.
