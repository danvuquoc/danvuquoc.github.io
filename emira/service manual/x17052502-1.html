USB Charging Port

Print

Operation Code: 17.05.25-02

    Removal

  1. Open arm rest.
  2. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  3. Disengage clips (x2) securing charging port to centre console and remove USB charging port.
  4. Disconnect harness connectors from USB charging port.
  5. Remove USB charging port.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
