ABS Module

Print

Operation Code: 33.03.12-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front undertray. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector from vehicle dynamics domain master (VDDM) module.
  3. Remove M6 nut securing ground cable to vehicle.
  4. Remove ground cable and move harness aside.
  5. Label brake pipes (x6) connected to VDDM module
  6. Remove union nuts (x2) securing inlet brake hoses to VDDM module. Torque 18 Nm.

    7. CAUTION: Plug pipe connections to prevent fluid loss and ingress of moisture or dirt.

    CAUTION: If brake fluid is spilt on the paintwork wash off immediately with clean water.

    CAUTION: Brake fluid will discharge from brake hose when removing union nut. Use a container to collect brake fluid.

  7. Remove inlet brake hoses (x2).
  8. Remove union nuts (x4) securing outlet brake hoses to VDDM module. Torque 16 Nm.

    9. CAUTION: Plug pipe connections to prevent fluid loss and ingress of moisture or dirt.

    CAUTION: If brake fluid is spilt on the paintwork wash off immediately with clean water.

    CAUTION: Brake fluid will discharge from brake hose when removing union nut. Use a container to collect brake fluid.

  9. Remove brake outlet hoses (x4).
  10. Remove M8x20 bolts (x3) securing VDDM module bracket to vehicle. Torque 24 Nm.
  11. Remove VDDM module.

    12. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  12. Remove F10x20 torx bolt securing VDDM module to bracket. Torque 27 Nm.
  13. Remove VDDM module.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Calibrate replacement module using Lotus Insight tool.
  3. Bleed brakes using Lotus Insight tool.
