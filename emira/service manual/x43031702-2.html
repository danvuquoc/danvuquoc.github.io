Purge Line - Purge Valve To Purge Line Intermediate - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 43.03.17-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear bulkhead access panel. Refer to procedure.
  3. Release connector and disconnect purge line from purge line - intermediate to bleed line.

    4. NOTE: Plug purge line to prevent ingress of dirt.

  4. Release connector and disconnect purge line from purge valve.

    5. NOTE: Plug purge valve to prevent ingress of dirt.

  5. Release connector and disconnect purge line from bleed line.

    6. NOTE: Plug bleed line to prevent ingress of dirt.

  6. Release clip securing purge line to bleed line air intake fitting.
  7. Remove purge line.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
