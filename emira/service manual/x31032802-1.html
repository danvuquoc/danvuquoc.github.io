Anti-Roll Bar - Rear

Print

Operation Code: 31.03.28-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear diffuser. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove and discard M12 torque nut securing right side anti-roll bar drop link to anti roll bar. Torque 36 Nm.

    3. NOTE: Insert 5mm hex bit into ball pin stud to prevent anti roll bar drop link ball joint from spinning during removal / installation.

  3. Remove and discard M12 torque nut securing left side anti-roll bar drop link to anti roll bar. Torque 36 Nm.
  4. Remove M10x35 bolts (x4) securing anti-roll bar brackets to vehicle. Torque 45 Nm.
  5. Remove anti-roll bar brackets (x2).
  6. Remove anti-roll bar.

    7. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  7. Remove anti-roll bar bushes (x2) from anti-roll bar.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal except for the following:
  2. Install new anti-roll bar bushes so the split in the bush is orientated to front of car.
