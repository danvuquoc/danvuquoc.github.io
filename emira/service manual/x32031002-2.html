Hose Assembly - Reservoir To Pump - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 32.03.10-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front undertray. Refer to procedure.
  2. Release clamp securing reservoir/pump hose to pump.
  3. Disconnect reservoir/pump hose from pump.

    4. WARNING: Power steering fluid is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  4. Remove radiator duct outlet left side. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove radiator duct outlet right side.

    6. NOTE: Procedure is the same as left side component.

  6. Remove scrivets (x3) securing HVAC air inlet to vehicle.
  7. Remove M5x12 screws and washers (x2) securing HVAC air inlet to filter lid.
  8. Remove HVAC air inlet.
  9. Remove scrivets (x6) securing centre radiator duct to front subframe.
  10. Remove centre radiator duct.
  11. Release clip securing reservoir/pump hose to return hose.
  12. Release clamp securing reservoir/pump hose to reservoir.
  13. Disconnect reservoir/pump hose from reservoir.
  14. Remove reservoir/pump hose.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Fill power steering system. Refer to technical data.
  3. Bleed power steering system. Refer to procedure.
