Air Intake - Electronic Throttle Actuator - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 42.04.08-02

    Removal

  1. Remove charge air cooler. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear bulkhead access panel. Refer to procedure.
  3. Disconnect bleed line quick connector from air intake.

    4. NOTE: Pinch the two buttons on the quick connector and pull to remove.

    NOTE: Plug bleed line to prevent ingress of dirt.

  4. Remove screws (x2) securing air intake to electronic throttle actuator. Torque 6 Nm.
  5. Remove air intake.

    6. NOTE: Remove and discard air intake gasket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded air intake gasket.
