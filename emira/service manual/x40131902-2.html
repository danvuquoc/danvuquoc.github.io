High Temperature Thermostat Housing Assembly - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.13.19-02

Important information

WARNING: Risk of scalding. Allow coolant to cool completely before proceeding.

WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    Removal

  1. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove charge air distributor. Refer to procedure.
  3. Disconnect harness connector from high temperature thermostat.
  4. Remove bolt securing coolant feed pipe to high temperature thermostat. Torque 8 Nm.
  5. Disconnect coolant feed pipe from high temperature thermostat.

    6. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

    NOTE: Remove and discard O-ring.

  6. Disconnect coolant return pipe from high temperature thermostat.
  7. Remove bolts (16) (x3) securing high temperature thermostat (1) to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  8. Remove high temperature theermostat.

    9. Remove and discard gasket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.

    2. NOTE: Renew discarded O-ring and gasket.
