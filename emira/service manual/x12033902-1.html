Bumper - Rear - Centre - Grill

Print

Operation Code: 12.03.39-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear diffuser. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove and discard cable ties (x3) securing bumper harness to centre bumper grill.
  3. Move harness aside.
  4. Remove 5x12 self tapping screws (x8) securing centre bumper grill to rear bumper.
  5. Remove centre bumper grill.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded cable ties.
