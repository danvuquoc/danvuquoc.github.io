Primary Thermostat - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.13.03-02

Important information

WARNING: Risk of scalding. Allow coolant to cool completely before proceeding.

WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    Removal

  1. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove engine mount - right side. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove boot box access panel. Refer to procedure.
  4. Release spring clamp securing coolant hose (4) to coolant pipe (3).
  5. Disconnect coolant hose from coolant pipe.

    6. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  6. Remove bolts (x4) securing heat shield (5) to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  7. Remove heat shield.
  8. Remove M6 bolts (2) (x4) securing coolant pipe to engine. Torque 9 Nm.
  9. Remove coolant pipe.
  10. Remove bolts (6) (x5) securing primary thermostat (1) to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  11. Remove primary thermostat.

    12. Remove and discard gasket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.

    2. NOTE: Renew discarded gasket.
