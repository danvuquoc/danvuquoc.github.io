Door Sill Outer Panel - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 10.02.13-02

    Removal

  1. Remove sill finisher left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear fender left side. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove front fender left side. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove M6x20 bolt securing rear of sill outer panel to door sill bracket. Torque 10 Nm.
  5. Remove M6x16 bolts (x2) securing front of sill outer panel to bodyside panel. Torque 10 Nm.
  6. Remove M6x16 bolts (x3) securing top of sill outer panel to vehicle. Torque 10 Nm.
  7. Remove M5x18 bolts (x8) securing bottom of sill outer panel to inner door sill panel. Torque 6 Nm.
  8. Using assistance remove sill outer panel with lower air duct.

    9. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  9. Remove scrivets (x4) securing lower air duct to sill outer panel.
  10. Remove lower air duct from sill outer panel.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Check gap and flush tolerances - see Alignment Specifications for Body Panels and Closures section.
