    Removal

  1. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  3. Disconnect harness connector from Mass Airflow (MAF) sensor and move harness aside.
  4. Remove screws (x2) securing MAF sensor to MAF housing.
  5. Remove MAF sensor.
