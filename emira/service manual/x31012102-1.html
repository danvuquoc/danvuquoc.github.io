Anti-Roll Bar - Front

Print

Operation Code: 31.01.21-02

    Removal

  1. Remove undertray - front. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove and discard M12 nut securing left side drop link to anti-roll bar. Torque 36 Nm.

    3. NOTE: Insert 5mm hex bit into ball pin stud to prevent anti roll bar drop link ball joint from spinning during removal / installation.

  3. Disengage left side anti-roll bar drop link ball joint from anti-roll bar.
  4. Remove and discard M12 nut securing right side drop link to anti-roll bar. Torque 36 Nm.

    5. NOTE: Insert 5mm hex bit into ball pin stud to prevent anti roll bar drop link ball joint from spinning during removal / installation.

  5. Disengage right side anti-roll bar drop link ball joint from anti-roll bar.
  6. Remove M10 nuts (x4) securing anti-roll bar brackets to front structure. Torque 45 Nm.
  7. Remove anti-roll bar brackets.
  8. Remove anti-roll bar from vehicle.

    9. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  9. Remove anti-roll bar bushes from anti-roll bar.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Install new anti-roll bar bushes so the split in the bush is orientated to the rear of car.
