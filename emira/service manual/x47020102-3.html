Transmission Assembly - V6

Print

Operation Code: 47.02.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Release clips (x2) securing harness to transmission.
  3. Disconnect harness connector from transmission.
  4. Release clip securing harness to transmission.
  5. Disconnect harness connector from starter.
  6. Release clips (x2) securing harness to transmission.
  7. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from shift actuator.
  8. Disconnect shift actuator link rod from transmission.
  9. Remove M8x20 bolts (x3) securing shift actuator to bracket.
  10. Remove shift actuator.
  11. Release clips (x2) securing harness to bracket.
  12. Remove M10x25 bolts (x2) securing bracket to transmission. Torque 65 Nm.
  13. Remove M8x20 bolts (x2) securing bracket to transmission.
  14. Remove bracket from transmission.
  15. Disconnect harness connector from transmission.
  16. Remove M10x25 bolts (x3) securing front transmission mount to transmission. Torque 65 Nm.
  17. Remove transmission mount.
  18. Remove M10x25 bolts (x3) securing rear transmission mount to transmission. Torque 65 Nm.
  19. Remove M8x20 bolt securing rear transmission mount to transmission. Torque 24 Nm.
  20. Remove transmission mount.
  21. Remove M6x12 bolt securing transmission breather pipe to engine block.
  22. Remove M10 bolts (x4) securing transmission to engine block. Torque 45 Nm.
  23. Remove M12x55 bolts (x6) securing transmission to engine block. Torque 65 Nm.
  24. Using assistance remove transmission assembly.
