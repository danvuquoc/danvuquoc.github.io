Door Glass - Alignment

Print

Operation Code: 11.01.00-00

    Raising/Lowering Glass During Alignment

  1. Install window motor to window lift mechanism.
  2. Install 5x22 screws (x3) securing window motor to window lift mechanism.
  3. Connect harness connectors (x2) to window motor.
  4. Support window motor using one hand whilst operating window switch to raise/lower door glass.

    5. NOTE: Door interior panel must be plugged into door harness to enable window switches to operate window regulator motor.

  5. To set auto drop, fully raise window and hold switch for 2 seconds until a click is heard. Repeat process for fully lowered position.

    6. NOTE: Perform with door open.

  6. Close door, raise and lower window to ensure door glass drop is working correctly.

    Level Adjustment

  1. Install screws (x2) in middle of holes in door glass.

    2. NOTE: Tolerance between clamp screws and holes in door glass allows for a small amount of rotational adjustment to level door glass.

  2. Push door glass forwards into guide rail channel, keeping screws in middle of holes in door glass.

    3. NOTE: Make sure door glass is located into front door glass channel during installation.

  3. Tighten screws (x2) securing door glass to window lift mechanism.

    Height Adjustment

  1. Turn screws (x2) on rear of retaining clamps on window lift mechanism to adjust door glass height when in fully closed position.

    2. NOTE: On upwards glass movement, when stepped stops of retaining clamps touch corresponding tabs on window lift mechanism runners, a signal is sent to window motor controller module to stop window motor. This adjustment is normally not necessary.

    Tilt Adjustment

  1. Using a screwdriver, turn studs (x2) on window lift mechanism runners while using a spanner to tighten the nuts (x2) at the same time to adjust lower edge of door glass inwards/outwards. Torque 3.5 Nm.

    2. NOTE: Studs on a new window lift mechanism are set in the nominal position. It is recommend to install window lift mechanism in position, hold studs in position with a screwdriver and use a spanner to tighten nuts at same time, then see if adjustment is required. Make sure both studs are adjusted equally.

  2. Adjust window lift mechanism to align rear upper edge of door glass.

    3. NOTE: Lower mounting brackets of window lift mechanism have a elongated holes to allow inward/outward movement of rear upper edge of door glass for seal engagement.

  3. Tighten M6x16 bolts (x2) securing window lift mechanism to door. Torque 7 Nm.

    4. NOTE: Holes in base of door provide access to tighten bolts when lower mounting brackets of window lift mechanism are in desired position.
