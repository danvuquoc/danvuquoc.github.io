Master Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 33.03.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove brake master cylinder reservoir. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove brake union nuts (x2) from brake master cylinder. Torque 16 Nm.
  3. Remove nuts (x2) securing brake master cylinder to brake booster. Torque 25 Nm.

    4. CAUTION: If brake fluid is spilt on the paintwork wash off immediately with clean water.

    CAUTION: Plug pipe connections to prevent fluid loss and ingress of moisture or dirt.

  4. Remove brake master cylinder.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Bleed brakes using Lotus Insight tool.
