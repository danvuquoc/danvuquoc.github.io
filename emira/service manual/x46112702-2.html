Pipe - Water Pump - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 46.11.27-02

    Removal

  1. Remove airbox assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.
  3. Release clamp securing coolant hose to water pump pipe.
  4. Disconnect coolant hose from water pump pipe.
  5. Allow coolant to drain.

    6. WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain coolant into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  6. Release clamp securing coolant hose to water pump pipe.
  7. Disconnect coolant hose from water pump pipe.
  8. Release clamp securing coolant hose to water pump pipe.
  9. Disconnect coolant hose from water pump pipe.
  10. Release clip securing engine harness to water pump pipe.
  11. Remove M6 nut securing water pump pipe to rear bulkhead. Torque 10 Nm.
  12. Remove M6 nut securing water pump pipe to rear bulkhead. Torque 10 Nm.
  13. Remove M6 nut securing water pump pipe to rear bulkhead. Torque 10 Nm.
  14. Remove water pump pipe.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Refill coolant.
  3. Start engine and allow to idle.

    4. NOTE: Run the engine with the reservoir cap off to bleed the system.

  4. Refit coolant reservoir cap.
