Exhaust Manifold - Right side - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.21.14-02

    Removal

  1. Remove downpipe. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove boot box access panel. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove oxygen sensor from exhaust manifold.
  4. Remove nuts (x5) securing exhaust manifold to cylinder head. Torque 37 Nm.
  5. Remove exhaust manifold.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
