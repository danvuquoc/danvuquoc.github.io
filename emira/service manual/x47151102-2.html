Driveshaft Bearing Bracket - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 47.15.11-02

    Removal

  1. Remove connecting shaft. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove tie bar mount - rear right side lower. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove engine mount bracket - rear right side lower. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove nut (5) securing catalytic converter to exhaust bracket (7). Torque 20 Nm.
  5. Remove M8x20 bolts (6) (x2) securing exhaust bracket to driveshaft bearing bracket (9). Torque 20 Nm.
  6. Remove exhaust bracket.
  7. Remove M10 bolts (8) (x3) securing driveshaft bearing bracket to engine. Torque 58 Nm + 90 degrees.
  8. Remove driveshaft bearing bracket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of the removal procedure.
