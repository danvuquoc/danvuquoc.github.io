Pressure / Temperature Sensor - Indirect - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 42.05.17-02

    Removal

  1. Depressurise fuel system. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector from fuel pressure / temperature sensor.
  3. Remove fuel pressure / temperature sensor. Torque 38 Nm.

    4. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

    NOTE: Plug fuel rail to fuel pressure/temperature sensor aperture to prevent ingress of dirt.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Apply clean engine oil to threads and mating surfaces of fuel pressure/temperature sensor.
