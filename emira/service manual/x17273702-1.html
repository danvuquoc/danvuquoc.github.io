Speaker - Fascia

Print

Operation Code: 17.27.37-02

    Removal

  1. Remove sun sensor / speaker panel. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove 4x16 self tapping screws (x3) securing speaker to windscreen defroster panel.
  3. Disconnect harness connector from fascia speaker.
  4. Remove fascia speaker.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
