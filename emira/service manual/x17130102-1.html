Headlamp Assembly - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 17.13.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove wheelarch liner - front - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M6x16 bolts with washers (x2) and M6 nuts with washers (x2) securing radiator support bracket. Torque 10 Nm.
  3. Move bracket aside to gain access to headlamp.
  4. Remove M4x16 bolt securing headlamp adjuster cable to fender bracket. Torque 2.25 Nm.
  5. Remove M6x35 bolt securing front of headlamp to fender. Torque 10 Nm.

    6. NOTE: Access front headlamp fixing through opening between headlamp and fender.

  6. Remove M6 nut securing rear of headlamp to fender. Torque 8 Nm.
  7. Remove M6x35 bolts (x2) securing rear of headlamp to fender. Torque 10 Nm.
  8. Remove headlamp from vehicle through wheelarch area.
  9. Disconnect electrical connector connecting headlamp to main harness.

    10. NOTE: Access electrical connector through opening between headlamp aperture.

    Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  10. Remove screws (x3) securing control module to headlamp.
  11. Remove control module from headlamp to gain access to electrical connectors.
  12. Disconnect electrical connectors (x4) connecting headlamp harness to control module.
  13. Remove control module from headlamp.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Check and adjust headlamp alignment.
