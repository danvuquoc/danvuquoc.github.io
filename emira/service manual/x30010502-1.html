Front Subframe Assembly

Print

Operation Code: 30.01.05-02

    Removal

  1. Remove radiator. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove auxiliary radiator assembly. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove recirculation pump. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove receiver dryer assembly. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove front fender left side.
  6. Remove front fender right side. Refer to procedure.

    7. NOTE: Procedure is the same as for left side component.

  7. Remove front lower wishbone assembly left side. Refer to procedure.
  8. Remove front lower wishbone assembly right side.

    9. NOTE: Procedure is the same as for left side component.

  9. Remove front upper wishbone assembly left side. Refer to procedure.
  10. Remove front upper wishbone assembly right side.

    11. NOTE: Procedure is the same as for left side component.

  11. Remove front spring and damper assembly left side. Refer to procedure.
  12. Remove front spring and damper assembly right side.

    13. NOTE: Procedure is the same as for left side component.

  13. Remove steering rack assembly. Refer to procedure.
  14. Remove front brake hose left side. Refer to procedure.
  15. Remove front brake hose right side.

    16. NOTE: Procedure is the same as for left side component.

  16. Remove heater bypass water valve. Refer to procedure.
  17. Remove heater/air conditioning housing assembly. Refer to procedure.
  18. Remove horn left side. Refer to procedure.
  19. Remove horn right side.

    20. NOTE: Procedure is the same as for left side component.

  20. Disconnect harness connector from siren.
  21. Remove M6x16 bolts (x2) securing siren to front subframe.
  22. Remove siren.
  23. Release clips (x2) securing harness to front subframe.
  24. Release clips (x2) securing harness to coolant hose.
  25. Release clips (x2) securing harness to front subframe.
  26. Release clip securing harness to front subframe.
  27. Release clips (x6) securing harness to front subframe.
  28. Move harness aside.
  29. Remove M6x20 bolt securing AC pipe to front subframe.
  30. Remove M6x16 bolt securing coolant hose to front subframe. Torque 10 Nm.
  31. Remove M6x16 bolt securing AC pipe to front subframe. Torque 10 Nm.
  32. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing AC pipe and PAS pipe to front subframe. Torque 9 Nm.
  33. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing AC pipes to front subframe. Torque 9 Nm.
  34. Remove M8 nut securing ground cable to front subframe. Torque 24 Nm.
  35. Remove M6x16 bolt securing coolant hose to front subframe. Torque 10 Nm.
  36. Remove M6x30 bolt securing AC pipe to front subframe. Torque 5 Nm.
  37. Remove M6x20 bolt securing PAS pipe to front subframe. Torque 9 Nm.
  38. Remove M6x16 bolts (x3) securing bracket to front subframe. Torque 10 Nm.
  39. Remove M6x40 bolt securing wiper motor to front subframe. Torque 10 Nm.
  40. Remove M6x16 bolts (x3) securing multibracket to front subframe. Torque 10 Nm.
  41. Install suitable lifting table under front subframe.
  42. Lower vehicle until front subframe is supported on lifting table.
  43. Remove M10x30 bolts (x6) securing left side cover plate to vehicle. Torque 45 Nm.
  44. Remove left side cover plate.
  45. Remove M10x30 bolts (x6) securing right side cover plate to vehicle. Torque 45 Nm.
  46. Remove right side cover plate.
  47. Remove M10x30 bolts (x3) securing left side front subframe to vehicle. Torque 45 Nm.
  48. Remove M10x30 bolts (x3) securing right side front subframe to vehicle. Torque 45 Nm.
  49. Remove front subframe.

    50. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted positions of shims and washers.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Perform a front suspension geometry inspection and adjustment. Refer to procedure.
