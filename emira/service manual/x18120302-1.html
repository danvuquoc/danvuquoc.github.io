Actuator - Demist / Defog

Print

Operation Code: 18.12.03-02

    Removal

  1. Remove instrument panel assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M5x12 bolts (x2) securing left side foot duct to underbody assembly. Torque 5 Nm.
  3. Disengage left side foot duct from air distribution unit.
  4. Remove left side foot duct from air distribution unit.
  5. Repeat steps 3 through 4 for right side foot duct.
  6. Disengage harness connector from air distribution unit.
  7. Remove M8x25 bolt securing air distribution unit to underbody assembly. Torque 18 Nm.
  8. Remove air distribution unit.
  9. Disengage harness connector from demist/defog actuator.
  10. Remove screws (x2) securing demist/defog actuator to air distribution unit.
  11. Remove demist/defog actuator.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. After installation perform a diagnostic read and clear error memory using Lotus Insight tool.
