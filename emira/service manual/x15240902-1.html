Carpet - Luggage Compartment - Rear

Print

Operation Code: 15.24.09-02

    Removal

  1. Remove tailgate latch cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove luggage compartment floor carpet. Refer to procedure.
  3. Release clips (x8) securing luggage compartment rear carpet to bootbox.
  4. Disconnect harness connector from luggage compartment lamp.
  5. Remove luggage compartment rear carpet.

    6. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  6. Release clips (x4) securing luggage compartment lamp to luggage compartment rear carpet.
  7. Remove luggage compartment lamp.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
