Anti-Roll Bar Drop Link - Rear

Print

Operation Code: 31.03.29-02

    Removal

  1. Raise and support vehicle on 2 post lift. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear wheel. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove and discard M12 nut securing anti-roll bar drop link to wishbone. Torque 36 Nm.

    4. NOTE: Insert 5mm hex bit into ball pin stud to prevent anti roll bar drop link ball joint from spinning during removal / installation.

  4. Remove and discard M12 nut securing anti-roll bar drop link to anti roll bar. Torque 36 Nm.
  5. Remove anti-roll bar drop link.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew all discarded fixings that have been removed during removal procedure.
