Wiper Arm - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 17.21.19-02

    Removal

  1. Open access panel - front. Refer to procedure.
  2. Move wiper arms to the vertical position.
  3. Mark the position of the wiper arm location.
  4. Unclip wiper arm cap.
  5. Remove M10 nut securing wiper arm to splined pivot. Torque 30 Nm.
  6. Remove the wiper arm from splined pivot.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. NOTE: Make sure wiper arm is aligned correctly during installation. Use position marked during removal procedure.
