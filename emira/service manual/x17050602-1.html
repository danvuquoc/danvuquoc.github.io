Auxiliary Power Socket

Print

Operation Code: 17.05.06-02

    Removal

  1. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  2. Move both seats as far forward as possible.
  3. Carefully pull and remove left side rear quarter insert trim panel.
  4. Carefully pull and remove right side rear quarter insert trim panel.
  5. Remove M5x12 Torx bolt securing left side luggage rope hook to vehicle.
  6. Remove left side luggage rope hook.
  7. Remove M5x12 Torx bolt securing right side luggage rope hook to vehicle.
  8. Remove right side luggage rope hook.
  9. Remove tank bay trim.
  10. Release clips (x4) securing centre console armrest cover to centre console.
  11. Remove cover and 12V socket.
  12. Disconnect harness connector from 12V socket.

    13. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  13. Remove nut securing auxiliary power socket to centre console trim.
  14. Remove auxiliary power socket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
