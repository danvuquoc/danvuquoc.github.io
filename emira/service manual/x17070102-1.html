Instrument Pack

Print

Operation Code: 17.07.01-02

    Removal

  1. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove lower steering column shroud. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove dash panel - middle. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove M4x16 self tapping screws (x3) securing instrument panel cap assembly to instrument panel.
  5. Remove instrument panel cap assembly.
  6. Remove M4x16 self tapping screws (x2) securing lower instrument panel cover to instrument panel.
  7. Disengage clips (x8) and remove lower instrument panel cover.
  8. Release upper tabs (x2) securing instrument pack to mounts to gain access to connectors.

    9. NOTE: Drop instrument pack down slightly and then lever out to release the upper tabs.

  9. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from instrument pack.

    10. NOTE: Upper fascia panel not shown for clarity.

  10. Remove instrument pack.

  2. After installation perform a diagnostic read and clear error memory using Lotus Insight tool.
