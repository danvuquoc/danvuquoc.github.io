Switch - Paddle Shift Assembly

Print

Operation Code: 17.04.02-02

    Removal

  1. Remove steering wheel keypad left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove steering wheel keypad right side.

    3. NOTE: Procedure is the same for left side component.

  3. Remove screws (x2) securing left side paddle shift switches to steering wheel.
  4. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from stalk module.
  5. Remove paddle shift switches from steering wheel.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
