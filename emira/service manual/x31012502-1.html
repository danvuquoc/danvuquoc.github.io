Bush - Wishbone Upper - Front - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 31.01.25-02

    Removal

  1. Remove wishbone assembly upper - front. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove old bushes using a hydraulic press.
  3. Bushes can only be removed in one direction. Refer to illustration.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Clean wishbone bores before installing new bushes.
  3. Lubricate the outer surface of the bushes with P80 rubber lubricant to ease installation.
  4. Make sure bushes are inserted into the side of wishbone bore with the chamfered edge.
  5. Push bushes into wishbone until flange of bush contacts wishbone.
