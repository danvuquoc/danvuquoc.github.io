Switch - Hazard Warning

Print

Operation Code: 17.05.08-02

    Removal

  1. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rubber pad from centre console.
  3. Release clips (x11) securing centre console finisher to centre console.

    4. NOTE: Release clips working from front to rear.

  4. Remove centre console finisher.
  5. Remove M4x13 self tapping screws (x2) securing drive mode switch to centre console.
  6. Disconnect harness connector from drive mode switch.
  7. Remove drive mode switch.
  8. Remove M4x13 self tapping screws (x2) securing infotainment switch to centre console.
  9. Disconnect harness connector from infotainment switch.
  10. Remove infotainment switch.
  11. Remove M4x16 screws (x3) securing switch carrier panel to centre console.
  12. Remove switch carrier panel.
  13. Disconnect harness connector from hazard warning switch.
  14. Release clips (x2) securing hazard warning switch to switch carrier panel.
  15. Remove hazard warning switch.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
