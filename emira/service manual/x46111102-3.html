Chargecooler Radiator Assembly - V6

Print

Operation Code: 46.11.11-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front bumper. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove scrivets (x6) and remove front air duct.
  3. Disengage hose clamp and remove hose secured to radiator.

    4. WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain coolant into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  4. Remove M6x16 bolts (x6) and remove bracket. Torque 10 Nm.
  5. Remove M6x16 bolts (x9) and remove bracket from radiator. Torque 10 Nm.
  6. Disengage hose clamp and remove hose secured to radiator.
  7. Remove chargecooler radiator assembly.

    8. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess coolant.

    Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  8. Remove bushes (x4) from radiator.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Refill charge cooler coolant reservoir.
  3. Start engine and allow to idle.

    4. NOTE: Run the engine with the reservoir cap off to bleed the system.

  4. Refit charge cooler coolant reservoir cap.
