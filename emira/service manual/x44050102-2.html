Charcoal Canister - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 44.05.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove coolant reservoir. Refer to procedure.
  3. Release quick connectors (x3) securing evaporative pipes to charcoal canister.

    4. NOTE: Pinch the two buttons on the quick connector and pull to remove.

  4. Disconnect evaporative pipes from charcoal canister.
  5. Remove M5 nut securing charcoal canister to bracket. Torque 6 Nm.
  6. Remove M5 nut securing charcoal canister to bracket. Torque 6 Nm.

    7. NOTE: Charcoal canister shown transparent for clarity.

  7. Lift and remove charcoal canister.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal.

    2. NOTE: Push quick connector until and audible click is heard.
