Module - Audio

Print

Operation Code: 17.27.45-02

    Removal

  1. Remove centre console assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove passenger footrest. Refer to procedure.
  3. Pull back portion of carpet.
  4. Remove scrivet securing passenger footwell trim to vehicle.
  5. Remove M5x16 bolt securing passenger footwell trim to vehicle. Torque 6 Nm.
  6. Remove M6x16 bolt securing passenger footwell trim to vehicle. Torque 6 Nm.
  7. Remove passenger footwell trim.
  8. Disconnect harness connectors (x13) from audio module.
  9. Remove M6x16 bolts (x4) securing audio module to vehicle. Torque 6 Nm.
  10. Remove audio module.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. After installation perform a diagnostic read and clear error memory using Lotus Insight tool.
