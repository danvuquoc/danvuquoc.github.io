B Pillar Trim - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 15.09.01-02

    Removal

  1. Open door.
  2. Position driver seat as far forward as possible.
  3. Remove seat belt cover.
  4. Remove bolt securing seat belt to B pillar. Torque 45 Nm.
  5. Remove airbag trim cover.
  6. Remove M6x20 bolt securing B pillar trim to B pillar. Torque 10 Nm.
  7. Carefully pull B pillar trim to disengage clips (x2).
  8. Remove B pillar trim.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
