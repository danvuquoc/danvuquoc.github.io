Oil Sump - Upper - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.03.04-02

    Removal

  1. Remove transmission assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove oil pick up. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove oil filter. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove bolts (x16) securing upper oil sump to engine block.
    Torque 21 Nm.
    Bolt A. Torque 10 Nm.
  5. Remove nuts (x2) securing upper oil sump to engine block.
    Torque 21 Nm. Nut A.
    Torque 10 Nm.
  6. Remove upper oil sump.
  7. Remove and discard O rings (x2) from timing chain cover.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Clean mating surfaces.
  3. Renew discarded O rings.
