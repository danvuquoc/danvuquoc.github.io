Hose - Engine Outlet To Body Pipe - Left Side - V6

Print

Operation Code: 46.02.01-02

    Removal

  1. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove front engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove rear wheelarch liner - left side. Refer to procedure.
  4. Disconnect Mass Air Flow (MAF) sensor connector.
  5. Loosen hose clamps (x2) securing intake tube to throttle body and airbox assembly.
  6. Release hose clamp securing hose to intake tube.
  7. Disconnect vacuum hose from intake tube.
  8. Remove intake tube.
  9. Release quick connector from coolant hose.
  10. Disconnect coolant hose from body pipe.
  11. Release quick connector from expansion tank overflow pipe.
  12. Disconnect expansion tank overflow pipe from coolant hose.
  13. Release clamp securing coolant hose to engine outlet.
  14. Disconnect coolant hose from engine outlet.
  15. Remove coolant hose.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
