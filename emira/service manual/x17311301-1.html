Battery Disconnect

Print

Operation Code: 17.31.13-01

    Disconnect

  1. Before disconnecting battery perform the following procedure:
  2. Turn engine off. Open tailgate and both doors.

    3. NOTE: Open both doors to prevent damage to the door glass when opening and closing the doors when the battery is disconnected.

  3. Remove key and store away from vehicle.
  4. Wait up to 15 minutes for vehicle to 'deep sleep'. DIM and IHU screen go blank and interior lights extinguish.

    5. NOTE: If cooling fans or pumps are still running, even after 15 minutes, do not disconnect battery until they have stopped.

    NOTE: Automatic transmission models will default to Park (P) if battery is disconnected or becomes discharged.

    NOTE: The electronic parking brake will default to the on position if the battery is disconnected or discharged.

  5. Remove battery cover. Refer to procedure.

    6. WARNING: Risk of burns. Arcing of the battery terminals can occur If you connect or disconnect the battery with electrical load present. Make sure all electrical loads are turned off before you disconnect or connect the battery.

    WARNING: Risk of Explosion. Batteries produce explosive gases. Keep sparks, flames and cigarettes away. Ventilate when charging or using in enclosed space. Always wear the correct PPE when working near batteries.

    WARNING: Risk of Acid burns - Batteries contain sulphuric acid - avoid contact with skin, eyes or clothing. If in contact with skin or eyes; flush with copious amounts of water. Remove contaminated clothing. Seek immediate medical attention. If ingested; seek immediate medical attention . Do not induce vomiting or give fluids to drink

    WARNING: Risk of electrical burns. Always remove metal jewellery and never place metal objects near live battery terminals.

    CAUTION: Risk of damage to control units. Wait at least 15 minutes after turning off ignition before disconnecting the battery.

    NOTE: If the vehicle is fitted with security coded audio equipment, check that the code is available for entering after battery reconnection.

    NOTE: Check that the security alarm is disarmed. If the battery is disconnected when armed, the alarm will be triggered.

  6. Loosen negative terminal nut sufficiently to disconnect clamp. Do not fully remove the nut. Torque 12 Nm.
  7. Disconnect battery sensor terminal.
  8. Remove negative terminal clamp from the battery terminal.
  9. Move negative cable away from battery terminal and insulate so accidental connection cannot be made.
  10. Unclip the battery fuse box cover.
  11. Loosen the positive terminal nut sufficiently to disconnect clamp. Do not fully remove the nut.
  12. Remove positive terminal and battery fuse box assembly from battery and move aside.

    Reconnect

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure. Except for the following:

    2. CAUTION: Risk of damage to electrical system. If you connect a battery with the wrong polarity damage can occur to the electrical system. Always check the correct terminals are being connected.
