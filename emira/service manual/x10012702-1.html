Fender Front - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 10.01.27-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front bumper. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove radiator outlet duct left side. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove scrivet securing centre radiator outlet duct to front fender.
  4. Match mark radiator support bracket and radiator reinforcement.
  5. Remove M6 nuts with washers (x2) securing radiator support bracket to radiator reinforcement. Torque 10 Nm.
  6. Release clips (x2) securing harness and connector to radiator support bracket.
  7. Remove M4x16 bolt securing headlamp adjuster cable to fender bracket. Torque 2.25 Nm.
  8. Remove M6x20 bolts (x3) securing fender bracket to chassis tub. Torque 10 Nm.
  9. Remove M6x20 bolt securing fender bracket to cowl panel bracket. Torque 10 Nm.
  10. Remove M6x45 bolt securing fender bracket to cowl panel bracket. Torque 10 Nm.
  11. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing front bumper support bracket to vehicle. Torque 10 Nm.
  12. Remove front bumper support bracket to vehicle.
  13. Remove M6x20 bolts and washers (x2) securing fender to front side fender mounting bracket. Torque 10 Nm.
  14. Remove M6x20 bolts and washers (x2) securing fender to inner fender mounting bracket. Torque 10 Nm.
  15. Remove M6x25 bolt and washer securing fender to fender reinforcement.
  16. Using assistance, release fender from vehicle and disconnect harness connector from headlamp.
  17. Using assistance, remove fender.

    18. CAUTION: Place on secure suitable surface to prevent damage.

    Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  18. Remove M6x16 bolts with washers (x2) securing radiator support bracket to fender. Torque 10 Nm.
  19. Remove radiator support bracket.
  20. Remove M6x35 bolts (x3) securing headlamp to fender. Torque 10 Nm.
  21. Remove M6 nut securing headlamp to fender. Torque 8 Nm.
  22. Remove headlamp.
  23. Remove M6x20 bolts (x4), remove brackets (x2) and remove lower front fender garnish. Torque 10 Nm.
  24. Remove M5x18 bolts (x5) and remove front access hatch trim. Torque 4 Nm.
  25. Remove M6 nuts (x2) and remove front bumper support bracket.

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Check fender gap and flush tolerances - see Alignment Specifications for Body Panels and Closures section.
