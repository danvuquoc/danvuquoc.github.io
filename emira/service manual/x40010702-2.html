Bracket - Engine Mount - Body - Left Side - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.01.07-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine mount to transmission bracket. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M6x16 bolt securing expansion tank bracket to engine mount bracket. Torque ?? Nm.
  3. Remove M10x30 bolts (x4) securing engine mount bracket to upper subframe. Torque ?? Nm.
  4. Remove engine mount bracket.

    5. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  5. Remove M8x20 bolts (x4) securing engine pad to bracket. Torque ?? Nm.
  6. Remove engine pad from bracket.

    7. NOTE: Always record the quantity and fitted positions of washers.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
