Damper - Clutch Actuation - V6

Print

Operation Code: 47.01.13-02

    Removal

  1. Remove gearbox filter and check valve. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove clutch pipe fittings (x2) from clutch damper.
  3. Remove M6x12 screws (x2) securing clutch damper to bracket. Torque 8 Nm.
  4. Remove clutch damper.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Bleed clutch.
  3. Check and top up brake fluid level. Refer to technical data.
