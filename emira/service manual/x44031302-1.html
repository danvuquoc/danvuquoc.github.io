Fuel Depressurisation

Print

Operation Code: 44.03.13-02

    Removal

  1. Open tailgate.
  2. Release rear engine cover from retaining clips (x4).
  3. Remove rear engine cover.
  4. Release front engine cover from retaining clips (x4).
  5. Remove front engine cover.
  6. Remove fuel pump relay fuse.

    7. NOTE: Fuse no.29 located in rear fuse box.

    NOTE: Refer to Lotus Insight tool for further information.

  7. Start and operate engine until fuel starvation occurs.
  8. Crank engine for a few seconds.
  9. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  10. Clean quick connector before removal.
  11. Disconnect fuel pipe quick connector.

    12. WARNING: Be aware of the possibility of fuel pressure retention in the fuel line cause by a system fault.

    CAUTION: Do not bend or twist the nylon tube.

    NOTE: Pinch the two buttons on the quick connector and pull to remove.

    NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess gasoline.

    NOTE: Cover quick connector with a clean plastic bag to prevent ingress of dirt.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.

    2. NOTE: Push quick connector until an audible click is heard.
