Dual Mass Flywheel - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.11.14-02

    Removal

  1. Remove transmission assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove crankshaft position sensor. Refer to procedure.
  3. Use suitable flywheel holding tool to lock flywheel ring gear to engine block.
  4. Remove bolts (1) (x8) securing dual mass flywheel (2) to engine. Torque 45 Nm + 90 degrees.

    5. CAUTION: To avoid damaging crankshaft, do not use an impact wrench/driver or a similar compressed air tool to remove bolts.

  5. Using assistance, remove dual mass flywheel.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. NOTE: Dowel pin of crankshaft determines installation position.

  2. Inspect rear radial shaft sealing ring (3) for leaks, renew if necessary.
  3. Progressively tighten dual mass flywheel bolts in a diagonal pattern.
