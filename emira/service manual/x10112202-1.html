Tailgate Latch Actuator

Print

Operation Code: 10.11.22-02

    Removal

  1. Remove tailgate latch cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Release straps (x2) and remove vehicle tool kit.
  3. Release strap and remove warning triangle.
  4. Remove luggage floor carpet.
  5. Disengage clips (x8) securing rear boot trim to boot box.
  6. Refer to illustration for trim clip location.
  7. Remove rear boot trim.
  8. Disengage tailgate release cable from tailgate latch actuator.
  9. Remove tailgate release cable from tailgate latch actuator.
  10. Disconnect harness connector from tailgate latch actuator.
  11. Remove M6x12 bolts (x2) securing tailgate latch actuator to boot box. Torque 9 Nm.
  12. Remove tailgate latch actuator.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
