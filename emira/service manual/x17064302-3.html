Oxygen Sensor - Pre Catalytic Converter - Left Side - V6

Print

Operation Code: 17.06.43-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear bulkhead access panel. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M6x14 bolt securing upper heatshield to lower heatshield. Torque 10 Nm.
  3. Open tailgate.
  4. Disconnect harness connector from oxygen sensor.
  5. Release clip securing engine harness to bracket.
  6. Remove M6x16 Allen bolt securing heatshield to cylinder head. Torque 10 Nm.
  7. Remove M6 nut securing heat shield to cylinder head. Torque 10 Nm.
  8. Remove M6x14 bolt securing upper heatshield to lower heatshield. Torque 10 Nm.
  9. Remove M8x25 bolt securing upper heatshield to cylinder head. Torque 24 Nm.
  10. Remove heatshield.
  11. Release clip securing harness connector to bracket.
  12. Unscrew oxygen sensor from exhaust manifold. Torque 45 Nm.
  13. Remove oxygen sensor.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. After installation perform a diagnostic read and clear error memory using Lotus Insight tool.
