Oil Drain - Refill - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.15.25-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front engine bay trim cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove oil filler cap.
  3. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove oil drain plug from sump and discard oil drain plug seal. Torque 40 Nm.
  5. Allow engine oil to drain.

    6. WARNING: Engine oil is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew oil drain plug seal.
  3. Install oil drain plug. Torque 30 Nm.
  4. Fill engine with oil.

    5. NOTE: Service fill volume 5 litres.

    NOTE: Dry fill volume 6 litres.

  5. Allow several minutes for oil to drain through to sump.
  6. Install oil filler cap.
  7. Install rear undertray.
  8. Check engine oil level using dipstick.

    9. NOTE: Oil level should be between the two dots on lower end of dipstick.

  9. Top up oil level if necessary.

    10. NOTE: The difference between high and low dipstick marks is equivalent to approximately 1.5 litres of oil.

  10. Install engine cover.
