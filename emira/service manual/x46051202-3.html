Bracket - Fan - Engine Bay Cooling - V6

Print

Operation Code: 46.05.12-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine bay trim cover - front Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M6x12 bolts (x2) and nuts (x2) securing fan assembly to RH rear frame stay. Torque 10 Nm.
  3. Remove M6x12 bolts (x2) securing fan assembly to vehicle. Torque 10 Nm.
  4. Remove fan assembly.

    5. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  5. Remove M4x10 bolts (x4) and washers (x4) securing fan to fan bracket.
  6. Remove fan bracket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
