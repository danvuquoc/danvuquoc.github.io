Transmission Assembly - V6

Print

Operation Code: 47.03.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector from starter.
  3. Remove bolts (x2) securing flywheel cover to adapter plate. Torque 9 Nm.
  4. Remove flywheel cover.
  5. Remove M10x32 bolts (x3) securing front transmission mount to transmission. Torque 65 Nm.
  6. Remove transmission mount.
  7. Remove M10x27 bolts (x4) securing rear transmission mount to transmission. Torque 65 Nm.
  8. Remove transmission mount.
  9. Remove M10x75 bolt securing transmission to engine block. Torque 37 Nm.
  10. Remove M10x60 bolts (x2) securing transmission to engine block. Torque 37 Nm.
  11. Remove M10x35 bolt securing transmission to engine block. Torque 37 Nm.
  12. Remove M12x80 bolts (x2) securing transmission to engine block. Torque 46 Nm.
  13. Remove M12x50 bolts (x2) securing transmission to adapter plate. Torque 64 Nm.
  14. Using assistance remove transmission assembly.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
