Flywheel - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.11.14-02

    Removal

  1. Remove clutch assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Use suitable flywheel holding tool to lock flywheel ring gear to engine block.
  3. Remove flywheel bolts (x8) securing flywheel to engine. Torque 75 Nm.
  4. Remove flywheel from engine block.

    5. NOTE: The spacer ring between the flywheel and engine may fall out during removal.

  5. Remove spacer ring from engine block.

    6. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  6. Remove bolts (x6) and washers securing flywheel ring gear to flywheel. Torque 30 Nm.
  7. Remove flywheel ring gear.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Clean flywheel and ring gear mating surfaces with suitable degreaser.
  3. Apply single bead of permabond A130 to flywheel bolts (x8).

    4. CAUTION: The permabond A130 acts as both a thread lock and sealant to prevent oil leaking from engine onto flywheel and clutch assembly.

  4. Progressively tighten flywheel ring gear and flywheel bolts in a diagonal pattern.
