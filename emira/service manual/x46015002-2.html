Pipe - Sill Auxiliary Radiator Outlet To Engine - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 46.01.50-02

    Removal

  1. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove door sill inner panel - right side.

    3. NOTE: Procedure is the same as left side component.

  3. Remove M6x20 screws (x2) securing bracket to vehicle. Torque 8 Nm.
  4. Remove bracket and foam.
  5. Remove M6x20 bolt securing bracket to vehicle. Torque 8 Nm.
  6. Remove M5x10 screw securing bracket to vehicle. Torque 6 Nm.
  7. Remove bracket and foam.
  8. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing bracket to vehicle. Torque 8 Nm.
  9. Remove M6x12 screws (x3) securing bracket to vehicle. Torque 6 Nm.
  10. Remove bracket and foam.
  11. Remove M6 nut securing bracket to vehicle. Torque 8 Nm.
  12. Remove M6x12 screws (x3) securing bracket to vehicle. Torque 6 Nm.
  13. Remove bracket and foam.
  14. Remove M6x20 screw securing bracket to vehicle. Torque 8 Nm.
  15. Remove M6 nut securing bracket to vehicle. Torque 8 Nm.
  16. Remove bracket and foam.
  17. Release clamp securing auxiliary radiator sill outlet pipe to link hose.
  18. Disconnect auxiliary radiator sill outlet pipe from link hose.

    19. WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain coolant into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  19. Release quick connector and disconnect auxiliary radiator sill outlet pipe from coolant hose.
  20. Remove auxiliary radiator sill outlet pipe.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Refill coolant.
