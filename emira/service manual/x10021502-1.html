Door Sill Inner Panel - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 10.02.15-02

    Removal

  1. Remove door sill outer panel left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M5x18 bolts (x11) and washers securing door sill inner panel to underbody assembly. Torque 6 Nm.

    3. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  3. Remove M6x20 bolt securing rear of door sill inner panel to bracket. Torque 10 Nm.
  4. Remove door sill inner panel.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
