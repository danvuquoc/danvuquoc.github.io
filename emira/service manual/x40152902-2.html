Oil Filter Housing Drain Plug - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.15.29-02

Important Information

WARNING: Engine oil is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested. Wear protective gloves, protective clothing and safety glasses.

    Removal

  1. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove oil drain plug from oil filter housing. Torque 20 Nm.

    4. NOTE: Remove and discard seal.

  4. Allow engine oil to drain.

    5. NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded seals.
  3. Check and top up engine oil.
