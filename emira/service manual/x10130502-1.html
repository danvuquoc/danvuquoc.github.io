Trim Cover Rear Quarter - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 10.13.05-02

    Removal

  1. Remove tailgate trim - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Release retaining clips (x6) securing engine cover to vehicle.
  3. Remove engine cover.
  4. Remove M6x16 bolt securing left side engine cover to vehicle. Torque 10 Nm.
  5. Remove scrivets (x2) securing left side engine cover to vehicle.
  6. Remove engine cover - left side.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
