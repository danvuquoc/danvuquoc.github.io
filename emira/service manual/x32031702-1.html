Track Rod End - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 32.03.17-02

    Removal

  1. Remove road wheel - front - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Record position of nut on steering rack threads.

    3. NOTE: Recording nut position will assist installation and set up of wheel alignment.

  3. Loosen nut securing left side track rod end to steering rack. Torque 45 Nm.

    4. NOTE: Only loosen nut enough to remove track rod end.

  4. Loosen but do not fully remove nut securing left side track rod end ball joint to hub carrier.

    5. NOTE: Loosen the ball joint nut until the locking threads are clear of the ball joint pin threads.

  5. Use ball joint splitter to separate left side track rod end from hub carrier.
  6. Remove and discard nut securing left side track rod end ball joint to hub carrier. Torque 45 Nm.
  7. Unwind left side track rod end from steering rack and remove.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Install track rod end to previously recorded position of nut on steering rack.
  3. Renew all discarded fixings that have been removed during removal procedure.
  4. Perform a front steering and suspension geometry inspection and adjustment. Refer to procedure.
