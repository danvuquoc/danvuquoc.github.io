Dash Panel - Middle - Passenger

Print

Operation Code: 14.05.04-02

    Removal

  1. Remove infotainment screen assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove glove box assembly. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove 4x16 self tapping screws (x4) securing dash panel to instrument panel.
  4. Disengage trim clips (x7) securing dash panel to instrument panel.
  5. Remove dash panel from instrument panel to gain access to glove box switch connector.
  6. Disconnect harness connector from glove box switch.
  7. Remove middle dash panel from instrument panel.

    8. Do not carry out further disassembly if remove for access only.

  8. Disengage clips (x2) and remove glove box switch from dash panel.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
