Crankshaft Oil Seal - Front - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.11.04-02

    Removal

  1. Remove crankshaft pulley. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove and discard crankshaft oil seal.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Apply multipurpose grease to lip of oil seal.
  3. Use SST 09316-60011 to install oil seal.

    4. CAUTION: Keep the lip free from foreign matter.

    CAUTION: Do not tap the oil seal at an angle.

    CAUTION: Make sure that the oil seal edge does not stick out of the timing chain case.
