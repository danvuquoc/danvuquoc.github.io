Wishbone Assembly Lower - Front - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 31.01.18-02

    Removal

  1. Remove wheel arch liner - front. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove anti-roll bar - front. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove M14x110 bolt and M14 nut securing lower damper mounting to lower wishbone. Discard nut. Torque 190 Nm.
  4. Loosen but do not fully remove nut securing lower wishbone ball joint to hub carrier.

    5. NOTE: Loosen the ball joint nut until the locking threads are clear of the ball joint pin threads.

  5. Use ball joint splitter to separate lower wishbone ball joint from hub carrier.
  6. Remove and discard nut securing lower wishbone ball joint to hub carrier. Torque 86 Nm.
  7. Mark position of washers on lower wishbone fixings to aide installation and alignment.

    8. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  8. Remove M12x95 cambolts and M12 nuts (x2) securing lower wishbone to subframe assembly. Torque 86 Nm.
  9. Remove lower wishbone from vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Only torque tighten wishbone bolts once vehicle is in the normal ride height position.
  3. Renew lock nut fasteners that have been removed during removal procedure.
  4. Perform a front steering and suspension geometry inspection and adjustment. Refer to procedure.
