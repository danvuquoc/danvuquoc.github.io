Cover - Door Pocket - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 15.13.18-02

    Removal

  1. Remove interior door pull trim - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M4x13 screws (x2) securing door pocket cover to interior door pull trim.
  3. Release clips (x9) securing door pocket cover to interior door pull trim.
  4. Remove door pocket cover.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
