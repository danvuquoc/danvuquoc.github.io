A Pillar Trim - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 15.07.01-02

    Removal

  1. Carefully pull A pillar trim outwards, then rearwards to disengage clips (x2) securing A pillar trim to vehicle.

    2. CAUTION: Gently pull A pillar trim out and rearwards to prevent damage to the dog house.

    NOTE: Windscreen glass removed for image clarity.

  2. Remove M6x18 bolt securing curtain airbag and A pillar tether strap to vehicle.
  3. Remove A pillar trim to access harness connector.
  4. Disconnect harness connector from tweeter speaker.
  5. Remove A pillar trim.

    6. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  6. Remove tweeter speaker from A pillar trim.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
  2. Position A pillar trim lower locating mounting tabs (x2) to instrument panel.
  3. Slightly pull down airbag curtain down and install A pillar tether strap between airbag curtain mounting and the vehicle A pillar.

    4. CAUTION: Take care not to rotate airbag curtain. Make sure retaining plate is orientated correctly and positioned flush against vehicle A pillar.

  4. Install M6x18 bolt securing curtain airbag and A pillar tether strap to vehicle A pillar. Torque 6 Nm.

    5. NOTE: Make sure bolt passes through A pillar tether strap.

  5. Mark bolt with paint pen to indicate torque has been applied.
  6. Engage clips (x2) securing A pillar trim to vehicle A pillar.
