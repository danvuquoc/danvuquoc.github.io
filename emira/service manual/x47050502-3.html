Cable - Gearshift - V6

Print

Operation Code: 47.05.05-02

    Removal

  1. Remove airbox assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disengage clip securing gearshift cable to selector assembly.
  3. Remove gearshift cable from selector assembly.
  4. Remove M6 bolt securing gearshift cable clamp to bracket. Torque 9 Nm.
  5. Remove gearshift cable clamp.
  6. Release gearshift cable from clip.
  7. Remove centre console assembly. Refer to procedure.
  8. Remove ball joint securing gearshift cable to gear lever.
  9. Remove M6x12 bolt securing gearshift cable clamp to gearshift assembly.
  10. Remove gearshift cable clamp.
  11. Remove fuel tank shear panel. Refer to procedure.
  12. Withdraw gearshift cable from engine bay.
  13. Remove M6x25 bolt securing gearshift cable bracket to vehicle. Torque 10 Nm.
  14. Remove gearshift cable from adhesive strips on fuel tank.
  15. Withdraw gearshift cable from cabin through aperture.
  16. Remove gearshift cable.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Check and adjust gearshift cable. Refer to procedure.
