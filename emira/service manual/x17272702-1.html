Speaker - Door - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 17.27.27-02

    Removal

  1. Remove door trim assembly - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector from door speaker.
  3. Remove 4x16 screws (x3) securing door speaker to door water deflector panel.
  4. Remove door speaker.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
