Door Waist Seal Outer - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 11.01.33-02

    Removal

  1. Remove left side door trim assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disengage clips (x2) securing door waist seal corner piece to door and remove corner piece.
  3. Remove door waist inner seal secured to inner door.
  4. Remove door waist outer seal secured to outer door.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
