Power Assisted Steering (PAS) Pump - V6

Print

Operation Code: 32.03.23-02

    Removal

  1. Release tension on auxiliary drive belt and remove from power steering pump pulley. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove banjo bolt and discard washers (x2) from power steering pump. Torque 45 Nm.

    3. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of shims, washers, locktabs or clips.

  3. Remove power steering fluid hose and move aside.

    4. WARNING: Power steering fluid is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  4. Disengage hose clamp securing power steering fluid hose to power steering pump.
  5. Remove hose from power steering pump and drain oil.

    6. NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  6. Install suitable transmission jack lift under engine/transmission assembly.
  7. Remove M12x90 bolt, nut and washer securing engine mount right side to vehicle. Torque 86 Nm.
  8. Remove M12x35 bolts (x2) securing front engine mount to vehicle. Torque 65 Nm.
  9. Remove M8x25 bolts (x2) securing engine mount right side to vehicle. Torque 24 Nm.
  10. Using transmission jack lift, raise engine/transmission assembly approximately 100 mm (4 in).
  11. Remove bolts (x2) securing power steering pump to engine. Torque 43 Nm.
  12. Remove power steering pump through wheelarch.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded washers.
  3. Fill power steering system. Refer to technical data.
  4. Bleed power steering system. Refer to procedure.
