Washer Bottle Assembly

Print

Operation Code: 17.21.05-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear wheel arch liner left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector from washer bottle level sensor.
  3. Remove M6 nyloc nut and washer securing washer bottle to vehicle. Torque 6 Nm.

    4. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  4. Remove hose from washer pump.
  5. Remove rear engine bay trim cover. Refer to procedure.
  6. Remove M6x16 bolt securing coolant expansion tank to upper mounting bracket. Torque 8 Nm.
  7. Release coolant reservoir and set aside to access connector.
  8. Disconnect harness connector from washer bottle pump.
  9. Remove M6 nyloc nut and washer securing washer bottle to vehicle. Torque 6 Nm.

    10. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  10. Remove washer bottle.

    11. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  11. Remove washer bottle level sensor.
  12. Disengage washer bottle pump from washer bottle.
  13. Remove washer pump.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
