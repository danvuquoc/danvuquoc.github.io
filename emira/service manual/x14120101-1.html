Glove Box Assembly

Print

Operation Code: 14.12.01-01

    Removal

  1. Remove dash end finisher - right side. Refer to procedure.

    2. NOTE: Procedure is the same for left side component.

  2. Open glove box.
  3. Disengage clips (x8) securing glove box inner latch cover to glove box and remove inner latch cover.
  4. Remove M4x16 self tapping screws (x2) securing bottom of glove box to instrument panel.
  5. Remove M4x16 self tapping screws (x4) securing top of glove box to instrument panel.
  6. Remove glove box a small amount to gain access to harness connector.

    7. CAUTION: Take care not to damage glove box harness when removing glovebox.

    NOTE: Support glove box.

  7. Disconnect glove box harness connector from instrument panel harness.
  8. Remove glove box assembly.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
