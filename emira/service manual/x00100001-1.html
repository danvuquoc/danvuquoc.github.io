Raise and Support Vehicle - 2 Post Lift

Print

Operation Code: 00.10.00-01

    Lift

  1. Position the vehicle in the centre of two post lift.

    2. CAUTION: Do not jack or support the vehicle on any other points than those indicated. Failure to comply is likely to result in damage to the body or chassis components.

  2. Position lift arms under lifting points on both sides of vehicle. Refer to illustration for lifting point locations.
  3. Lift vehicle until wheels are clear of the ground then check the stability of vehicle on the lift. If necessary lower lift and reposition vehicle and lift arms.

    4. WARNING: Never work on an incorrectly supported vehicle.

  4. Raise vehicle to required height.

    5. WARNING: Removing large or heavy components can affect the stability of the vehicle. Always check the stability of vehicle during procedures that remove large or heavy components.

    Lower

  1. Lower the vehicle to the floor.

    2. WARNING: Risk of injury. Remove hands and feet from any moving parts. Keep hands and feet clear of car and lift when lowering.

    CAUTION: Risk of damage to vehicle and tools. Remove tools and components from under the vehicle before lowering.

  2. Remove lift arms from underneath vehicle.
  3. Remove vehicle from twin post lift.
