Auxiliary Drive Belt - V6

Print

Operation Code: 41.03.02-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear wheel arch liner - right side.

    3. NOTE: Procedure is the same for left side component.

  3. Rotate belt tensioner counter clockwise to relieve tension from drive belt.

    4. NOTE: If reusing belt, take note of and reinstall in the original direction

    NOTE: Vehicle not shown for clarity.

  4. Use allen key to lock tensioner in fully released position.
  5. Remove belt from external idler pulley, internal idler pulley and supercharger pulley.
  6. Unlock and rotate tensioner clockwise to fully tensioned position.

    7. NOTE: This allows greater clearance when removing the belt.

  7. Remove belt from power steering pump pulley.
  8. Remove belt from external idler pulley.
  9. Remove auxiliary drive belt.

    Installation

  1. Install belt to alternator pulley.
  2. Use suitable block and place between top of alternator pulley and thermostat housing.

    3. CAUTION: Do not force the block to fit with excessive force as this can cause damage to the belt and to the thermostat housing.

  3. Refer to illustration for belt route.
  4. Rotate belt tensioner counter clockwise to relieve tension from drive belt.
  5. Use suitable bar to pull the belt up toward external idler pulley at top of engine through idler bracket pulleys.
  6. Install belt to upper pulleys.
  7. Remove allen key.
  8. Rotate belt tensioner clockwise.
  9. Check belt position.
  10. Remove block.
  11. Start engine and observe belt and pulley operation and direction.
