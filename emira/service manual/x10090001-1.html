Open Access Hatch - Front

Print

Operation Code: 10.09.00-01

    Removal

  1. Unclip trims (x3) from access hatch grille.

    2. NOTE: Unclip from upper edge first.

  2. Refer to illustration for trim clip location.
  3. Remove M6x20 bolts (x3) securing the access panel to access panel brackets. Torque 10 Nm.
  4. Open access hatch and engage access hatch stay.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
