Power Assisted Steering (PAS) Pump Motor - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 32.03.21-02

    Removal

  1. Remove radiator duct outlet left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove radiator duct outlet right side.

    3. NOTE: Procedure is the same as left side component.

  3. Remove scrivets (x3) securing HVAC air inlet to vehicle.
  4. Remove M5x12 screws and washers (x2) securing HVAC air inlet to filter lid.
  5. Remove HVAC air inlet.
  6. If fitted, remove water shield from HVAC unit. Refer to TSB-131-24007.
  7. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from pump motor.
  8. Remove M8x25 bolt securing cooler/pump pipe to pump motor. Torque 18 Nm.

    9. WARNING: Power steering fluid is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  9. Disconnect cooler/pump pipe from pump motor and discard O-ring.
  10. Remove steering rack assembly. Refer to procedure.
  11. Release clamp securing reservoir/pump hose to pump motor.
  12. Disconnect reservoir/pump hose from pump motor.

    13. NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  13. Remove M8x80 bolts (x2) and plate nut securing pump motor to bracket. Torque 18 Nm.
  14. Manoeuvre and remove pump motor.

    15. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  15. Remove M8 nuts (x4) securing bracket to pump motor. Torque 18 Nm.
  16. Remove bracket.
  17. Remove mounts (x4) from pump motor.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded O-ring.
  3. Fill power steering system. Refer to technical data.
  4. Bleed power steering system. Refer to procedure.
