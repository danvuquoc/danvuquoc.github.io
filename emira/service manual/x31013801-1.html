Spring / Damper Assembly - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 31.01.38-01

    Removal

  1. Remove wheelarch liner - front - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove anti-roll bar drop link front - left side. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove M14x110 bolt and M14 nut securing lower damper mounting to lower wishbone. Discard nut. Torque 190 Nm.
  4. Remove M10x30 bolts (x4) securing spring / damper assembly bracket to subframe. Torque 45 Nm.
  5. Lift spring / damper assembly upwards to clear lower wishbone then remove from vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew lock nut fasteners that have been removed during removal procedure.
