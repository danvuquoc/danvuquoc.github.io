Air Filter Element - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 42.01.04-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove 6x20 screws (x4) securing ECM to airbox assembly. Torque 6 Nm.
  3. Move ECM aside to gain access to airbox lid fasteners.
  4. Release clip securing ECM harness to airbox lid.
  5. Remove 5x20 screws (x3) securing airbox lid to airbox assembly. Torque 1.2 Nm.
  6. Remove airbox lid from airbox assembly.
  7. Release hose clamp securing air inlet hose to air filter element. Torque 3 Nm.
  8. Disconnect air inlet hose from air filter element.
  9. Remove air filter element.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Clean inside of airbox before fitting new element.
