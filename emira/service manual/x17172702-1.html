Lamp - Cup Holder - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 17.17.27-02

    Removal

  1. Remove switch panel assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove glove box assembly. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove engine start switch. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove rubber pads (x2) from cup holders.
  5. Remove M4x16 self tapping screws (x3) securing switch pack to centre console and remove switch pack. Torque 2 Nm.
  6. Disengage harness connectors (x3) from switch pack.
  7. Remove M4x16 self tapping screws (x4) securing cup holders to centre console. Torque 2 Nm.
  8. Remove M4x16 self tapping screws (x2) securing centre console upper section to lower section. Torque 2 Nm.
  9. Remove M4x16 self tapping screws (x2) securing centre console upper section to instrument panel. Torque 2 Nm.
  10. Carefully disconnect harness connectors (x3) from instrument panel harness.
  11. Disengage fir tree clip securing tunnel harness to underbody assembly.
  12. Remove centre console upper assembly.
  13. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from cup holder lamps.
  14. Remove M4x16 self tapping screws (x4) securing cupholder to centre console upper assembly.
  15. Remove cup holder.
  16. Release cup holder lamp from cup holder.
  17. Remove cup holder lamp.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
