Bowl Assembly - Fuel Filler

Print

Operation Code: 44.01.06-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear wheel arch liner right side. Refer to procedure.

    2. NOTE: Procedure is the same for left side component.

  2. Open fuel filler flap.
  3. Disengage harness connector secured to fuel flap solenoid.
  4. Loosen hose clamp securing fuel filler pipe to fuel tank hose.
  5. Remove M8x25 bolts (x2) securing filler pipe to vehicle. Torque 24 Nm.
  6. Disconnect fuel evaporation hose quick connector.
  7. Remove M6 nuts (x4) securing fuel filler bowl to vehicle. Torque 6 Nm.
  8. Pull fuel filler bowl assembly down over fuel filler pipe assembly.
  9. Release fuel filler pipe from fuel tank hose.
  10. Remove fuel filler bowl and fuel filler pipe assembly.

    11. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  11. Release hose clamp and remove drainage hose.
  12. Remove fuel filler pipe from filler bowl assembly.
  13. Disengage clips (x4) securing fuel filler cap and remove fuel filler cap.
  14. Disengage and remove fuel flap release solenoid from fuel filler bowl assembly.
  15. Remove spring securing fuel filler flap to filler bowl assembly.
  16. Remove filler cap hinge from fuel filler bowl assembly.
  17. Remove fuel flap from fuel filler bowl assembly.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
