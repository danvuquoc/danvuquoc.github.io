Door Mirror Glass - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 12.09.04-02

    Removal

  1. Carefully pull mirror glass away from mirror body to disengage clips (x8).
  2. Refer to illustration for clip type and location.
  3. Partially pull mirror away from mirror body to access harness connectors (x2).
  4. Disconnect mirror glass harness connectors (x2).
  5. Remove mirror glass from vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
