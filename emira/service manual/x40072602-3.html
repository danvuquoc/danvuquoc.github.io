Cam Cover - Right Side - Bank 1 - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.07.26-02

    Removal

  1. Remove ignition coils right side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove oil control valves. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove camshaft position sensors. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove oil control valve pipe right side. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove bolts (x12) securing cylinder head cover to cylinder head. Bolt A.
    Torque 21 Nm. Rest of bolts.
    Torque 10 Nm.
  6. Remove cylinder head cover.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
