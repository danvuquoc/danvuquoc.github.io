Primary Coolant Pump Housing Assembly - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.13.15-02

Important Information

WARNING: Risk of scalding. Allow coolant to cool completely before proceeding.

WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    Removal

  1. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove charge air distributor. Refer to procedure.
  3. Release clamps (13) (x2) and remove hose (14). Torque 2 Nm.

    4. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

    NOTE: Remove and discard clamps.

  4. Release clamp and remove charge air cooler pipe (7).
  5. Remove M6 bolt (4) securing coolant pipe (2) to coolant pump housing (3). Torque 8 Nm.
  6. Disconnect coolant pipe from coolant pump housing.

    7. NOTE: Remove and discard O-ring.

  7. Remove bolt (5) securing coolant pipe (1) to coolant pump housing. Torque 8 Nm.
  8. Disconnect coolant pipe from coolant pump housing.

    9. NOTE: Remove and discard O-ring.

  9. Release spring clamp securing coolant hose (6) to coolant pump housing.
  10. Disconnect coolant hose from coolant pump housing.
  11. Remove bolt (8) securing coolant pipe (9) to engine and move coolant pipe aside. Torque 9 Nm.
  12. Remove bolts (10) (x6) securing coolant pump housing to engine. Torque 9 Nm.
  13. Remove coolant pump housing.

    14. NOTE: Remove and discard gasket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded clamps, O-rings and gasket.
