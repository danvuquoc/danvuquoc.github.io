Switch - Airbag Defeat

Print

Operation Code: 17.05.10-02

    Removal

  1. Remove glove box inner liner. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disengage clips (x2) and remove airbag defeat switch from glove box inner liner.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.

    2. NOTE: Check telltale in Driver Information Module (DIM) Overhead Console illuminate and extinguish when disabling/enabling Passenger Airbag (PAB).
