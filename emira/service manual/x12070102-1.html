Licence Plate Plinth - Front

Print

Operation Code: 12.07.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove screws (x2) securing licence plate to licence plate bracket and remove licence plate.
  2. Remove M6x16 bolts with washers (x4) securing licence plate bracket to plinth.
  3. Remove licence plate bracket from vehicle.
  4. Remove screws (x5) securing plinth to bumper.
  5. Remove plinth from vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. If you install plinth to a new bumper do the following procedure:
  3. With masking tape to protect front bumper, measure and mark the centre of the front bumper as shown.
  4. Position plinth on bumper so centre of plinth aligns with centre mark of bumper and profile of plinth engages with edge of bumper.
  5. Use the plinth as a template to mark the fixing hole positions on the bumper.
  6. Remove plinth from bumper and pre-drill fixing hole positions.
  7. Continue to install plinth to bumper as reverse of removal procedure.
