Steering Rack Assembly - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 32.03.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front road wheel left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove front road wheel right side.

    3. Procedure is the same for left side component.

  3. Remove front anti roll bar. Refer to procedure.
  4. Loosen nut securing left side track rod end ball joint to hub carrier.

    5. NOTE: Loosen ball joint nut until locking threads are clear of ball joint pin threads.

  5. Use ball joint splitter to separate left side track rod end from hub carrier.
  6. Remove and discard nut securing left side track rod end ball joint to hub carrier. Torque 45 Nm.
  7. Repeat steps 4 to 6 for right side of steering rack.
  8. Remove bolts (x6) securing right side anti-roll bar mounting bracket to front subframe. Torque 24 Nm.
  9. Remove right side anti-roll bar mounting bracket from front subframe.
  10. Remove bolts (x6) securing left side anti-roll bar mounting bracket to front subframe. Torque 24 Nm.
  11. Remove left side anti-roll bar mounting bracket from front subframe.
  12. Remove M8x30 bolt securing lower steering column to steering rack. Torque 24 Nm.
  13. Disconnect lower steering column from steering rack.
  14. Remove banjo bolts (x2) securing hydraulic pipes to steering rack. Torque 32 Nm.

    15. Oil will discharge from hydraulic pipes when removing banjo bolts. Use a container to collect oil.

    Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  15. Remove bolts (x2) securing left side steering rack mounting to front subframe. Torque 45 Nm.

    16. Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  16. Remove bolts (x2) securing right side steering rack mounting to front subframe. Torque 45 Nm.
  17. Remove mounting bracket from steering rack.
  18. Remove steering rack from vehicle.

    19. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  19. Remove mounting bush from steering rack.
  20. Loosen nut securing left side track rod end to steering rack. Torque 45 Nm.

    21. Only loosen nut enough to remove track rod end.

  21. Remove left side track rod end from steering rack.
  22. Record position of nut on steering rack threads.

    23. Recording nut position will assist installation and set up of wheel alignment.

  23. Repeat steps 20 to 22 for right side of steering rack.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Install nuts securing track rod end to steering rack in positions previously recorded.
  3. Renew all discarded fixings that have been removed during removal procedure.
  4. Make sure road wheels and steering wheel are in straight ahead position when connecting steering column to steering rack.
  5. Renew banjo bolt sealing washers.
  6. Fill power steering system. Refer to technical data.
  7. Bleed power steering system. Refer to procedure.
  8. Perform a wheel alignment inspection and adjustment. Refer to procedure.
