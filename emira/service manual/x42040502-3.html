Electronic Throttle Body - V6

Print

Operation Code: 42.04.05-02

    Removal

  1. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  3. Loosen hose clamps (x2) securing intake pipe to Mass Air Flow (MAF) sensor housing and throttle body.
  4. Release hose clamp securing supercharger vacuum hose to intake pipe.
  5. Remove supercharger vacuum hose from intake pipe.
  6. Release and remove intake pipe.

    7. WARNING: Allow engine to cool completely before proceeding to prevent injury.

  7. Release hose clamp securing coolant bypass hose to throttle body.

    8. NOTE: Place a suitable container or rag under the hose to prevent coolant spillage.

  8. Disconnect coolant bypass hose from throttle body and plug hose.
  9. Release hose clamp securing hose to supercharger.
  10. Disconnect hose from supercharger.
  11. Disconnect fuel evaporation hose from supercharger.
  12. Release hose clamp securing coolant bypass hose to throttle body.
  13. Disconnect coolant bypass hose from throttle body and block off with suitable plug.
  14. Release harness connector from throttle body.
  15. Remove bolts (x4) securing throttle body to supercharger. Torque 10 Nm.
  16. Remove throttle body.

    17. NOTE: Discard and replace throttle body gasket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Top up coolant and check fluid level.
  3. Perform a diagnostic read and clear memory using Lotus Insight tool.
