Bracket - Engine Mount - Left Side - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.01.06-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove expansion tank cap.

    3. CAUTION: Allow engine to cool.

  3. Using a suitable tool, drain the fluid from expansion tank.
  4. Disconnect harness connector from coolant expansion tank.
  5. Release clip securing coolant overflow pipe to tank.
  6. Disconnect coolant overflow pipe.
  7. Release hose clamp securing coolant to hose to tank.
  8. Disconnect coolant hose from tank.

    9. NOTE: Plug hose to prevent fluid loss and the ingress of dirt and foreign materials.

    NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  9. Remove M8x16 bolt securing expansion tank to bracket.
  10. Release clip and remove expansion tank.
  11. Remove airbox assembly. Refer to procedure.
  12. Using a suitable tool, support the weight of the engine.
  13. Remove M8x20 bolt securing engine ground to engine mount. Torque ?? Nm.
  14. Remove engine ground from engine mount.
  15. Remove M6x16 bolt securing bracket to engine mount. Torque ?? Nm.
  16. Remove bracket from engine mount.
  17. Remove M12x100 bolt and nut securing engine mount to engine mount bracket. Torque ?? Nm.
  18. Remove M12x50 bolts (x3) securing engine mount to transmission. Torque ?? Nm.
  19. Move engine mount car forward
  20. Remove engine mount.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Check and top up coolant as required.
