Transmission Oil Drain - Refill - Auto - V6

Print

Operation Code: 47.02.12-00

Important Information

WARNING: Transmission oil is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested. Wear protective gloves, protective clothing and safety glasses.

    Drain

  1. Start engine.

    2. NOTE: Make sure that electrical systems such as air conditioning system, audio system and lighting system are off and let engine coolant temperature rise to normal running temperature.

  2. Using selector, slowly select P R N D buttons, allowing transmission enough time to select each gear, then move selector back to ‘P’ - Park.

    3. NOTE: Slowly selecting P R N D will allow enough time for the fluid to circulate through each part of the transaxle.

  3. Select either ‘P’ - Park or ‘N’ – Neutral.
  4. Press and hold ‘P’ – Park button for 15 seconds. Engine coolant temperature display will change to automatic transmission fluid temperature display.

    5. NOTE: Although display will change, the temperature value will not be shown until transmission fluid temperature rises to over 40°C.

  5. Allow temperature within transmission to rise to 40°C.
  6. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.
  7. Position a suitable container under level/overflow plug.
  8. Using a 6 mm hexagon drive socket, remove level/overflow plug.

    9. WARNING: Hot transmission oil may drain out.

  9. Using a 6mm hexagon socket wrench, remove level/overflow tube.
  10. Allow transmission oil to drain.

    11. WARNING: Up to 3 litres of hot transmission oil may drain out.

    NOTE: Dispose of transmission oil appropriately.

  11. Install level/overflow tube. Torque 2 Nm.

    Refill

  1. Remove engine bay trim cover rear. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove bolt securing speedometer blanking plug to transmission.
  3. Remove speedometer plug and O-ring.
  4. Using a suitable funnel, fill transmission with oil through speedometer drive hole until it begins to flow out of overflow plug hole and wait for it to slow down to a drip.
  5. Install level/overflow plug. Torque 40 Nm.
  6. Temporarily Install speedometer blanking plug and new O-ring.
  7. Check and top up transmission oil if necessary. Refer to procedure.
