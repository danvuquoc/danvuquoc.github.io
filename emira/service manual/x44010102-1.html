Fuel Tank

Print

Operation Code: 44.01.01-02

    Removal

  1. Depressurise fuel system. Refer to procedure.
  2. Open fuel filler cap.

    3. WARNING: Before removing the fuel tank from the vehicle it must be drained.

  3. Using a siphon pump, drain fuel into a suitable sealed container.

    4. WARNING: Fuel vapour is highly flammable and in confined spaces is also explosive and toxic. Always have a fire extinguisher containing FOAM, CO2, GAS or POWDER close at hand when handling or draining fuel.

  4. Remove M5x12 bolts (x2) securing luggage hooks to vehicle.
  5. Remove luggage hooks (x2).
  6. Remove fuel tank bay trim.
  7. Remove M6 nuts and washers (x4) securing carpet to vehicle. Torque 7 Nm.
  8. Pull away carpet (x2) to access fuel pump cover bolts.
  9. Remove M6x20 bolts (x4) securing fuel pump cover to vehicle. Torque 7 Nm.
  10. Remove fuel pump cover.
  11. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from fuel pump.
  12. Remove grommet.
  13. Disconnect hose fitting from fuel tank.

    14. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  14. Disconnect fuel outlet hose fitting.

    15. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  15. Disengage clip securing shift cable to selector assembly.
  16. Remove shift cable from selector assembly.
  17. Remove M5 nut and washer securing cable to selector assembly. Torque 5 Nm.

    18. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  18. Remove shift cable from selector assembly.
  19. Remove M8x30 bolts (x2) securing shift cable to bracket. Torque 16 Nm.
  20. Remove bracket from shift cable.
  21. Release shift cable from bracket.
  22. Release shift cable from clip.
  23. Remove rear bulkhead access panel. Refer to procedure.
  24. Remove M6 nut securing ground cable to vehicle. Torque 10 Nm.
  25. Remove ground cable.
  26. Remove rear wheel arch liner right side.

    27. NOTE: Procedure is the same for left side component.

  27. Loosen hose clamp securing fuel filler pipe to fuel tank hose.
  28. Remove fuel filler pipe from fuel tank hose.
  29. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.
  30. Remove fuel tank shear panel. Refer to procedure.
  31. Remove M6x25 bolts (x2) securing shift cable brackets (x2) to vehicle. Torque 10 Nm.
  32. Pull shift cables (x2) out of engine bay.
  33. Remove shift cables from adhesive strips on fuel tank.
  34. Using assistance support weight of fuel tank.
  35. Remove M8x25 bolts (x8) securing fuel tank mounting brackets (x2) to vehicle. Torque 24 Nm.

    36. NOTE: Move shift cables aside so they do not interfere with the fuel tank.

  36. Remove fuel tank mounting brackets (x2).
  37. Using assistance remove fuel tank.

    38. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  38. Remove M6 nyloc nuts and washers (x6) securing fuel pump locking ring to fuel tank.

    39. CAUTION: Apply a suitable lithium based lubricant such as Castrol Moly Grease to threads of the fuel pump retaining studs to prevent ‘Galling’.

    NOTE: Remove nuts in a diagonal sequence.

  39. Remove fuel pump locking ring.
  40. Remove fuel pump and sender unit from fuel tank.

    41. NOTE: Tilt the fuel pump during removal to prevent the float from fouling on the fuel tank.

  41. Remove and discard fuel pump seal.

    Installation

  1. Installation is reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew fuel pump seal.
  3. Inspect condition of foam pads before installation. Replace if necessary.
  4. Install original M6 nyloc nuts and washers (x6) securing fuel pump locking ring to fuel tank. Torque 2 Nm.

    5. CAUTION: Apply a suitable lithium based lubricant such as Castrol Moly Grease to threads of the fuel pump retaining studs to prevent ‘Galling’.

    CAUTION: Ensure clamp ring is flush against top of fuel pump flange.

    NOTE: Install nuts in a diagonal sequence.

  5. Whilst using finger pressure to hold the fuel pump in position, remove and discard the original M6 nyloc nuts and washers (x6).
  6. Remove fuel pump locking ring and inspect fuel pump seal fitment.

    7. NOTE: Continue using finger pressure to hold fuel pump in position.

  7. Install fuel pump locking ring.

    8. NOTE: Continue using finger pressure to hold fuel pump in position.

  8. Install new nyloc nuts and washers (x6). Torque 2 Nm.

    9. NOTE: Tighten nuts in a diagonal sequence.

    NOTE: Continue using finger pressure to hold fuel pump in position.

  9. Apply a final tightening to nyloc nuts (x6). Torque 3 Nm.

    10. CAUTION: Ensure clamp ring is flush against top of fuel pump flange.

    NOTE: Tighten nuts in a diagonal sequence.
