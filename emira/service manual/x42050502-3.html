Fuel Rail - V6

Print

Operation Code: 42.05.05-02

    Removal

  1. Depressurise fuel system. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove supercharger. Refer to procedure.
  3. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove M8x55 bolts (x2) and remove lower supercharger bracket. Torque 24 Nm.
  5. Release fir tree clip securing harness to engine.
  6. Remove fuel pipe clamp.
  7. Disconnect fuel pipe quick connector.

    8. CAUTION: Take care when removing fuel hose as there may still be residual fuel pressure.

    NOTE: Wrap fuel pipe outlet with suitable cloth to absorb any spilled fuel.

    NOTE: Protect fuel rail end connection by wrapping in suitable plastic.

  8. Remove and discard cable ties (x6) securing engine harness to fuel rail.
  9. Disconnect harness connectors (x3) from right side fuel injectors.
  10. Move engine harness aside.
  11. Remove bolts (x5) securing fuel rail to intake manifold. Torque 21 Nm.
  12. Remove fuel rail and fuel injectors from intake manifold.

    13. CAUTION: Take care not to drop fuel injectors when removing fuel rail.

  13. Remove and discard insulators (x6) from intake manifold.

    14. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  14. Remove fuel injectors (x6) from fuel rail.

    15. NOTE: Cover injector ports to prevent ingress.

    NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

    NOTE: Remove the O-rings (x6) from the injectors.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Install new insulators (x6) to intake manifold.
  3. Renew cable ties.
  4. Apply light coat of spindle oil or gasoline to O ring going to fuel rail.

    5. CAUTION: Be careful not to twist the O-rings.

  5. Push and turn fuel injectors (x6) to install into fuel delivery pipes.

    6. CAUTION: Check injectors turn smoothly. If not reinstall with new O-rings.

    NOTE: Position fuel injector connectors outward.
