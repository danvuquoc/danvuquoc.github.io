Ignition Coil - Single - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.17.15-02

    Removal

  1. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove bolts (x2) securing ignition coil to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  4. Disconnect harness connector from ignition coil.
  5. Remove ignition coil.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Apply spark plug connector grease to spark plug connector (3) of ignition coil (2).
