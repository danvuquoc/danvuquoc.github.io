Rear Subframe Assembly - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 30.01.02-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove boot box. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove fuel filler bowl assembly. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove rear hub carrier left side. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove rear upper wishbone assembly left side. Refer to procedure.
  6. Remove rear hub carrier right side.

    7. NOTE: Procedure is the same for left side component.

  7. Remove rear upper wishbone assembly right side.

    8. NOTE: Procedure is the same for left side component.

  8. Remove rear anti roll bar. Refer to procedure.
  9. Release clips (x5) securing body harness to rear subframe.
  10. Move body harness aside to clear rear subframe.
  11. Release clips (x3) securing engine ground harness to rear subframe.
  12. Remove M8x25 bolt securing engine ground harness to rear subframe. Torque 24 Nm.
  13. Remove engine ground harness.
  14. Remove M6x25 bolts (x2) securing battery positive cable to rear subframe. Torque 10 Nm.
  15. Release clips (x6) securing engine bay harness to rear subframe.
  16. Move engine bay harness aside to clear rear subframe.
  17. Install suitable lifting table under rear subframe.
  18. Lower vehicle until rear subframe is supported on lifting table.
  19. Remove M8x25 bolts (x2) securing back stay frame - left side to rear subframe. Torque 24 Nm.
  20. Remove M10x30 bolt securing back stay frame - right side to rear subframe. Torque 45 Nm.
  21. Remove M10x30 bolts (x3) securing rear subframe to underbody assembly. Torque 45 Nm.
  22. Remove M10x30 bolts (x2) and nuts securing rear subframe to underbody assembly. Torque 45 Nm.

    23. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  23. Remove M10x30 bolts (x3) securing rear subframe to underbody assembly. Torque 45 Nm.
  24. Remove M10x30 bolts (x2) and nuts securing rear subframe to underbody assembly. Torque 45 Nm.

    25. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  25. Remove rear subframe assembly.
  26. Remove any shim plates from bottom of rear subframe side rails.

    27. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of shim plates.

  27. Remove rear subframe adapter shim plates (x2).

    28. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  28. Remove M8x30 bolts (x3) securing engine mounting bracket to rear subframe. Torque 24 Nm.
  29. Remove M8x30 bolts (x4) securing engine mounting bracket to rear subframe. Torque 24 Nm.
  30. Remove engine mounting bracket.
  31. Remove M6x25 bolts (x17) and washers securing exhaust heatshield to rear subframe. Torque 8 Nm.
  32. Remove exhaust heatshield.
  33. Remove M10x30 bolts (x3) securing left side reinforcement bracket to rear subframe. Torque 45 Nm.
  34. Remove left side reinforcement bracket.
  35. Remove M10x30 bolts (x3) securing right side reinforcement bracket to rear subframe. Torque 45 Nm.
  36. Remove right side reinforcement bracket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Trial fit rear subframe assembly using no shim plates.
  3. Temporarily fit M10x30 bolts and nuts (x4) during trial installation.
  4. Measure gap between bottom of chassis side rails and subframe horizontal surface.
  5. Apply duralac anti-corrosion compound to subframe and shim mating surfaces.
  6. Install shim plates as required.
  7. Perform a rear suspension geometry inspection and adjustment. Refer to procedure.
