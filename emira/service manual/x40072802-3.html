Gasket Cam Cover - Right Side - Bank 1 - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.07.28-02

    Removal

  1. Remove cam cover right side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove and discard cam cover gasket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Clean contact surface.
  3. Apply seal packing as shown in illustration.

    4. NOTE: The gasket and cam cover must be installed within 3 minutes of applying the seal packing.

    NOTE: Do not start the engine within 2 hours of applying sealant.
