Starter Motor - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.19.01-02

    Removal

  1. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove airbox assembly. Refer to procedure.
  3. Disconnect electrical connector and remove cover to reveal ground nut.

    4. NOTE: Components not shown for clarity.

  4. Remove nut securing live battery cable to starter motor.
  5. Remove M8x16 bolts (x2) securing clamp to starter motor. Torque 20 Nm.
  6. Remove clamp.
  7. Remove M12x90 bolt and M10x35 bolt securing starter motor to engine.
    Torque 65 Nm.
    Torque 45 Nm.
  8. Remove starter motor.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
