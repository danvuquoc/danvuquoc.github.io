Oil Filter Housing Cooler - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.15.27-02

Important Information

WARNING: Engine oil is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested. Wear protective gloves, protective clothing and safety glasses.

    Removal

  1. Remove oil filter housing/cooler assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove screws (x6) securing cooler to oil filter housing.
  3. Remove oil filter housing cooler.

    4. NOTE: Remove and discard gasket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of the removal procedure.

    2. NOTE: Renew discarded gasket.
