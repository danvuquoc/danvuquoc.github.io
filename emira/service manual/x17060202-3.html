Knock Sensors - V6

Print

Operation Code: 17.06.02-02

    Removal

  1. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove intake manifold. Refer to procedure.
  3. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) secured to knock sensors and move harness aside.
  4. Remove bolts (x2) securing knock sensors to the engine. Torque 20 Nm.
  5. Remove knock sensors (x2).

    6. NOTE: Knock sensors must be refitted parallel with engine.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
