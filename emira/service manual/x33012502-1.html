Brake Pads - Rear - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 33.01.25-02

    Removal

  1. Remove road wheel - rear - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove duct radiator outlet - left side. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove brake master cylinder reservoir cap.
  4. Reduce the brake fluid level to allow for brake fluid displacement when caliper pistons are pushed back.
  5. Remove brake pad retaining pins (x2) in direction shown.

    6. NOTE: Clean off any build up of dirt or surface oxidisation from brake pad retaining pins before removing.

  6. Remove brake pad anti-rattle spring.
  7. Ease caliper pistons back.
  8. Remove brake pads from caliper.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except the following:
  2. Clean calipers, retaining pins and anti-rattle springs.

    3. NOTE: Make sure anti-rattle spring is installed with arrow pointing downwards.

  3. Operate brake pedal at least 5 times to seat pads against brake disc.

    4. WARNING: Always check brake pads are seated correctly before driving the vehicle.

  4. Top-up brake fluid level as required.
