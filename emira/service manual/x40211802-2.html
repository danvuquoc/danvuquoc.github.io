Coolant Feed Line - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.21.18-02

    Removal

  1. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  3. Release clip securing engine harness to coolant feed line bracket.
  4. Release clamp securing coolant hose to coolant feed line.

    5. NOTE: Discard clamp.

  5. Disconnect coolant hose from coolant feed line.
  6. Remove bolt securing coolant feed line bracket to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  7. Remove bolt securing coolant feed line to turbocharger. Torque 8 Nm.
  8. Remove bolt securing coolant feed line to engine. Torque 21 Nm.
  9. Remove coolant feed line.

    10. NOTE: Discard O-rings.

    NOTE: Plug exposed connections to prevent ingress of dirt.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded O-rings.
  3. Renew discarded clamp.
