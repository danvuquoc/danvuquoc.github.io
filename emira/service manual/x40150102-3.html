Oil Pump Assembly - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.15.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove timing chain cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove plug from oil pump assembly. Torque 49 Nm.
  3. Remove relief valve and spring.
  4. Remove bolts (x8) securing oil pump assembly to timing chain cover. Torque 9Nm

    5. NOTE: Bolt A is 22mm.

    NOTE: Bolt B is 40mm.

  5. Remove oil pump drive rotor and driven rotor.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Coat drive and driven rotors with engine oil.
  3. Install drive and driven rotors into timing chain cover with marks facing outward (oil pump cover side).
  4. Check that rotors revolve smoothly.
  5. Coat oil pump relief valve with engine oil.
