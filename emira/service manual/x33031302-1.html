Brake Servo

Print

Operation Code: 33.03.13-02

    Removal

  1. Remove brake master cylinder. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disengage hose clamp securing vacuum hose to brake servo.
  3. Remove vacuum hose from brake servo.
  4. Remove lower fascia panel. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove bolts (x4) securing brake servo and extension plinth to vehicle.
  6. Disengage brake servo pushrod from brake pedal.
  7. Remove brake servo and extension plinth.

    8. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  8. Remove nuts (x2) securing brake servo extension plinth. Torque 24 Nm.
  9. Remove extension plinth.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
