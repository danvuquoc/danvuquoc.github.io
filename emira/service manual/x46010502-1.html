Radiator Fan

Print

Operation Code: 46.01.05-02

    Removal

  1. Open access hatch. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M5x16 screws (x2) securing radiator hose to fan shroud.
  3. Disconnect harness connector from radiator fan.
  4. Remove bolts (x8) securing radiator fan shroud to radiator.

    5. NOTE: Radiator shown removed from vehicle for clarity.

  5. Remove radiator fan shroud.
  6. Disengage fir tree clip securing harness connector to fan shroud.
  7. Disengage clip securing harness to radiator fan shroud.
  8. Remove harness from fan shroud.
  9. Remove bolts (x3) securing radiator fan to fan shroud.
  10. Remove radiator fan from fan shroud.
  11. Remove bolts (x3) securing radiator fan to radiator fan motor.
  12. Remove radiator fan from radiator fan motor.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
