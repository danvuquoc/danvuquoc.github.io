Ignition Coils - Right Side - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.17.10-02

    Removal

  1. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove bolt securing ground harness to engine.
  3. Disconnect harness connector from camshaft position sensor.
  4. Disconnect harness connectors (x3) from ignition coils and move harness aside.
  5. Remove bolts (x3) securing ignition bolts to engine. Torque 9 Nm.
  6. Remove ignition coils (x3).

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
