Catalytic Converter - V6

Print

Operation Code: 45.01.11-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear diffuser. Refer to procedure.

    2. WARNING: Allow the engine to cool completely before proceeding.

  2. Loosen clamp securing silencer assembly to catalyst. Torque 60 Nm.
  3. Move clamp on to silencer assembly.
  4. Remove nuts (x3) securing downpipe to catalytic converter. Torque 55 Nm.
  5. Remove catalytic converter.
  6. Remove and discard exhaust O-ring gasket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew exhaust O-ring gasket.
