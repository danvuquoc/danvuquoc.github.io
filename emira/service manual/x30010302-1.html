Shear Panel - Fuel Tank Closing Panel

Print

Operation Code: 30.01.03-02

    Removal

  1. Raise and support vehicle on 2 post lift. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M8x20 bolts (x24) securing fuel tank shear panel to vehicle and with assistance remove fuel tank shear panel. Torque 24 Nm.

    3. NOTE: With assistance, support the weight of the shear panel during removal.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
