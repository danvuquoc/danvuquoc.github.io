Air Duct - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 12.03.37-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear wheelarch liner left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M4x8 self tapping screws (x2) securing air duct to rear fender.
  3. Disengage retaining clips (x4) securing air duct to rear fender.
  4. Remove air duct from rear fender.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
