Air Filter Element - V6

Print

Operation Code: 42.01.04-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect Mass Air Flow (MAF) sensor connector and move harness aside.
  3. Loosen hose clamps (x2) securing intake tube to MAF housing and throttle body.
  4. Release hose clamp securing hose to intake tube.
  5. Remove vacuum hose from intake tube.
  6. Remove the intake tube from MAF housing and throttle body.
  7. Release clips (x2) engine harness from airbox assembly and move aside.
  8. Remove 8x19 screws (x3) securing airbox assembly together and remove airbox outer shell.
  9. Disengage and remove airbox outer shell.
  10. Remove air filter element.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
