Window Guide Rail - Front - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 11.02.03-02

    Removal

  1. Remove door glass left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove door mirror left side. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove M6x20 bolt securing lower window guide rail to door. Torque 7 Nm.
  4. Remove M6x12 bolts (x2) securing upper window guide rail to door inner mounting bracket. Torque 10 Nm.
  5. Remove window guide rail.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
