Exhaust Manifold - Left Side - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.21.15-02

    Removal

  1. Remove downpipe. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove oil dipstick tube. Refer to procedure.
  3. Disengage clip securing oxygen sensor connector to heat shield.
  4. Disconnect air fuel ratio sensor.
  5. Remove M6 nut securing heat shield to cylinder head. Torque 10 Nm.
  6. Remove M6x16 bolt securing heat shield to cylinder head. Torque 10 Nm.
  7. Remove M6x14 bolt securing heat shield to heat shield. Torque 10 Nm.
  8. Remove M8x25 bolt and nut securing heat shield to cylinder head. Torque 24 Nm.
  9. Remove M6x14 bolt from heat shield.
  10. Remove heat shield.
  11. Remove bolt securing exhaust manifold to stay. Torque 44 Nm.
  12. Remove oxygen sensor from exhaust manifold.
  13. Remove bolts (x3) securing heat shield to exhaust manifold. Torque 12 Nm.
  14. Remove heat shield.
  15. Remove nuts (x6) securing exhaust manifold to cylinder head. Torque 37 Nm
  16. Remove exhaust manifold.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
