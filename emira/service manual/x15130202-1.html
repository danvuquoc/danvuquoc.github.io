Door Trim Assembly - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 15.13.02-02

    Removal

  1. Open door - left side.
  2. Remove M6x30 bolts (x2) securing door trim to door panel. Torque 5 Nm.
  3. Carefully pull door trim upwards then away from door to access connections.

    4. NOTE: Only pull door trim away from door enough to gain access to door trim connections.

  4. Refer to illustration for door trim mounting locations.

    5. NOTE: Door trim insulation has been made transparent for image clarity.

  5. Disconnect harness connector connecting door trim harness to door wiring harness.
  6. Disengage door release outer cable from bracket.
  7. Disengage door release cable from door release handle.
  8. Remove door trim from vehicle.

    9. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  9. Disconnect harness connector and remove harness clips (x3) from door pocket moulding.
  10. Remove 4x16 screws (x16) securing door pocket moulding to door trim and remove door pocket moulding.
  11. Disconnect harness connector and remove harness clips (x6) from door trim.
  12. Remove 4x16 screws (x16) securing interior door pull trim to door trim.
  13. Pull electrical harness through door trim as you remove the interior door pull trim.
  14. Remove interior door pull trim from door trim.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following.
  2. Renew all discarded cable ties.
  3. Do a function check of the left side door electrical switches and lamps.
