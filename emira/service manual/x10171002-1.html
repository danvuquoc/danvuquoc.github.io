Door Emergency Lock - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 10.17.10-02

    Remove

  1. Remove door release handle - exterior - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M5x10 torx bolt securing emergency lock barrel to exterior door handle. Torque 6 Nm.
  3. Remove 5x16 self tapping screws (x2) securing emergency lock barrel to exterior door handle.
  4. Remove emergency lock barrel from exterior door handle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
