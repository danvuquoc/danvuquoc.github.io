Knock Sensors - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 17.06.02-02

    Removal

  1. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove catalytic converter. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove bootbox access panel. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove bolts (x4) securing heatshield to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  5. Remove heatshield.
  6. Remove bolts (x4) securing heatshield to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  7. Remove bolts (x3) securing heatshield to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  8. Remove heatshield.
  9. Remove bolt (4) securing heatshield (5) to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  10. Release and remove heatshields (5 and 6).
  11. Mark position of knock sensors (A16/1 and A16/2) on crankcase.
  12. Disconnect harness connectors (7) (x2) from knock sensors.
  13. Remove bolts (8) (x2) securing knock sensors to crankcase. Torque 20 Nm.
  14. Remove knock sensors.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. NOTE: If renewing knock sensors, transfer marking of installation position from old knock sensors.

  2. After installation perform a diagnostic read and clear error memory using Lotus Insight tool.
