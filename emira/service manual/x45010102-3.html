Downpipe - V6

Print

Operation Code: 45.01.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.

    2. WARNING: Allow the exhaust to cool down before proceeding to prevent injury.

  2. Remove fir tree clip securing O2 harness connector to bracket.
  3. Disconnect left side post catalyst O2 sensor connector.
  4. Remove fir tree clip securing O2 harness connector to bracket.
  5. Disconnect right side post catalyst O2 sensor connector.
  6. Remove M10 nuts (x2) securing downpipe to hanger mount. Torque 55 Nm.
  7. Remove nuts (x3) securing downpipe to catalytic converter. Torque 55 Nm.
  8. Remove bolts (x2) securing downpipe to bank A exhaust manifold. Torque 55 Nm.
  9. Remove nuts (x2) securing downpipe to bank B exhaust manifold. Torque 55 Nm.
  10. Remove downpipe and exhaust O ring gaskets.

    11. NOTE: Discard all O ring gaskets.

    Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  11. Remove O2 sensors (x2) from downpipe. Torque 45 Nm.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew exhaust gaskets.
