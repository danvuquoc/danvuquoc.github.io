Door Glass - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 11.01.05-02

    Removal

  1. Remove door water deflector panel - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Position door glass to gain access to door glass fixings.
  3. Remove screws (x2) securing door glass to regulator.
  4. Carefully release door glass from retaining clamps (x2) on regulator.
  5. Carefully tilt door glass and remove from door.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except the following:
  2. Check door glass alignment and reset window one touch facility. Refer to procedure.
