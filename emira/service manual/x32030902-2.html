Hose Assembly - Return - Rear - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 32.03.09-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front undertray. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove banjo bolt securing return hose to steering rack. Torque 32 Nm.

    3. Oil will discharge from hose assembly when removing banjo bolt. Use a container to collect oil.

    Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  3. Remove radiator duct outlet left side. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove radiator duct outlet right side.

    5. NOTE: Procedure is the same as left side component.

  5. Remove scrivets (x3) securing HVAC air inlet to vehicle.
  6. Remove M5x12 screws and washers (x2) securing HVAC air inlet to filter lid.
  7. Remove HVAC air inlet.
  8. Remove scrivets (x6) securing centre radiator duct to front subframe.
  9. Remove centre radiator duct.
  10. Release clips (x2) securing return hose to reservoir/pump hose and harness.
  11. Remove M6x16 bolt securing return hose to crossmember. Torque 10 Nm.
  12. Disconnect return hose from reservoir.
  13. Remove return hose.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew banjo bolt sealing washers.
  3. Fill power steering system. Refer to technical data.
  4. Bleed power steering system. Refer to procedure.
