Pulley / Arm Assembly - Belt Tensioner - V6

Print

Operation Code: 41.03.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove auxiliary drive belt. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove alternator. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove AC compressor. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove bolts (x5) securing AC compressor bracket to engine.
  5. Remove AC compressor bracket.
  6. Remove bolt securing belt tensioner pulley to belt tensioner. Torque 43 Nm.

    7. NOTE: The bolt for the belt tensioner pulley is counter threaded.

  7. Remove belt tensioner pulley.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
