Access Hatch - Front

Print

Operation Code: 10.09.01-02

    Removal

  1. Open access hatch - front. Refer to procedure.
  2. With assistance support weight of access panel.
  3. Disengage access hatch stay.
  4. Remove M8x25 bolts and washers (x4) securing access hatch to access hatch hinges. Torque 24 Nm.

    5. NOTE: To aid installation mark position of access hatch to access hatch hinges.

    NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  5. With assistance remove access panel from vehicle.

    6. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  6. Remove M6x20 bolts and washers (x3) securing brackets to access hatch. Torque 10 Nm.
  7. Remove brackets (x3).

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Check gap and flush tolerances - see Alignment Specifications for Body Panels and Closures section.

    3. NOTE: Make sure access hatch is aligned correctly during installation. Use position marked during removal procedure.

    NOTE: Only fully tighten bolts when you have confirmed the panel alignment is correct.
