Driveshaft Bearing Bracket - V6

Print

Operation Code: 47.15.11-02

    Removal

  1. Remove right side driveshaft. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M10x80, M10x50 and M10x32 bolts securing driveshaft bearing bracket to engine. Manual transmission: Torque 52 Nm. Automatic transmission: Torque 65 Nm.
  3. Remove driveshaft bearing bracket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
