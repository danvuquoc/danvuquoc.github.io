Spring / Damper Assembly - Left Side - Rear

Print

Operation Code: 31.03.40-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear wheel arch liner. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear anti-roll bar drop link. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove M14x110 bolt and M14 nut securing lower damper mounting to lower wishbone. Torque 190 Nm.
  4. Remove M8x20 bolts (x2) and M8 nuts (x2) securing upper section of top mount bracket to subframe. Torque 24 Nm.
  5. Remove M10x20 bolt securing side section of top mount bracket to chassis tower. Torque 45 Nm.
  6. Remove Damper assembly.

    7. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  7. Remove rear road spring left side. Refer to procedure.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew lock nut fasteners that have been removed during removal procedure.
