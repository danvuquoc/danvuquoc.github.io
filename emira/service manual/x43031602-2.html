Bleed Line - Valve Purge Line - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 43.03.16-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove electronic throttle actuator. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove AC compressor. Refer to procedure.
  4. Release connector and disconnect purge line from bleed line.

    5. NOTE: Plug purge valve to prevent ingress of dirt.

  5. Release connector and disconnect bleed line from charge air distributor.

    6. NOTE: Plug charge air distributor to prevent ingress of dirt.

  6. Remove bolt securing bleed line to engine.
  7. Remove bolt securing bleed line to engine.
  8. Remove bleed line.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
