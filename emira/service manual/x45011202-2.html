Clamp - Catalyst To Exhaust Pipe - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 45.01.12-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear diffuser. Refer to procedure.

    2. WARNING: Allow the engine to cool completely before proceeding.

  2. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.
  3. Loosen clamp securing exhaust pipe to silencer. Torque 60 Nm.
  4. Loosen clamp securing exhaust pipe to catalytic converter. Torque 25 Nm.

    5. NOTE: Discard gasket.

  5. Remove exhaust pipe.
  6. Remove clamp from exhaust pipe.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded gasket.
