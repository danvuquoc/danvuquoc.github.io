Upper Wishbone - Rear- Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 31.03.16-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear wheel arch liner. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M14x100 bolt and discard torque nut securing upper wishbone to hub. Torque 190 Nm.
  3. Remove M12x85 bolt and discard nut torque securing rear of upper wishbone to vehicle. Torque 105 Nm.

    4. NOTE: Bolts securing upper wishbone to subframe are both oriented rearwards on 4 cylinder models, and point away from each other on V6 models.

  4. Remove M12x85 bolt and discard torque nut securing front of upper wishbone to vehicle. Torque 105 Nm.

    5. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  5. Remove upper wishbone.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew all torque nut fixings that have been removed during removal procedure.
  3. Only torque tighten wishbone bolts once vehicle is in the normal ride height position.
  4. Perform a rear suspension geometry inspection and adjustment. Refer to procedure.
