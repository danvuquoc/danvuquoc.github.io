Headlining

Print

Operation Code: 12.04.03-02

    Removal

  1. Remove driver seat. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove passenger seat.

    3. NOTE: Procedure is the same for driver side component.

  3. Remove sunvisor left side. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove sunvisor right side.

    5. NOTE: Procedure is the same as left side component.

  5. Remove overhead console. Refer to procedure.
  6. Remove screws (x2) securing overhead console chassis to vehicle.
  7. Remove A pillar trim left side. Refer to procedure.
  8. Remove A pillar trim right side.

    9. NOTE: Procedure is the same as left side component.

  9. Remove rear quarter trim left side. Refer to procedure.
  10. Remove rear quarter trim right side.

    11. NOTE: Procedure is the same as left side component.

  11. Release clips (x6) securing rear view mirror trim to windscreen assembly.
  12. Remove rear view mirror trim.
  13. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from rain sensor and rear view mirror.
  14. Disengage clips (x2) securing middle of headliner to vehicle.
  15. Disengage clips (x3) securing rear of headliner to vehicle.
  16. Disconnect headlining harness connector from body harness.
  17. Release clips (x2) securing headlining harness to vehicle.
  18. Using assistance remove headliner.

    19. NOTE: Fold rear view mirror forwards to aid headliner removal.

    Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  19. Disconnect harness connectors (x4) from microphones (x2).
  20. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from left and right LED lamps (x2).
  21. Remove headlining harness.
  22. Disengage clip securing microphones (x2) to headlining.
  23. Remove microphones (x2).
  24. Disengage clips (x4) securing LED lamps (x2) to headlining.
  25. Remove LED lamps (x2).

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
