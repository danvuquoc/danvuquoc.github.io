Intake Manifold - V6

Print

Operation Code: 42.07.07-02

    Removal

  1. Remove fuel injectors. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M8x55 bolts (x2) securing lower supercharger bracket to engine. Torque 24 Nm.
  3. Remove lower supercharger bracket.
  4. Remove bolts (x3) securing intake manifold to engine. Torque 21 Nm.
  5. Remove nuts (x3) securing intake manifold to engine. Torque 21 Nm.
  6. Remove bolts (x3) securing intake manifold to engine. Torque 21 Nm.
  7. Remove nut securing intake manifold to engine. Torque 21 Nm.
  8. Remove intake manifold.

    9. NOTE: Discard gasket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. NOTE: Clean the mating surfaces of the engine and the intake manifold.

  2. Renew gasket.
