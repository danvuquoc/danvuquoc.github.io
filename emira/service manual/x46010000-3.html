Coolant Replace - V6

Print

Operation Code: 46.01.00-00

    Drain

  1. Set heater to coldest temperature.

    2. NOTE: This must be done in order to prevent airlocks in the HVAC system.

  2. Remove rear engine bay trim cover. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove downpipe. Refer to procedure.
  4. Loosen right side cylinder drain cock plug. Torque 12.7 Nm.
  5. Remove front wheelarch liner right side. Refer to procedure.
  6. Remove front undertray. Refer to procedure.

    7. WARNING: Allow engine to cool completely before proceeding.

  7. Remove radiator reservoir cap.
  8. Release hose clamp securing hose to radiator.
  9. Disconnect hose from radiator and allow coolant to drain completely.

    10. WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain coolant into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

    Refill

  1. Reconnect hose to radiator and secure hose clamp.
  2. Use hose clamp in coolant reservoir overflow hose.
  3. Install vacuum purge and refill adapter to coolant reservoir.

    4. NOTE: Ensure the tap is open on the adapter.

  4. Install vacuum pump and air line to adapter.
  5. Use air line to create vacuum in cooling system.

    6. NOTE: The coolant hoses will collapse during this process. This is normal.

    NOTE: Allow vacuum level to reach 20.25 inHg (50-60 CmHg).

  6. Close tap and remove air line and vacuum pump from adapter.

    7. NOTE: Wait 3 minutes before proceeding to monitor vacuum.

    NOTE:If vacuum drops before adding coolant inspect cooling system for leaks and repeat previous step.

  7. Install coolant hose to adapter.

    8. NOTE: Ensure coolant hose is full of coolant with no air bubbles.

  8. Open tap and allow system to fill with coolant.

    9. NOTE: The cooling system is full when the gauge reads zero.

  9. Remove adapter and refit coolant reservoir cap.
  10. Start and operate engine.

    11. NOTE: Hold engine RPM between 2000 - 2500 RPM.

    NOTE: Check there are no leaks coming from the radiator and bleed points.

    NOTE: Continue to operate the engine until the radiator cooling fans have turned on and then turned off.

  11. Turn off engine and allow to cool completely.
  12. Check coolant level when the car is cold and top up if required.

    13. NOTE: Make sure coolant is filled between the min and max lines.
