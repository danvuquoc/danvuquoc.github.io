Sealing Ring - Dual Clutch - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 47.02.21-02

Important Information

WARNING: Transmission oil is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested. Wear protective gloves, protective clothing and safety glasses.

    Removal

  1. Remove dual clutch. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove and discard sealing rings (6).

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded sealing rings.
  3. Ensure sealing rings are correctly seated.
