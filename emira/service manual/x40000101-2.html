Engine Assembly - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.00.01-01

    Removal

  1. Remove left side seat. Refer to procedure.
  2. Move right side seat forward to aid future access.
  3. Disconnect 12v battery. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove engine bay fan assembly. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove exhaust pipe. Refer to procedure.

    6. NOTE: When placing vehicle on 2 post ramp, make sure there is tool access to the lower control arm fixings.

  6. Remove rear left hub carrier. Refer to procedure.
  7. Remove rear right hub carrier.
  8. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.
  9. Remove rear left wheel arch liner. Refer to procedure.
  10. Remove rear right wheel arch liner.
  11. Release harness from clip securing harness to lower control arm.

    12. NOTE: Repeat step for right side.

  12. Release clips (x3) securing harness to lower subframe.
  13. Move harness aside.
  14. Loosen M12x100 bolt and nut securing lower control arm, until it clears the rear bulkhead. Torque 105 Nm.

    15. NOTE: Repeat step for right side.

  15. Remove M12x50 bolts (x2) securing tie bar mount to carrier bracket. Torque 106 Nm.
  16. Remove M6x20 bolts (x11) securing lower subframe to upper crossmember panel. Torque 8 Nm.

    17. NOTE: Not all fixings are shown in image.

  17. Remove M10x30 bolts (x2) securing lower subframe to bulkhead. Torque 45 Nm.
  18. Remove M10x30 bolts (x4) securing lower subframe to upper subframe. Torque 45 Nm.

    19. NOTE: Repeat step for right side.

  19. Loosen, but do not fully remove M10x30 bolts (x2) securing lower subframe to upper subframe. Torque 45 Nm.

    20. NOTE: Repeat step for right side.

  20. Using a suitable tool support lower subframe.
  21. Remove M10x30 bolts (x4) securing lower subframe to upper subframe.
  22. Remove lower subframe.
  23. Remove airbox assembly. Refer to procedure.
  24. Depressurise and evacuate AC system. Refer to procedure.
  25. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  26. Disconnect harness connector.
  27. Disconnect harness connector and move harness aside.
  28. Remove cover from alternator harness.
  29. Remove M8 nut securing alternator harness from alternator. Torque 16 Nm.
  30. Remove alternator harness and move aside.
  31. Remove bolts (x2) securing AC hoses (x2) to compressor.
  32. Remove AC hoses (x2) and move aside.

    33. NOTE: Discard all seals.

    NOTE: Plug hoses and compressor to prevent the ingress of dirt and foreign material.

  33. Disconnect coolant hoses (x2).
  34. Release clip securing hose to water pipe.
  35. Remove coolant hose from water pipe and move aside.
  36. Disconnect coolant hose from thermostat housing and move aside.
  37. Release clip securing hose to water pipe.
  38. Remove coolant hose from water pipe and move aside.
  39. Release clip securing hose to water pipe.
  40. Remove hose from water pipe and move aside.
  41. Disconnect hose from intercooler.

    42. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  42. Remove M6x12 bolt securing bracket to charge intercooler. Torque 8 Nm.
  43. Disconnect harness connector from expansion tank.
  44. Release bleeder hose from expansion tank.
  45. Release clip securing hose to expansion tank.
  46. Remove hose from expansion tank.

    47. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  47. Remove M6x16 bolt securing expansion tank to bracket.
  48. Lift tank in an upwards direction to release rear clip.
  49. Remove expansion tank.
  50. Disconnect hoses (x3) from 3 way diverter valve.
  51. Disconnect hoses (x2) and move aside.
  52. Release clip securing hose to expansion tank.
  53. Remove hose from expansion tank.
  54. Remove M6x16 bolt securing bracket to engine mount. Torque 10 Nm.
  55. Remove bracket from engine mount.
  56. Disconnect harness connector from transmission.
  57. Disconnect harness connector from starter motor.
  58. Remove cap covering starter motor connection.
  59. Remove M8 nut securing harness to starter motor. Torque 16 Nm.
  60. Remove harness from starter motor.
  61. Disconnect harness connector from engine bay temperature sensor.
  62. Remove coolant hoses (x3).
  63. Disconnect fuel line.

    64. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  64. Remove washer bottle. Refer to procedure.
  65. Disconnect harness connector from turbocharger diverter valve.
  66. Release clip securing hose to diverter valve.
  67. Remove hose from diverter valve.
  68. Remove screws (x3) securing diverter valve to throttle body.
  69. Remove diverter valve.
  70. Install support fixture to lifting table and position under engine assembly.
  71. Insert mounts (6 and 7) with adaptors (8, 9, 10 and 12) to support fixture (5) as shown.
  72. Raise lifting table and align mounts so they are fully in contact with mounting points (1a, 2a, 3b, 4a and 5a) on engine assembly.
  73. Tighten screws (15) securing mounts to support fixture.
  74. Using a suitable tensioning strap, secure engine assembly to support fixture.
  75. Remove M12x50 bolts (x3) securing left side transmission mount to transmission. Torque 106 Nm.
  76. Release clips (x2) securing O2 sensor harness to engine mount.
  77. Remove M10x40 bolts (x4) securing right side engine mount to engine. Torque 45 Nm.
  78. Carefully lower engine assembly until it is clear of the vehicle.

    79. CAUTION: Be careful not to damage any components.

    CAUTION: Always check engine assembly to vehicle clearances.

  79. Remove engine assembly.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Refill coolant.
  3. Recharge AC system.
  4. Renew all discarded seals.
  5. Renew all discarded cable ties.
