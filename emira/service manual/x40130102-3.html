Water Pump Assembly - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.13.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove idler bracket. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove crankshaft pulley. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove idler pulley. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove thermostat housing. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove water pump pulley. Refer to procedure.
  6. Remove bolts (x16) securing water pump assembly to engine block. Bolt A.
    Torque 21 Nm. Bolt B and C.
    Torque 9.1 Nm.
  7. Remove water pump assembly.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. NOTE: Make sure that there is no oil on threads of bolts A.

    NOTE: Renew bolts C (x2) or reuse them after applying adhesive 1344 or equivalent.

  2. Renew water pump assembly gasket. Refer to procedure.
