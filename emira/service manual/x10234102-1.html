Bumper Lower - Rear

Print

Operation Code: 10.23.41-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear bumper. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect rear bumper harness connectors (x8).
  3. Disengage harness clips (x5) from rear bumper lower and move harness aside.
  4. Remove M6x8 torx bolts (x2) securing licence plate to licence plate bracket and remove licence plate.
  5. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing licence plate bracket to bumper. Torque 10 Nm.
  6. Remove licence plate bracket from rear bumper lower.
  7. Remove M6x20 bolts and washers (x4) securing bumper lower to bootbox. Torque 5 Nm.
  8. Release clip (x16) securing rear bumper lower to rear bumper.
  9. Remove rear bumper lower from rear bumper.

    10. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  10. Remove 5x12 screws (x10) securing grille frame left side to rear bumper lower.
  11. Remove grille frame left side from rear bumper lower.
  12. Repeat steps 11 and 12 for right side side of bumper lower.
  13. Remove M5x12 screws (x8) securing centre grille frame to rear bumper lower.
  14. Remove centre grille frame from rear bumper lower.
  15. Remove M4x16 bolts (x2) securing rear fog lamp to rear bumper lower. Torque 2.25 Nm.
  16. Remove rear fog lamp from rear bumper lower.
  17. Release clips (x4) securing licence plate lamp mounting plate to rear bumper lower.
  18. Remove M5x12 screws (x2) securing licence plate lamp mounting plate to rear bumper lower.
  19. Remove licence plate lamp mounting plate from rear bumper lower.
  20. Release clips (x2) securing rear parking sensor to rear bumper.
  21. Remove rear parking sensor from rear bumper.
  22. Repeat steps 21 and 22 for remaining rear parking sensors (x3).

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Check rear bumper gap and flush tolerances.
