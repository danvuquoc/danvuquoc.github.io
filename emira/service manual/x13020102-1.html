Seat Driver

Print

Operation Code: 13.02.01-02

Procedure Introduction

NOTE: If renewing drivers seat due to side airbag deployment, the ECU - airbag control must also be renewed, refer to procedure.

    Removal

  1. Open door.
  2. Carefully pull seat side trim to disengage clips (x3) securing trim to seat.
  3. Slide off cable ties (x4) from trim panel.

    4. NOTE: Do not cut cable ties.

  4. Remove trim.
  5. Remove M10x25 bolt, spacer and washer securing seat belt to seat. Torque 45 Nm.

    6. NOTE: Record fitted position of spacer and washer.

  6. Move seat to fully back position.
  7. Remove M10x30 bolts (x2) securing front of seat rail to vehicle. Torque 47 Nm.

    8. NOTE: Retain spacers (x2) under seat rails.

  8. Move seat to fully forward position.
  9. Move seat backrest to fully forward position.
  10. Remove M10x30 bolts (x2) securing rear of seat rail to vehicle. Torque 47 Nm.

    11. NOTE: Retain spacers (x2) under seat rails.

  11. Fold seat to fully forward position.
  12. Tilt seat backwards to access electrical connectors.
  13. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from drivers seat.
  14. Remove drivers seat.

    15. CAUTION: Take care to not damage components or body work while removing the seat.

    NOTE: Seat should be removed with the rails coming out first.

    Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  15. Remove bolt, spacer and washer securing seat belt buckle to seat.

    16. NOTE: Record fitted position of spacer and washer.

    NOTE: Make sure that the seat belt buckle tang fits into the seat frame slot.

  16. Remove seat belt buckle.
  17. Remove nuts (x4) securing seat rail to seat.
  18. Remove bolts (x2) securing seat rail to seat.
  19. Remove seat rail.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
