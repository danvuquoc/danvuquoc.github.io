Amplifier

Print

Operation Code: 17.27.12-02

    Removal

  1. Remove subwoofer left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connectors (x3) from amplifier.
  3. Remove M6x16 screws (x2) securing bracket to vehicle. Torque 10 Nm.
  4. Remove M6 nut securing amplifier to vehicle.
  5. Remove amplifier and bracket assembly.

    6. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  6. Remove M5x10 security bolts (x4) securing amplifier to bracket. Torque 4 Nm.
  7. Remove amplifier.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
