Diagnostic Module - Tank Leakage (DMTL) - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 44.05.30-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector from Diagnostic Module - Tank Leakage (DMTL).
  3. Release quick connector securing outlet hose to DMTL.

    4. NOTE: Pinch the two buttons on the quick connector and pull to remove.

  4. Disconnect outlet hose from DMTL.

    5. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  5. Release clamp securing evaporative pipe to DMTL.
  6. Disconnect evaporative pipe from DMTL.

    7. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  7. Remove M6x25 bolts (x2) securing DMTL to rear bulkhead. Torque 10 Nm.
  8. Remove M6x25 bolt securing DMTL to rear bulkhead. Torque 10 Nm.

    9. NOTE: Parts removed for clarity.

  9. Remove DMTL.

    10. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  10. Remove 5x10 screws (x3) securing bracket to DMTL. Torque 2 Nm.
  11. Remove bracket.
  12. Remove filter from DMTL.

    13. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal except for the following:

    2. NOTE: Push quick connector until and audible click is heard.

  2. Perform a diagnostic read and clear error memory using Lotus Insight tool.
