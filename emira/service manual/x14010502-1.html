Clutch Pedal

Print

Operation Code: 14.01.05-02

    Removal

  1. Remove throttle pedal assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove clutch potentiometer. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove clip and pin securing clutch master cylinder to clutch pedal.
  4. Release brake servo pushrod from brake pedal.
  5. Remove bolts (x3) securing clutch pedal spring bracket to pedal box.
  6. Unhook brake pedal return spring.
  7. Remove M6 bolts (x3) and M6 nuts (x3) securing pedal assembly to pedal box. Torque 16 Nm.
  8. Remove pedal assembly.
  9. Remove Torx screws (x3) securing pedal shaft to pedal shaft bracket.
  10. Remove clutch pedal from pedal shaft.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Lubricate pivot bush of clutch pedal with Syntheso GLK1 or equivalent.
