Speaker - E-call

Print

Operation Code: 17.27.39-02

    Removal

  1. Remove instrument panel assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector from E-call speaker.
  3. Remove self tapping screws (x2) securing E-call speaker to fascia panel.
  4. Remove E-call speaker.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
