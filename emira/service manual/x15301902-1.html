Carpet - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 15.30.19-02

    Removal

  1. Remove centre console assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove lower fascia panel. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove rear quarter trim. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove M5x12 Torx bolt securing left side luggage rope hook to vehicle.
  5. Remove left side luggage rope hook.
  6. Remove M5x12 Torx bolt securing right side luggage rope hook to vehicle.
  7. Remove right side luggage rope hook.
  8. Remove tank bay trim.
  9. Release fasteners (x2) securing floor mat to carpet.
  10. Remove floor mat.
  11. Release clips (x4) securing footrest to carpet.
  12. Remove footrest.
  13. Remove scrivet securing carpet to A pillar.
  14. Remove M6 nuts and washers (x2) securing carpet to vehicle. Torque 7 Nm.
  15. Remove carpet.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. NOTE: Ensure holes in carpet are centrally located over seat bolt holes and studs at rear.
