Trim Panel - Gear Select Controls

Print

Operation Code: 17.04.04-02

    Removal

  1. Remove gear selector lever. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rubber pad from centre console.
  3. Disengage clips (x11) securing centre console finisher to centre console.
  4. Remove centre console finisher.
  5. Disengage clips (x4) securing trim panel to gear selector.
  6. Remove trim panel.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
