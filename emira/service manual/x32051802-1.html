Steering Wheel

Print

Operation Code: 32.05.18-02

    Removal

  1. Remove airbag module - drivers. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect steering wheel harness connector.
  3. Remove and discard M12x35 bolt securing steering wheel to steering column. Torque 60 Nm.

    4. NOTE: Before removing bolt, ensure the steering wheel is straight and the road wheels are pointing straight ahead. Mark the steering wheel position in relation to the stalk module.

  4. Carefully remove steering wheel so damage does not occur to stalk module connectors.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Install steering wheel to steering column so alignment marks align correctly.

    3. CAUTION: Do not install steering wheel with alignment marks misaligned or damage may occur to stalk module connectors.

  3. Renew steering wheel bolt.
