Fender Rear - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 10.01.29-02

    Removal

  1. Remove tailgate trim left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear bumper. Refer to procedure.
  3. Open door.
  4. Disengage fir tree clips (x4) securing harnesses to rear fender and move harnesses aside.
  5. Remove M4x8 self tapping screws (x2) securing side vent grill to rear fender.
  6. Disengage clips (x4) and remove side vent grille from rear fender.
  7. Remove emblem secured to rear fender. Refer to procedure.
  8. Disengage trim clips (x6) and remove fender panel secured to rear fender.
  9. Disengage trim clips (x6) and remove luggage side cover from vehicle.
  10. Remove M6x20 bolt securing fender to sill. Torque 10 Nm.
  11. Remove M6x20 bolts (x4) securing rear fender to vehicle. Torque 10 Nm.
  12. Remove M6x20 bolt securing fender to vehicle. Torque 10 Nm.
  13. Remove M6x20 bolts (x3) securing fender to vehicle. Torque 10 Nm.
  14. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing fender to vehicle. Torque 10 Nm.
  15. Remove M6x20 bolts (x4) securing fender to vehicle. Torque 10 Nm.

    16. NOTE: To aid installation mark position of fender to vehicle.

  16. Disconnect harness connector from rear lamp assembly.
  17. Using assistance, release and remove rear fender and tail lamp assembly.

    18. CAUTION: Place on secure and suitable surface to prevent damage.

    Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  18. Remove M8x16 bolt and washer securing rear lamp assembly to rear fender. Torque 10 Nm.
  19. Remove M6x12 bolt securing rear lamp assembly to rear fender. Torque 6 Nm.
  20. Remove M5 nut securing rear lamp assembly to rear fender. Torque 4 Nm.
  21. Remove rear lamp assembly.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Clean off excess adhesive from fender emblem area and renew adhesive.
  3. Check fender gap and flush tolerances - see Alignment Specifications for Body Panels and Closures section.
