Water Valve - Heater By-Pass - LHD

Print

Operation Code: 18.05.14-02

    Removal

  1. Remove radiator duct outlet left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove radiator duct outlet right side.

    3. NOTE: Procedure is the same for left side component.

  3. Remove scrivets (x3) securing HVAC air inlet to vehicle.
  4. Remove M5x12 screws and washers (x2) securing HVAC air inlet to filter lid.
  5. Remove HVAC air inlet.
  6. If fitted, remove water shield from HVAC unit. Refer to TSB-131-24007.
  7. Disconnect harness connector from heater bypass valve.
  8. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing heater bypass valve bracket assembly to subframe. Torque 10 Nm.
  9. Remove heater bypass valve and bracket assembly to access hose clamps.
  10. Disengage hose clamps (x2) securing inlet and outlet hoses to heater bypass valve.
  11. Install clamps to inlet and outlet hoses.

    12. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  12. Remove hoses from heater bypass valve.
  13. Remove heater bypass valve.

    14. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  14. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing heater bypass valve to bracket. Torque 10 Nm.
  15. Remove heater bypass valve.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
  2. Check and top up coolant if necessary.
