Switch - Access Hatch

Print

Operation Code: 17.33.06-02

    Removal

  1. Remove radiator outlet duct left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove radiator duct outlet right side.

    3. NOTE: Procedure is the same as for left side component.

  3. Remove scrivets (x3) securing HVAC air inlet to vehicle.
  4. Remove HVAC air inlet.
  5. Release clip securing access hatch switch harness to bracket.
  6. Disconnect harness connector from access hatch switch.
  7. Remove M4x10 bolt securing access hatch switch to bracket. Torque 2 Nm.
  8. Release tab and remove access hatch switch.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
