Electric Coolant Pump - High Temperature - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.13.18-02

Important Information

WARNING: Risk of scalding. Allow coolant to cool completely before proceeding.

WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    Removal

  1. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove catalytic converter. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove M6 bolts (11 and 13) (x11) securing heat shields (12 and 14) to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  4. Remove heat shields.
  5. Remove textile heat shield (15).
  6. Remove bolt (16) securing coolant pipe to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  7. Remove bolts (2) securing coolant pipes (3 and 5) to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  8. Disconnect coolant pipe (3) from engine.

    9. NOTE: Remove and discard O-ring.

    NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  9. Remove bolt (4) securing coolant pipe (5) to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  10. Disconnect coolant pipe (5) from engine.

    11. NOTE: Remove and discard O-ring.

  11. Release clamps (6) (x2) securing coolant hoses (7) (x2) to electric coolant pump (1). Torque 3 Nm.

    12. NOTE: Remove and discard clamps.

  12. Disconnect coolant hoses from electric coolant pump.
  13. Disconnect harness connector (9) from electric coolant pump.
  14. Remove bolts (10) (x3) securing electric coolant pump to engine. Torque 20 Nm.
  15. Remove electric coolant pump.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded O-rings and clamps.
