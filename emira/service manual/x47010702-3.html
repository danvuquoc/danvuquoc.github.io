Clutch Master Cylinder - V6

Print

Operation Code: 47.01.07-02

    Removal

  1. Remove radiator duct outlet duct. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove brake fluid reservoir cap.
  3. Use hose clamp on clutch master cylinder hose to minimise fluid loss.
  4. Disengage hose clamp and remove hose from clutch master cylinder.

    5. NOTE: Components not shown for clarity.

  5. Disengage quick connector and remove clutch pipe from clutch master cylinder.
  6. Remove clip and pin securing clutch master cylinder to clutch pedal.
  7. Remove M8x30 bolts (x2) securing clutch master cylinder to vehicle. Torque 25 Nm.

    8. NOTE: Install spanner to M8 nuts (x2) fitted to bolts from pedal box side.

  8. Remove clutch master cylinder.

    9. NOTE: Components not shown for clarity.

    Note: Spacer and gasket are fitted behind clutch master cylinder which may drop down during removal.

    Installation

  1. Installation is reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Bleed clutch.
  3. Check and top up brake fluid level.
