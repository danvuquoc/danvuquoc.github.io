Engine Assembly - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.00.01-01

    Removal

  1. Raise and support vehicle. Refer to procedure.
  2. Depressurise air conditioning system. Refer to procedure.
  3. Depressurise fuel system. Refer to procedure.
  4. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove electronic control module. Refer to procedure.
  6. Drain oil. Refer to procedure.
  7. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  8. Remove boot box. Refer to procedure.
  9. Remove left side drive shaft. Refer to procedure.
  10. Remove right side drive shaft. Refer to procedure.
  11. Remove airbox assembly. Refer to procedure.
  12. Disconnect harness connector from coolant expansion tank.
  13. Disengage clip securing coolant overflow pipe to coolant expansion tank.
  14. Disconnect coolant overflow pipe.
  15. Disengage hose clamp securing coolant hose to coolant expansion tank.
  16. Disconnect coolant hose from coolant expansion tank.
  17. Remove M8x16 bolt securing coolant expansion tank to vehicle.
  18. Release clip and remove coolant expansion tank.
  19. Clamp charge cooler inlet and return hoses.
  20. Disengage clamps (x2) and disconnect charge cooler hoses (x2) from supercharger.

    21. NOTE: Place an absorbent material under the charge cooler hoses to prevent coolant spilling on electronic components.

  21. Disengage hose clamps (x2) securing power steering fluid hose to power steering pump.
  22. Remove power steering fluid hose.

    23. NOTE: Use a suitable container to drain power steering fluid.

  23. Remove banjo bolt and discard washers securing power steering fluid hose to power steering pump. Torque 30 Nm.
  24. Disconnect power steering fluid hose from power steering pump.
  25. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from AC compressor.
  26. Disengage clips (x2) and move harness aside.
  27. Remove bolts securing AC pipes to AC compressor. Torque 8.5 Nm.
  28. Disconnect AC pipes (x2) from AC compressor.

    29. NOTE: Plug all ports.

  29. Remove M5 nut and washer securing crossgate cable to selector assembly.

    30. NOTE: Record position and quantity of washers.

  30. Remove crossgate cable from selector assembly.
  31. Remove M8x30 bolts (x2) securing crossgate cable to bracket. Torque 16 Nm.
  32. Remove bracket from crossgate cable.
  33. Disengage clip securing gearshift cable to selector assembly.
  34. Remove gearshift cable from selector assembly.
  35. Remove M6 bolt securing gearshift cable clamp to bracket. Torque 9 Nm.
  36. Remove gearshift cable clamp.
  37. Release gearshift cable from clip.
  38. Release hose clamps (x2) securing brake vacuum servo hose and right side ventilation hose to supercharger.
  39. Remove vacuum hoses (X2) secured to supercharger.
  40. Remove evaporation hose secured to supercharger.
  41. Release engine protection reservoir hose from supercharger.
  42. Remove M8x20 bolts (x3) securing clutch damper to bracket. Torque 20 Nm.
  43. Remove clutch pipe fittings (x2) from clutch damper and plug to minimise fluid loss.
  44. Remove M8 bolts (x2) securing clutch slave cylinder to transmission. Torque 12 Nm.
  45. Remove clutch slave cylinder and position aside.
  46. Remove M8x20 bolt securing ground cable to transmission
  47. Disengage hose clamp securing hose to oil pump.
  48. Remove hose from oil pump.
  49. Remove bolts (x2) securing downpipe to bank A exhaust manifold. Torque 55 Nm.
  50. Remove nuts (x2) securing downpipe to bank B exhaust manifold. Torque 55 Nm.

    51. NOTE: Discard all O ring gaskets.

  51. Disconnect hose clamp securing coolant thermostat hose to engine.
  52. Remove M6x16 bolts (x2) securing oil cooler feed and return lines to engine.
  53. Disengage electrical connector securing engine harness to body harness.
  54. Remove oil feed and return lines from engine.
  55. Disengage hose clamp from vacuum pipe.
  56. Remove rear vacuum pipe.
  57. Disengage quick connectors from coolant hoses.

    58. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess coolant.

  58. Remove coolant hoses and push into engine compartment.
  59. Remove M10x27 bolts (x2) securing transmission mount bracket to gearbox. Torque 45 Nm.
  60. Remove transmission mount bracket
  61. Remove cover to access battery ground cable nut.
  62. Remove nut and washers (x3) securing battery and ground cables to rear subframe.

    63. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  63. Remove battery cables (x3) from rear subframe and move aside.
  64. Disengage clip securing fuel feed pipe to radiator inlet hose.
  65. Remove cover from alternator battery cable.
  66. Remove M8 nut securing alternator battery cable to alternator. Torque 24 Nm.
  67. Remove battery cable from alternator and move aside.
  68. Release quick connectors (x3) from transmission oil cooler hoses.

    69. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  69. Disengage hoses and move aside.
  70. Release quick connector from fuel evaporation hose.

    71. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  71. Disengage fuel feed pipe and move aside.

    72. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

  72. Remove fuel evaporation hose and move aside.
  73. Disengage clips (x2) securing engine harness to purge canister bracket.
  74. Disengage clip securing engine harness to engine.
  75. Disconnect harness connector from oxygen sensor.
  76. Remove harness and place on top of engine.
  77. Disengage clip securing oxygen sensor connector to heat shield.
  78. Remove M6 nut securing heat shield to cylinder head. Torque 10 Nm.
  79. Remove M6x16 bolt securing heat shield to cylinder head. Torque 10 Nm.
  80. Remove M6x14 bolt securing heat shield to heat shield. Torque 10 Nm.
  81. Remove M8x25 bolt and nut securing heat shield to cylinder head. Torque 24 Nm.
  82. Remove M6x14 bolt from heat shield.
  83. Remove heat shield.
  84. Disengage clips (x2) securing battery cable to bracket and transmission mount.
  85. Remove harness and move aside.
  86. Remove M12x25 bolts (x4) securing transmission mount to rear subframe. Torque 45 Nm.
  87. Remove transmission mount assembly.
  88. Install lifting hook A132E6182S to rear right side of cylinder head.
  89. Install lifting hook A132E6183S to front left side of cylinder head.
  90. Install engine balancer to engine lifting hooks.
  91. Install engine crane to engine balancer.
  92. Use engine crane to support engine.
  93. Remove M14x80 bolt securing transmission to front engine mount. Torque 135 Nm.
  94. Remove M14x90 bolt and nut securing transmission mount to rear subframe engine mount. Torque 135 Nm.
  95. Use engine crane to slowly lift and remove engine from vehicle.

    96. WARNING: To prevent injury please keep a suitable distance from the engine when it is being removed from the vehicle.

    CAUTION: Take care not to damage any hoses or harnesses during removal.

    NOTE: Place a suitable container under the engine to catch any excess liquids that may escape while removing the engine.

    NOTE: Place engine and transmission on a flat surface and support with wooden blocks.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew all discarded washers.
  3. Renew all discarded gaskets.
