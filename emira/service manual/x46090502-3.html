Oil Pump - V6

Print

Operation Code: 46.09.05-02

    Removal

  1. Remove transmission filter and check valve assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disengage hose clamp securing hose to oil pump.
  3. Remove hose from oil pump.
  4. Remove M5x30 bolts and washers (x4) securing oil pump to bracket. Torque 3 Nm.

    5. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  5. Remove oil pump.

    6. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  6. Remove hose fittings (x2) from oil pump.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Check and adjust the transmission oil level as required. Refer to procedure.
  3. Activate oil pump for 30 seconds using Lotus Insight tool.
  4. Check and adjust the transmission oil level as required. Refer to procedure.
