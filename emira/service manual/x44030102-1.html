Pump - Fuel And Sender Unit

Print

Operation Code: 44.03.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove fuel tank. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M6 nyloc nuts and washers (x6) securing fuel pump locking ring to fuel tank.

    3. CAUTION: Apply a suitable lithium based lubricant such as Castrol Moly Grease to threads of the fuel pump retaining studs to prevent ‘Galling’.

    NOTE: Remove nuts in a diagonal sequence.

  3. Remove fuel pump locking ring.
  4. Remove fuel pump and sender unit from fuel tank.

    5. NOTE: Tilt the fuel pump during removal to prevent the float from fouling on the fuel tank.

  5. Remove and discard fuel pump seal.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew fuel pump seal.
  3. Install original M6 nyloc nuts and washers (x6) securing fuel pump locking ring to fuel tank. Torque 2 Nm.

    4. CAUTION: Apply a suitable lithium based lubricant such as Castrol Moly Grease to threads of the fuel pump retaining studs to prevent ‘Galling’.

    CAUTION: Ensure clamp ring is flush against top of fuel pump flange.

    NOTE: Install nuts in a diagonal sequence.

  4. Whilst using finger pressure to hold the fuel pump in position, remove and discard the original M6 nyloc nuts and washers (x6).
  5. Remove fuel pump locking ring and inspect fuel pump seal fitment.

    6. NOTE: Continue using finger pressure to hold fuel pump in position.

  6. Install fuel pump locking ring.

    7. NOTE: Continue using finger pressure to hold fuel pump in position.

  7. Install new nyloc nuts and washers (x6). Torque 2 Nm.

    8. NOTE: Tighten nuts in a diagonal sequence.

    NOTE: Continue using finger pressure to hold fuel pump in position.

  8. Apply a final tightening to nyloc nuts (x6). Torque 3 Nm.

    9. CAUTION: Ensure clamp ring is flush against top of fuel pump flange.

    NOTE: Tighten nuts in a diagonal sequence.
