Washer Jet

Print

Operation Code: 17.21.07-02

    Removal

  1. Open access hatch front. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove hoses (x2) from washer jets.

    3. NOTE: Use a suitable container to catch any spilled washer fluid.

  3. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from washer jets.
  4. Disengage clips (x2) securing washer jets to brackets.
  5. Remove washer jets.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
