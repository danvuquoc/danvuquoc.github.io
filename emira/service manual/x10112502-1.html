Tailgate Hinge - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 10.11.25-02

    Removal

  1. Remove tailgate. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M8x25 bolts (x2) securing tailgate hinge to rear bulkhead. Torque 24 Nm.

    3. NOTE: To aid installation mark position of tailgate hinges to bulkhead.

  3. Remove tailgate hinge from vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. NOTE: Make sure tailgate hinge is aligned correctly during installation. Use position marked during removal procedure.
