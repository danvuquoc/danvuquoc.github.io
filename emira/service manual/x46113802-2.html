Hose - Water Pump To Charge Air Cooler - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 46.11.38-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.
  2. Release clamp securing coolant hose to charge air cooler.
  3. Disconnect coolant hose from charge air cooler.
  4. Allow coolant to drain.

    5. WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain coolant into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  5. Release clamp securing coolant hose to water pump.
  6. Disconnect coolant hose from water pump.
  7. Release clips (x2) securing coolant hose to drain hose and charge air cooler.
  8. Remove coolant hose.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Refill coolant.
  3. Start engine and allow to idle.

    4. NOTE: Run the engine with the reservoir cap off to bleed the system.

  4. Refit coolant reservoir cap.
