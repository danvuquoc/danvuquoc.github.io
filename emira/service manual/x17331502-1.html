Camera - Rear Parking

Print

Operation Code: 17.33.15-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear bumper. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector from rear parking camera.
  3. Release clips (x2) securing rear parking camera to rear bumper.
  4. Remove rear parking camera.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Apply 3M PT100 25x1.1 mm double coated foam tape to sides of rear parking camera.
