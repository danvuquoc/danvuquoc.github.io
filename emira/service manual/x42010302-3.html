Snorkel / Ducting Air Intake - V6

Print

Operation Code: 42.01.03-02

    Removal

  1. Remove airbox assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear wheel arch liner left side. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove M4x8 self tapping screws (x2) securing side vent grill to rear fender.
  4. Disengage and remove cold air intake hose from intake duct.
  5. Remove M4x8 self tapping screws (x2) securing side vent grill to rear fender.
  6. Disengage clips (x4) and remove side vent grille from rear fender.
  7. Remove M6x16 bolts (x2) securing intake duct to bracket.
  8. Remove outer intake duct moulding.
  9. Remove M4x8 self tapping screw securing side vent grille to vehicle.
  10. Remove M6 nut securing intake duct to vehicle.
  11. Remove intake duct.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
