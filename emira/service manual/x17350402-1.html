ECU - Airbag Control

Print

Operation Code: 17.35.04-02

    Removal

  1. Remove center console assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from airbag control ECU.
  3. Remove M8x12 bolts (x2) securing airbag control ECU to bracket. Torque 24 Nm.
  4. Remove M8x12 bolts (x2) securing airbag control ECU to bracket. Torque 24 Nm.
  5. Remove airbag control ECU.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
