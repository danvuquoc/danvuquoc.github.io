Gear Knob - V6

Print

Operation Code: 47.05.07-02

    Removal

  1. Loosen but do not fully remove M5x16 grub screw securing gear knob to gear lever.
  2. Remove gear knob.

    3. NOTE: Spring and seal may come away with the gear knob.

    Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  3. Remove spring and seal assembly from gear lever.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Fully tighten gear knob then loosen until the grub screw is aligned with the slot on the gear lever.
