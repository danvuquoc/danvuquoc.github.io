Wheelarch Liner - Rear - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 12.01.03-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear road wheel left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove scrivets (x10) securing wheelarch liner to vehicle.
  3. Remove scrivets (x12) securing wheel arch liner to vehicle.
  4. Remove wheelarch liner from vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
