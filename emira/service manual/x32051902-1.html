Upper Steering Column Assembly

Print

Operation Code: 32.05.19-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front undertray. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M8x30 bolt securing upper steering column to steering rack. Torque 24 Nm.
  3. Push steering column gaiter through firewall.
  4. Disengage steering column from steering rack.
  5. Remove steering wheel. Refer to procedure.
  6. Remove drivers stalk module. Refer to procedure.
  7. Remove lower fascia panel. Refer to procedure.
  8. Disengage fir tree clips (x2) securing harness to steering column.
  9. Remove M8x25 bolts (x4) securing steering column to cross car beam. Torque 24 Nm.
  10. Remove M8x25 bolts (x2) securing steering column to cross car beam. Torque 24 Nm.
  11. Remove steering column assembly.

    12. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  12. Remove M8x25 bolts (x4) and M8 nuts (x4) securing steering column mounts to steering column assembly. Torque 24 Nm.
  13. Remove steering column mounts.
  14. Remove steering column gaiter.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
