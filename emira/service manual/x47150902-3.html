CV Joint and Shaft Assembly - Left Side - V6

Print

Operation Code: 47.15.09-02

    Removal

  1. Remove CV joint inboard gaiter. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove clips (x2) securing CV joint gaiter to driveshaft and remove or pull gaiter away from CV joint.
  3. Clean CV joint spider and degrease using suitable solvent.

    4. NOTE: Inspect condition of grease for contamination before cleaning.

    NOTE: Inspect the spider bearings for any significant wear within the driving surface. Discard and replace if necessary.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. If necessary renew outboard CV joint and driveshaft assembly.
