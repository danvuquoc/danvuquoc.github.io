Bumper - Rear

Print

Operation Code: 10.23.15-02

    Removal

  1. Remove Centre High Mounted Stop Lamp (CHMSL). Refer to procedure.
  2. Apply tape to protect paintwork of adjoining panels before removing the bumper.
  3. Release straps (x2) and remove vehicle tool kit.
  4. Remove luggage floor carpet.
  5. Disengage clips (x8) securing rear boot trim to boot box.
  6. Refer to illustration for trim clip locations.
  7. Remove rear boot trim.
  8. Remove M6x30 bolts (x4) securing rear bumper to rear panel. Torque 5 Nm.
  9. Remove scrivets (x4) securing rear bumper to centre mounting.
  10. Remove diffuser - rear. Refer to procedure.
  11. Remove wheelarch liner - left side. Refer to procedure.
  12. Remove wheelarch liner - right side.
  13. Disconnect electrical connector (x2) connecting rear bumper harness.
  14. Remove M5x20 screw securing rear bumper to left side rear bumper bracket. Torque 5 Nm.
  15. Remove M5x20 screw securing rear bumper to right side rear bumper bracket. Torque 5 Nm.
  16. Disengage electrical connector from exhaust valve.
  17. Disengage clips (x4) securing rear bumper to left side rear bumper mounting.
  18. Disengage clips (x4) securing rear bumper to right side rear bumper mounting.
  19. Using assistance pull rear bumper from mounts and away from vehicle.

    20. NOTE: Make sure rear bumper harness does not catch when you remove the rear bumper.

    Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  20. Disengage clips (x4) securing left side air duct to rear bumper.
  21. Remove air duct from rear bumper.
  22. Repeat above procedure for right side air duct.
  23. Disengage harness clips (x16) securing rear bumper harness to bumper and move harness aside.
  24. Remove bolts (x2) securing licence plate to licence plate bracket and remove licence plate.
  25. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing licence plate bracket to rear bumper. Torque 10 Nm.
  26. Remove licence plate bracket from rear bumper lower.
  27. Remove M6x20 bolts and washers (x4) securing bumper lower to bootbox. Torque 5 Nm.
  28. Disengage clips (x16) securing rear bumper lower to rear bumper.
  29. Remove rear bumper from rear bumper lower.

  2. Check fender gap and flush tolerances - see Alignment Specifications for Body Panels and Closures section.
