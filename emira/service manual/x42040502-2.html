Electronic Throttle Actuator - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 42.04.05-02

    Removal

  1. Remove air intake - electronic throttle actuator. Refer to procedure.
  2. Release clip securing harness to throttle actuator.

    3. WARNING: Allow engine to cool completely before proceeding to prevent injury.

  3. Disconnect harness connector from throttle actuator.
  4. Remove screws (x2) securing throttle actuator to charge air distributor. Torque 6 Nm.
  5. Remove throttle actuator.

    6. NOTE: Remove and discard throttle actuator gasket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded throttle actuator gasket.
  3. Perform a diagnostic read and clear memory using Lotus Insight tool.
