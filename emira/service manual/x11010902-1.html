Door Cheater Seal - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 11.01.09-02

    Removal

  1. Open door.
  2. Disengage clips (x3) securing cheater seal to vehicle.
  3. Remove cheater seal.
  4. Remove adhesive securing cheater seal to vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Clean surface and apply new adhesive strip.
