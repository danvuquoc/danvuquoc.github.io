Battery

Print

Operation Code: 17.31.01-02

    Removal

  1. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M6x12 bolts (x2) securing battery clamp to battery support panel. Torque 8 - 10 Nm.
  3. Remove battery clamp.
  4. Remove battery through access panel.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
