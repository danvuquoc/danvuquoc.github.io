Oil Filter Housing / Cooler Assembly - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.15.26-02

Important Information

WARNING: Engine oil is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested. Wear protective gloves, protective clothing and safety glasses.

    Removal

  1. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove M6 bolt (9) securing coolant hose to engine. Torque 9 Nm.
  5. Remove oil drain plug (2) from oil filter housing (1). Torque 20 Nm.

    6. NOTE: Remove and discard seal.

  6. Allow engine oil to drain.

    7. NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  7. Release clips (3) securing harness to oil filter housing.
  8. Remove bolts (4) (x2) securing coolant hoses (5) to oil filter housing. Torque 8 Nm.
  9. Disconnect coolant hoses from oil filter housing.

    10. NOTE: Remove and discard O-rings.

    NOTE: Plug exposed connections to prevent ingress of dirt.

  10. Remove M6 bolts (6) (x2) securing oil filter housing to bracket. Torque 9 Nm.
  11. Remove bolts (7) (x4) securing oil filter housing to engine. Torque 20 Nm.
  12. Remove oil filter housing.

    13. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  13. Remove oil filter cover (8) with oil filter. Torque 25 Nm.

    14. NOTE: Remove and discard seal.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded O-rings and seals.
  3. Check and top up engine oil.
