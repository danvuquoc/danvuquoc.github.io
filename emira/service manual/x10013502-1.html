Bootbox Access Panel

Print

Operation Code: 10.01.35-02

    Removal

  1. Remove luggage compartment floor carpet. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove battery cover. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove M6 nut and trim clips (x4) securing boot trim to bootbox.
  4. Refer to image for clips and nut location.
  5. Remove boot trim.
  6. Remove M6x12 bolts (x6) securing access panel to bootbox. Torque 9 Nm.
  7. Remove access panel aperture.
  8. Remove M6x14 bolts (x4) securing access panel to bootbox. Torque 10 Nm.
  9. Remove access panel.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
