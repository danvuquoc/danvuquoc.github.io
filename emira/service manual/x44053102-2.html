Filter - Leak Detection - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 44.05.31-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Release quick connector securing outlet hose to Diagnostic Module - Tank Leakage (DMTL).

    3. NOTE: Pinch the two buttons on the quick connector and pull to remove.

  3. Disconnect outlet hose from DMTL.
  4. Release filter cover from DMTL.
  5. Remove filter cover.
  6. Remove filter.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal.

    2. NOTE: Push quick connector until and audible click is heard.
