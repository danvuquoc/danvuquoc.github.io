Road Spring - Left Side - Rear

Print

Operation Code: 31.03.44-02

    Removal

  1. Remove spring / damper assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M12x70 bolt and nut securing damper assembly to top mount. Torque 86 Nm.

    3. WARNING: It is required to use a road spring compressor tool to prevent injury.

    NOTE: During installation, make sure the upper damper mounting bolt is oriented so the head of the bolt is fitted to the longer part of the mounting bracket.

  3. Remove spring and damper assembly from top mount.
  4. Remove road spring from damper.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
