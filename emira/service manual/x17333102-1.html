Radar - Forward Looking

Print

Operation Code: 17.33.31-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front bumper. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector from radar.
  3. Remove M5 nuts (x3) securing cover to bracket. Torque 8 Nm.
  4. Remove cover.
  5. Release clips (x2) securing radar to bracket.
  6. Remove radar.
