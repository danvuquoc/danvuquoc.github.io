Rear Lamp Assembly - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 17.11.33-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear fender assembly left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M8x16 bolt and washer securing rear lamp assembly to rear fender. Torque 24 Nm.
  3. Remove M6x12 bolt securing rear lamp assembly to rear fender.
  4. Remove M5 nut securing rear lamp assembly to rear fender. Torque 6 Nm.
  5. Remove rear lamp assembly.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
