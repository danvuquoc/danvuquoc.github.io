Antenna - LTE

Print

Operation Code: 17.27.40-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear quarter trim panel. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector from antenna.
  3. Remove antenna.

    4. Antenna is secured using PT1100 acrylic tape.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Clean surface to remove excess adhesive.
  3. Renew PT1100 acrylic tape.
