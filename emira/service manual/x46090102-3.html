Oil Cooler - V6

Print

Operation Code: 46.09.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear bulkhead access panel. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disengage hose clamps (x2) securing oil hoses to oil cooler.
  3. Remove oil hoses (x2) from oil cooler.
  4. Disengage quick connectors (x2) securing coolant hoses to oil cooler.
  5. Remove coolant hoses (x2) from oil cooler.

    6. WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain coolant into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  6. Remove M5 nyloc nuts and washers (x4) securing oil cooler to vehicle. Torque 8 Nm.

    7. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  7. Remove oil cooler.

    8. CAUTION: Take car when removing the oil cooler as any excess fluid can cause damage to interior components.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Refill coolant.
  3. Start engine and allow to idle.

    4. NOTE: Run the engine with the reservoir cap off to bleed the system.

  4. Refit coolant reservoir cap.
  5. Check transmission oil level. Refer to procedure.
