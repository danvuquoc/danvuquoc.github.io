Sunvisor - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 15.05.03-02

    Removal

  1. Pull sunvisor down and release right side from supporting clip.
  2. Remove trim cover.
  3. Remove M6x14 bolts (x3) securing sunvisor to roof panel. Torque 10 Nm.
  4. Lower sunvisor from headliner until harness connector is visible.
  5. Disconnect harness connector.
  6. Remove sunvisor.
  7. Remove sunvisor supporting clip from headliner.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
