Duct Radiator Outlet - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 46.04.05-02

    Removal

  1. Open access hatch - front. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove quarter turn fasteners (x4) securing the radiator duct to adjoining panels.
  3. Disengage radiator duct lower edge first then disengage upper edge from cowl panel.
  4. Remove duct from vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
