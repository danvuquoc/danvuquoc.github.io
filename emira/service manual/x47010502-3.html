Slave Cylinder - V6

Print

Operation Code: 47.01.05-02

    Removal

  1. Remove starter motor. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove bolts (x2) securing starter motor adapter to engine.
  3. Remove starter motor adapter.
  4. Use hose clamp on clutch hose to limit fluid loss.
  5. Remove clutch hose from slave cylinder.
  6. Remove bolts (x2) securing clutch slave cylinder. Torque 12 Nm.
  7. Remove clutch slave cylinder.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Bleed clutch.
  3. Check and top up brake fluid level.
