Battery Cover

Print

Operation Code: 17.31.06-02

    Removal

  1. Open tailgate.
  2. Pull back trunk carpet to gain access to battery cover.
  3. Remove the nuts (x2) securing battery cover to trunk inner trim.
  4. Carefully pull right side of battery cover to disengage studs.
  5. Remove battery cover from vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
