Grille Frame - Front Access Panel

Print

Operation Code: 12.03.34-02

    Removal

  1. Open front access panel. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect washer hose from left and right side washer jets.
  3. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from left side and right side washer jets.
  4. Remove M6x25 screws and washers (x2) securing grille frame to gutter panel. Torque 5 Nm.
  5. Remove M6x16 screws and washers (x4) securing grille frame to gutter panel.
  6. Unclip the grille frame from the gutter panel and remove from vehicle.
  7. Refer to illustration for clip type and location.

    8. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  8. Release clips (x2) securing washer jets to grille frame.
  9. Remove washer jets.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
