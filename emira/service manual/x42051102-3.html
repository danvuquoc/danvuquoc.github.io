Fuel Injectors - V6

Print

Operation Code: 42.05.11-02

    Removal

  1. Remove fuel rail. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove fuel injectors (x6) from fuel rail.

    3. NOTE: Cover injector ports to prevent ingress.

    NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

    NOTE: Remove the O-rings (x6) from the injectors.

  3. Remove and discard insulators (x6) from intake manifold.

    Installation.

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew insulators (x6).
  3. Apply light coat of spindle oil or gasoline to O ring going to fuel rail.

    4. CAUTION: Be careful not to twist the O-rings.

  4. Push and turn fuel injectors (x6) to install into fuel delivery pipes.

    5. CAUTION: Check injectors turn smoothly. If not reinstall with new O-rings.

    NOTE: Position fuel injector connectors outward.
