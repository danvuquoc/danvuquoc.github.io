Anti-Roll Bar Drop Link Front - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 31.01.36-02

    Removal

  1. Remove road wheel - front - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove and discard M12 nuts (x2) securing drop link to upper wishbone and anti-roll bar. Torque 36 Nm.

    3. NOTE: Insert 5mm hex bit into ball pin stud to prevent anti roll bar drop link ball joint from spinning during removal / installation.

  3. Remove drop link from upper wishbone and anti-roll bar.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
