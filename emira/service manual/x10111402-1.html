Tailgate

Print

Operation Code: 10.11.14-02

    Removal

  1. Open tailgate.
  2. Disconnect tailgate harness and remove harness clips (x2) from bulkhead.
  3. Disengage tailgate harness from right side tailgate hinge bracket.
  4. With assistance support weight of tailgate.
  5. Unclip left and right side tailgate struts from tailgate.

    6. NOTE: Only disconnect tailgate strut from tailgate panel. Do not fully remove strut from vehicle.

  6. Remove M8x25 bolts (x4) securing tailgate to tailgate hinges. Torque 24 Nm.

    7. NOTE: To aid installation mark position of tailgate to tailgate hinges.

  7. Remove tailgate from vehicle.

    8. NOTE: Place tailgate on a suitable surface to prevent damage.

    Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  8. Remove M8x25 bolts and washers (x2) securing tailgate striker to tailgate. Torque 25 Nm.

    9. NOTE: To aid installation mark position of tailgate striker to tailgate panel.

    NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of shims and washers.

  9. Remove tailgate striker.
  10. Remove bump stops (x2).
  11. Release adhesive securing tailgate glass trims (x2) to tailgate.

    12. NOTE: The tailgate glass trim is retained using adhesive strips.

  12. Remove tailgate glass trims (x2).
  13. Using suitable equipment, cut adhesive securing tailgate glass to tailgate.
  14. Lift tailgate glass to access heated screen connectors.
  15. Disconnect tailgate heated screen from tailgate.

    16. NOTE: Reconnect heated screen connectors before bonding glass to tailgate.

  16. Using assistance remove tailgate glass.

    17. NOTE: If glass is to be reused place on suitable surface to prevent damage.

  17. Remove rubber spacers (x8) from tailgate.

    18. NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of spacers.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. NOTE: Make sure tailgate is aligned correctly during installation. Use position marked during removal procedure.

    NOTE: Only fully tighten bolts when you have confirmed the panel alignment is correct.

  2. Inspect heated screen trace lines for damage.
  3. Remove old adhesive from tailgate.
  4. Clean tailgate mating surface using betaclean 3300.
  5. Apply betaprime 5404 primer to tailgate aperture.
  6. Apply betaseal 1580 to tailgate aperture.
  7. Install rubber spacers (x8) into adhesive.
  8. Clean area of glass around tailgate glass heating element using betaclean 3300.
  9. Using assistance install tailgate glass to tailgate.

    10. NOTE: Reconnect heated screen connectors before bonding glass to tailgate.

  10. Check gap and flush tolerances - see Alignment Specifications for Body Panels and Closures section.
