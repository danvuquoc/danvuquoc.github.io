Cable - Crossgate - V6

Print

Operation Code: 47.05.04-02

    Removal

  1. Remove airbox assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M5 nut and washer securing crossgate cable to selector assembly.

    3. NOTE: Record position and quantity of washers.

  3. Remove crossgate cable from selector assembly.
  4. Remove M8x30 bolts (x2) securing crossgate cable to bracket. Torque 16 Nm.
  5. Remove bracket from crossgate cable.
  6. Remove centre console assembly. Refer to procedure.
  7. Remove M5 nyloc nut securing crossgate cable to crossgate lever. Torque 5 Nm.
  8. Disconnect crossgate cable rod end from crossgate lever.
  9. Remove M6x12 bolt securing crossgate cable clamp to gearshift assembly.
  10. Remove crossgate cable clamp.
  11. Remove fuel tank shear panel. Refer to procedure.
  12. Withdraw crossgate cable from engine bay.
  13. Remove M6x25 bolt securing crossgate cable bracket to vehicle. Torque 10 nm.
  14. Remove crossgate cable from adhesive strips on fuel tank.
  15. Withdraw crossgate cable from cabin through aperture.
  16. Remove crossgate cable.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Check and adjust crossgate cable. Refer to procedure.
