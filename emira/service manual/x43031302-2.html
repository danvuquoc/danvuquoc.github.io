Purge Solenoid Valve - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 43.03.13-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector (1) from purge solenoid valve (Y58/1).
  3. Release quick connector (2) and disconnect purge line from purge solenoid valve.

    4. CAUTION: Do not crush, kink or subject purge line to tension.

    NOTE: Pinch the two buttons on quick connector and pull to remove.

    NOTE: Plug quick connector to prevent ingress of dirt.

  4. Release purge solenoid valve with decoupling element (3) from purge line (4) and bracket (5).

    5. NOTE: Plug purge line to prevent ingress of dirt.

  5. Remove purge solenoid valve.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. NOTE: Push quick connector until an audible click is heard.

  2. After installation perform a diagnostic read and clear error memory using Lotus Insight tool.
