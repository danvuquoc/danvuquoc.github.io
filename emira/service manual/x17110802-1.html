Centre High Mounted Stop Lamp (CHMSL)

Print

Operation Code: 17.11.08-02

    Removal

  1. Remove trim panel - luggage compartment - rear. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove M6x25 bolts (x4) securing CHMSL to rear bumper. Torque 10 Nm.
  3. Lift CHMSL away from vehicle to gain access to electrical connector and disconnect harness.
  4. Remove CHMSL from vehicle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
