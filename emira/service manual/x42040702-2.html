Gasket - Air Intake to Electronic Throttle Actuator - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 42.04.07-02

    Removal

  1. Remove charge air cooler. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear bulkhead access panel. Refer to procedure.
  3. Release clamp securing breather hose to air intake.
  4. Disconnect breather hose from air intake.
  5. Remove screws (x2) securing air intake to electronic throttle actuator. Torque 6 Nm.
  6. Remove air intake.
  7. Remove and discard gasket.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded air intake gasket.
