Licence Plate Lamp

Print

Operation Code: 17.11.10-02

    Removal

  1. Disengage clips (x2) securing licence plate lamp(s) to rear bumper.
  2. Remove licence plate lamp(s) from rear bumper.
  3. Disconnect harness connector(s) from licence plate lamp(s).

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
