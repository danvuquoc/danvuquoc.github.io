Fan Assembly - Engine Bay Cooling - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 46.05.09-02

    Removal

  1. Remove fan inlet hose. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear bulkhead access panel. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove M8x20 bolts (x2) securing fan assembly to rear bulkhead.
  4. Remove M8x20 bolt securing fan assembly to rear bulkhead.

    5. NOTE: Access bolt through rear bulkhead access panel.

  5. Disconnect harness connector from fan assembly.
  6. Remove fan assembly.

    7. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  7. Release clamp securing fan outlet hose to fan.
  8. Remove fan outlet hose from fan.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
