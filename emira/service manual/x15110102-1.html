Rear Quarter Trim - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 15.11.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear bulkhead trim. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove B pillar trim. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove M6x20 bolts (x3) securing rear quarter trim to rear bulkhead. Torque 7 Nm.
  4. Remove M5x16 bolt securing rear quarter trim to bodyside panel. Torque 4 Nm.
  5. Carefully pull rear quarter trim to disengage clips (x5) securing trim panel to bodyside panel.
  6. Remove seatbelt from rear quarter trim.
  7. Remove rear quarter trim.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
