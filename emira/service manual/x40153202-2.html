Hose - Engine to Oil Filter Housing - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.15.32-02

Important Information

WARNING: Risk of scalding. Allow coolant to cool completely before proceeding.

WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    Removal

  1. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  4. Release spring clamp securing coolant hose to engine.
  5. Disconnect coolant hose from engne.
  6. Remove M6 bolts (x2) securing coolant hose to engine. Torque 9 Nm.
  7. Release spring clamp securing coolant hose to coolant switchover valve.
  8. Disconnect coolant hose from coolant switchover valve.
  9. Remove bolt securing coolant hose to oil filter housing. Torque 8 Nm.
  10. Disconnect coolant hose from oil filter housing.

    11. NOTE: Be prepared to catch any excess fluid.

    NOTE: Remove and discard O-ring.

  11. Remove coolant hose.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded O-ring.
