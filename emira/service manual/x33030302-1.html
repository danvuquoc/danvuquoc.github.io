Hose - Front - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 33.03.03-02

    Removal

  1. Remove front wheel arch liner left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disengage harness from brake hose bracket.
  3. Remove M6x12 bolt securing brake hose bracket to upper wishbone. Torque 8 Nm.
  4. Remove brake hose banjo bolt from brake caliper. Torque 24 Nm.
  5. Remove brake hose sealing washers (x2).

    6. CAUTION: Brake fluid will discharge from brake hose when removing banjo bolt. Use a container to collect brake fluid.

    CAUTION: Do not allow brake fluid to contact lower arm ball joint.

    NOTE: Always record quantity and fitted position of washers.

  6. Remove M10 brake union nut securing brake hose to brake line. Torque 16 Nm.
  7. Remove brake hose.

    8. CAUTION: Plug pipe connections to prevent fluid loss and ingress of moisture or dirt.

    CAUTION: If brake fluid is spilt on the paintwork wash off immediately with clean water.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:

    2. NOTE: Make sure the brake hose is free of kinks during installation.

  2. Renew brake hose sealing washers.
  3. Bleed brakes using Lotus Insight tool.
