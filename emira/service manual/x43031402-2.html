Bleed Line - Air Intake Pipe - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 43.03.14-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Release connector and disconnect purge line from bleed line.

    3. NOTE: Plug purge line to prevent ingress of dirt.

  3. Release clip securing purge pipe to bleed pipe.
  4. Release bleed pipe from engine harness.
  5. Remove screw securing bleed pipe to air intake pipe. Torque 9 Nm.
  6. Disconnect bleed line from air intake pipe.
  7. Release quick connector and disconnect bleed line from throttle air intake.

    8. NOTE: Pinch the two buttons on the quick connector and pull to remove.

  8. Release clip securing bleed line to purge line.
  9. Disconnect bleed line connections (x2) from charge air distributor.

    10. NOTE: Plug charge air distributor to prevent ingress of dirt.

  10. Remove bleed line.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.

    2. NOTE: Push quick connector until an audible click is heard.
