Rain - Solar Sensor

Print

Operation Code: 17.33.23-02

    Removal

  1. Disengage clips (x2) securing rear mirror cover to mirror cover trim.
  2. Remove rear mirror cover.
  3. Disengage clips (x4) securing rear mirror cover trim to windscreen.
  4. Remove rear mirror cover trim.
  5. Disconnect harness connector from rain sensor.
  6. Disengage retaining clip securing rain sensor to windscreen.
  7. Remove rain sensor.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
