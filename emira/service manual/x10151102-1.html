Door Hinge - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 10.15.11-02

    Removal

  1. Remove door. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove front wheel arch liner left side. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove self tapping screws (x4) securing front fender garnish to fender.
  4. Remove fender garnish.
  5. Remove M6x20 bolts (x2) securing front fender bracket to vehicle and move lower part of fender aside to access door hinge bolt. Torque 10 Nm.
  6. Remove M8x30 bolts (x4) securing door hinges to vehicle.
  7. Remove door hinges (x2).

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
