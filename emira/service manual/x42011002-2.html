Hose - Engine - Fresh Air Inlet - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 42.01.10-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Release clamp securing engine fresh air inlet hose to airbox. Torque 3 Nm.
  3. Release clamp securing engine fresh air inlet hose to clean air intake pipe. Torque 2 Nm.
  4. Remove engine fresh air inlet hose.

    5. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  5. Remove clamps (x2) from engine fresh air inlet hose.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
