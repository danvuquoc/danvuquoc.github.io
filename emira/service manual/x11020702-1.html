Window Lift Mechanism - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 11.02.07-02

    Removal

  1. Remove door glass left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove grommets from bottom of door to access bolts.
  3. Remove M6x16 bolts (x2) securing window lift mechanism to door. Torque 7 Nm.
  4. Remove M6 nuts (x2) securing window lift mechanism to door. Torque 3.5 Nm.

    5. NOTE: Restrain studs with screwdriver whilst tightening nuts.

  5. Manually operate window lift mechanism.
  6. Remove window lift mechanism.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
