Oil Drain - Refill - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.15.25-02

Important Information

WARNING: Engine oil is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested. Wear protective gloves, protective clothing and safety glasses.

    Drain

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove oil filler cap.
  3. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove oil drain plug from sump and discard oil drain plug seal.
  5. Allow engine oil to drain.

    6. NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

    Refill

  1. Renew discarded oil drain plug seal.
  2. Install oil drain plug. Torque 30 Nm.
  3. Fill engine with oil.

    4. NOTE: Service fill volume 6.0 litres.

    NOTE: Dry fill volume 6.7 litres.

  4. Install oil filler cap.
  5. Install rear undertray.
  6. Run engine for a short period until warm.

    7. NOTE: Engine oil temperature must be at least 70 °C.

  7. With engine still running, check engine oil level using vehicle health widget on driver display.

    8. NOTE: The oil level symbol with the oil level status is displayed, messages are also displayed stating oil capacity and if any action is required.

    NOTE: Determining oil level can take up to 30 minutes after driving vehicle.

  8. Top up oil level if necessary.
  9. Install engine cover.
