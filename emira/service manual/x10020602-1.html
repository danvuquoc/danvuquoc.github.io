Sill Finisher - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 10.02.06-02

    Removal

  1. Open door.
  2. Remove sill finisher decorative plate secured to sill finisher.

    3. WARNING: Wear correct PPE when removing sill finisher to prevent injury.

    NOTE: The decorative plate is retained using adhesive.

  3. Remove M6x20 bolts (x3) securing finisher to outer door sill panel.
  4. Disengage clips (x2) securing sill finisher to outer door sill panel.
  5. Remove sill finisher.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
