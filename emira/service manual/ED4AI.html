CHASSIS RIDE HEIGHT

Measuring Ride Height

Ride height is measured from the ground up to the machined tooling holes located in the chassis siderails (highlighted and shaded in yellow).

Measurements should be taken from with the vehicle at mid-laden weight (equivalent of 2 x 75 kg occupants + full fuel tank).

Before measuring and adjusting any vehicle geometry, the tires must be inflated to the specified pressures and the front and rear ride heights checked and adjusted.
