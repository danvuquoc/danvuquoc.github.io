Timing Chain Cover - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.08.09-02

    Removal

  1. Remove water pump assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove cam cover gasket left side. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove cam cover gasket right side. Refer to procedure.
  4. Remove upper oil sump. Refer to procedure.
  5. Remove bolts (x23) securing timing chain cover to engine block.
    Torque 21 Nm.
    Bolt A. Torque 43 Nm.

    6. CAUTION: Make sure there is no oil on the bolts before installation.

    NOTE: Tighten bolts in order of area 1, 2, 3, 4.

  6. Remove nuts (x2) securing timing chain cover to engine block. Torque 21 Nm.
  7. Remove timing chain cover.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Clean mating surfaces.
  3. Apply seal packing. Refer to procedure.
