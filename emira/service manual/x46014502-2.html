Auxiliary Radiator - Right Side - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 46.01.45-02

    Removal

  1. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove front bumper. Refer to procedure.
  3. Remove scrivets (x6) and remove front air duct.
  4. Release clamps (x2) securing auxiliary radiator inlet hoses to auxiliary radiators.
  5. Disconnect auxiliary radiator inlet hoses (x2) from auxiliary radiators.
  6. Release clamp securing auxiliary radiator outlet hose to radiator.
  7. Disconnect auxiliary radiator outlet hose from auxiliary radiator.
  8. Release clamp securing auxiliary radiator outlet hose to auxiliary radiator.
  9. Disconnect auxiliary radiator outlet hose from auxiliary radiator.
  10. Remove M6x16 bolts (x7) and remove bracket. Torque 10 Nm.
  11. Remove M6x16 bolts (x9) and remove bracket from radiator. Torque 10 Nm.
  12. Remove foam strips (x4).
  13. Remove auxiliary radiator.
  14. Remove foam strips (x4).
  15. Remove auxiliary radiator.

    16. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  16. Remove bushes (x4) from radiator.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Refill coolant.
