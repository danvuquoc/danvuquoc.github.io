Air Quality Sensor - V6

Print

Operation Code: 17.06.39-02

    Removal

  1. Remove radiator outlet duct left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove radiator outlet duct right side.

    3. NOTE: Procedure is the same for left side component.

  3. Remove scrivets (x3) securing HVAC air inlet to vehicle.
  4. Remove M5x12 bolts (x2) and washers (x2) securing HVAC air inlet to vehicle.
  5. Remove HVAC air inlet.
  6. If fitted, remove water shield from HVAC unit. Refer to TSB-131-24007.
  7. Disconnect harness connector from air quality sensor.
  8. Remove air quality sensor.

    9. NOTE: Turn sensor 90 degrees counter clockwise to release.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. After installation perform a diagnostic read and clear error memory using Lotus Insight tool.
