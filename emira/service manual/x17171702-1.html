Lamp - Door Puddle - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 17.17.17-02

    Removal

  1. Remove door trim - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector from door puddle lamp.
  3. Release clips (x4) securing door puddle lamp to door trim.
  4. Remove door puddle lamp.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
