Hose Assembly - Pressure - Front - V6

Print

Operation Code: 32.03.04-02

    Removal

  1. Remove radiator outlet duct - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove radiator outlet duct - right side.

    3. NOTE: Procedure is the same as for left side component.

  3. Remove scrivets (x3) securing HVAC air inlet to vehicle.
  4. Remove M5x12 screws and washers (x2) securing HVAC air inlet to filter lid.
  5. Remove HVAC air inlet.
  6. If fitted, remove water shield from HVAC unit. Refer to TSB-131-24007.
  7. Remove scrivets (x6) securing radiator centre outlet duct to vehicle.
  8. Remove radiator centre outlet duct.
  9. Remove M6x20 bolt securing front hose to vehicle. Torque 8 Nm.
  10. Loosen union nut securing front hose to cooler assembly pipe. Torque 45 Nm.
  11. Disconnect front hose from cooler assembly pipe.

    12. WARNING: Power steering fluid is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  12. Remove wheelarch liner front left side. Refer to procedure.
  13. Remove M6x20 bolts (x3) securing brackets (x2) to front subframe. Torque 8 Nm.
  14. Remove M6 nut securing bracket to front subframe. Torque 8 Nm.
  15. Release front hose from brackets (x2).
  16. Loosen union nut securing front hose to centre hose. Torque 45 Nm.
  17. Disconnect front hose from centre hose.

    18. WARNING: Power steering fluid is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  18. Release clip securing front harness to vehicle and position aside.
  19. Release hood stay from bracket, manoeuvre front hose away from stay and secure to bracket again.
  20. Remove front hose through front wheelarch.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Fill power steering system. Refer to technical data.
  3. Bleed power steering system. Refer to procedure.
