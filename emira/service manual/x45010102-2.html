Catalytic Converter - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 45.01.01-02

Important Information

WARNING: Do not attempt this procedure until the engine and exhaust are completely cool.

    Removal

  1. Remove right rear wheelarch liner.

    2. NOTE: Procedure is the same as left side component.

  2. Remove turbocharger assembly. Refer to procedure.
  3. Release union nut securing pre-cat oxygen sensor to catalytic converter. Torque 45 Nm.
  4. Remove pre-cat oxygen sensor.
  5. Loosen clamp bolt and remove exhaust pipe clamp. Torque: 17 Nm.

    6. NOTE: Discard gasket.

  6. Release union nut securing temperature sensor to catalytic converter. Torque 45 Nm.
  7. Remove temperature sensor.
  8. Remove rear silencer. Refer to procedure.
  9. Release clamp securing catalytic converter to exhaust pipe. Torque: 21 Nm.

    10. NOTE: Discard gasket.

  10. Remove exhaust pipe.
  11. Release union nut securing post-cat oxygen sensor to catalytic converter. Torque 45 Nm.
  12. Remove post-cat oxygen sensor.
  13. Release clamps (x2) securing Differential Pressure Sensor (DPS) hoses to catalytic converter.
  14. Disconnect DPS hoses from catalytic converter.
  15. Remove bolts (x2) securing DPS bracket and pipe to catalytic converter.
  16. Remove DPS bracket and pipe.
  17. Release union nut securing DPS sensor to catalytic converter. Torque 45 Nm.
  18. Remove DPS sensor.
  19. Remove M8x20 bolts (x2) securing catalytic converter to engine mounting bracket. Torque 20 Nm.
  20. Remove catalytic converter.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded gaskets.
