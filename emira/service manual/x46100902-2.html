Air Manifold - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 46.10.09-02

    Removal

  1. Remove fresh air inlet hose. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove screws (x3) securing air manifold to engine. Torque 4 Nm + 90 degrees.
  3. Release clamp securing air manifold to solenoid valve.
  4. Disconnect air manifold from solenoid valve.
  5. Release clamp securing air manifold to turbocharger. Torque 4 Nm.
  6. Disconnect air manifold from turbocharger.

    7. NOTE: Plug turbocharger to prevent ingress of dirt.

  7. Release clamp securing air manifold to charge air cooler. Torque 6 Nm.
  8. Disconnect air manifold from charge air cooler.

    9. NOTE: Plug charge air cooler to prevent ingress of dirt.

  9. Remove air manifold.

    10. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  10. Release clamp securing hose to air manifold.
  11. Remove hose.
  12. Remove clips (x6) securing heatshield to air manifold.
  13. Remove heatshield.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
