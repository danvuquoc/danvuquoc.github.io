Door Assembly - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 10.15.07-02

    Removal

  1. Open door and lower window.
  2. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  3. Disengage harness connector securing door harness to body harness.
  4. Remove M8x30 torx bolt securing door check strap to vehicle. Torque 18 Nm.

    5. CAUTION: Using assistance, support door to prevent damage to the door and hinge assemblies.

  5. Remove M8x30 bolts (x2) securing upper door hinge to door. Torque 24 Nm.
  6. Remove M8x30 bolts (x2) securing lower door hinge to door. Torque 24 Nm.
  7. Using assistance remove door.

    8. CAUTION: Place on secure and suitable surface to prevent damage.

    Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  8. Lift door waist seal to access door seal underneath.
  9. Disengage clips (x21) securing door seal to door and remove door seal.
  10. Remove M6x30 bolts (x2) securing door trim to door panel. Torque 5 Nm.
  11. Carefully pull door trim upwards then away from door to access connections.

    12. NOTE: Only pull door trim away from door enough to gain access to door trim connections.

  12. Refer to illustration for door trim mounting locations.

    13. NOTE: Door trim insulation has been made transparent for image clarity.

  13. Disconnect harness connector connecting door trim harness to door wiring harness.
  14. Disengage door release outer cable from bracket.
  15. Disengage door release cable from door release handle.
  16. Remove door trim.
  17. Disengage clips (x2) securing door waist seal corner piece to door and remove corner piece.
  18. Remove door waist inner seal secured to inner door.
  19. Remove door waist outer seal secured to outer door.
  20. Disconnect harness connector from window motor.
  21. Remove M5x22 screws (x3) securing window motor to water deflector panel.
  22. Remove window motor from water deflector panel.
  23. Disconnect harness connector from door speaker.
  24. Disconnect harness connector from door pressure sensor.
  25. Disconnect harness connector from door mirror harness.
  26. Disengage clips (x2) securing harness to water deflector panel.
  27. Disengage grommets (x3) from water deflector panel.

    28. NOTE: Push grommets through the panel into the door.

  28. Remove M6x16 bolts (x15) securing water deflector panel to door panel. Torque 5 Nm.
  29. Pull through harness and door release cable as you remove the water deflector panel from the door.
  30. Remove water deflector panel.
  31. Position door glass to gain access to door glass fixings.
  32. Remove screws (x2) securing door glass to regulator.
  33. Carefully release door glass from retaining clamps (x2) on regulator.
  34. Carefully tilt door glass and remove from door.
  35. Remove grommets from bottom of door to access bolts.
  36. Remove M6x16 bolts (x2) securing window lift mechanism to door. Torque 7 Nm.
  37. Remove M6 nuts (x2) securing window lift mechanism to door. Torque 3.5 Nm.

    38. NOTE: Restrain studs with screwdriver whilst tightening nuts.

  38. Manually operate window lift mechanism.
  39. Remove window lift mechanism.
  40. Remove clips (x3) securing door mirror harness to door panel.
  41. Partially remove door corner trim access panel to access door mirror fixings.
  42. Remove M6x20 bolts (x3) securing door mirror to door panel. Torque 10 Nm.

    43. NOTE: When the final bolt is removed support the door mirror so it does not drop unexpectedly.

  43. Carefully pull harness through door panel to remove door mirror from door panel.
  44. Remove door mirror.
  45. Remove M6x20 bolt securing lower window guide rail to door. Torque 7 Nm.
  46. Remove M6x12 bolts (x2) securing upper window guide rail to door inner mounting bracket. Torque 10 Nm.
  47. Remove window guide rail.
  48. Disengage clip securing door latch module pull rod to lock assembly.

    49. NOTE: Only applicable for the driver side door.

  49. Remove pull rod from door latch module.
  50. Disengage exterior door handle release cable from bracket.
  51. Disengage exterior door handle release cable from door latch module lever.
  52. Disconnect harness connector from door latch module.
  53. Remove M6x12 torx bolts (x3) securing door latch module to door panel. Torque 7 Nm.
  54. Remove door latch module.
  55. Disengage clip securing pull rod to lock assembly.

    56. NOTE: Inner door skin not illustrated for clarity.

  56. Disengage release cable from bracket.
  57. Disengage release cable from exterior door handle latch release lever.
  58. Remove M6 nuts (x3) securing exterior door handle to door panel. Torque 10 Nm.
  59. Remove exterior door handle.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew all discarded cable ties.
  3. Perform a function check of the left side door electrical switches and lamps.
  4. Check door gap and flush tolerances - see Alignment Specifications for Body Panels and Closures section.
  5. Check door glass alignment and reset window one touch facility. Refer to procedure.
