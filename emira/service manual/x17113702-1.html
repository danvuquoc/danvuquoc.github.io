Side Repeater Lamp - Left Side

Print

Operation Code: 17.11.37-02

    Removal

  1. Remove door mirror glass - left side. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove screws (x4) securing rear cover to door mirror.
  3. Remove rear cover.
  4. Release clips (x9) securing upper cover to door mirror.
  5. Remove upper cover.
  6. Remove screws (x2) securing side repeater lamp to door mirror.
  7. Disconnect harness connector from side repeater lamp.
  8. Remove side repeater lamp.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
