Hose - Coolant Feed - V6

Print

Operation Code: 46.03.09-02

    Removal

  1. Drain coolant. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove rear undertray. Refer to procedure.
  3. Release clamp securing coolant feed hose to heat exchanger.
  4. Disconnect coolant feed hose from heat exchanger.
  5. Release clamp securing coolant feed hose to oil cooler.
  6. Disconnect coolant feed hose from oil cooler.
  7. Remove coolant feed hose.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
