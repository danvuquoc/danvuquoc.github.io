Charge Air Cooler - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 46.10.01-02

    Removal

  1. Remove AC compressor. Refer to procedure.
  2. Release clamp securing coolant hose to charge air cooler.
  3. Disconnect coolant hose from charge air cooler.
  4. Allow coolant to drain.

    5. WARNING: Coolant is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested.

    NOTE: Drain coolant into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  5. Release clip securing coolant hose to charge air cooler.
  6. Release clamp securing charge air cooler to air intake. Torque 6 Nm.
  7. Release clamp securing charge air cooler to air manifold. Torque 6 Nm.
  8. Release clamp securing coolant hose to charge air cooler.
  9. Disconnect coolant hose from charge air cooler.
  10. Disconnect harness connector from pressure sensor.
  11. Remove M6x20 bolt securing charge air cooler to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  12. Remove M6x20 bolt securing charge air cooler to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  13. Remove M6x20 bolts (x4) securing charge air cooler to engine. Torque 8 Nm.
  14. Remove charge air cooler.

    15. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  15. Remove bushes (x2) and brackets (x2) from charge air cooler.
  16. Remove M6x12 bolt securing bracket to charge air cooler. Torque 8 Nm.
  17. Remove bracket from charge air cooler.
  18. Release clamps (x2) securing hoses (x2) to charge air cooler. Torque 6 Nm.
  19. Remove hoses (x2) from charge air cooler.
  20. Remove screws (x2) securing pressure sensor to charge air cooler.
  21. Remove pressure sensor from charge air cooler.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Refill coolant.
  3. Start engine and allow to idle.

    4. NOTE: Run the engine with the reservoir cap off to bleed the system.

  4. Refit coolant reservoir cap.
