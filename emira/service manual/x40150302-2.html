Oil Filter - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 40.15.03-02

Important Information

WARNING: Engine oil is a toxic substance and can be lethal if ingested. Wear protective gloves, protective clothing and safety glasses.

    Removal

  1. Drain engine oil. Refer to procedure.
  2. Loosen oil filter cover (1) slightly and allow engine oil to drain.

    3. NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  3. Remove oil filter cover.

    4. NOTE: Remove and discard seal (3).

  4. Remove oil filter (2).

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded seal.
