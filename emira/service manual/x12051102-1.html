Badge - Rear Fender Fixing Cover

Print

Operation Code: 12.05.11-02

    Removal

  1. Remove emblem secured to rear fender.

    2. NOTE: The emblem is retained using adhesive strips.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Clean off excess adhesive from fender emblem area and renew adhesive.
  3. Check emblem gap and flush tolerances.
