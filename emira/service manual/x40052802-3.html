Oil Control Valve Pipe - Right Side - V6

Print

Operation Code: 40.05.28-02

    Removal

  1. Remove auxiliary drive belt. Refer to procedure.
  2. Remove bolts (x2) securing power steering pump to engine. Torque 43 Nm.
  3. Lower power steering pump to access valve pipe lower banjo bolt.

    4. NOTE: Place a cloth on top of power steering pump to soak up any oil.

  4. Remove lower banjo bolt securing oil control valve pipe to engine.
  5. Allow oil to drain.

    6. NOTE: Drain oil into a suitable container and dispose of appropriately.

  6. Remove M6x30 bolt securing oil control valve pipe to engine.
  7. Remove banjo bolt securing top of oil control valve pipe to engine.
  8. Remove oil control valve pipe and discard O rings.
  9. Remove gauze filter from oil control valve pipe.

    10. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  10. Remove P clip from oil control valve pipe.

    11. NOTE: Record orientation of P clip

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Install P clip to oil control valve pipe.
  3. Clean gauze filter and install to upper banjo bolt.
  4. Install upper banjo bolt with new O rings to oil control valve hand tight only.
  5. Install lower banjo bolt with new O rings to oil control valve hand tight only.
  6. Ensure correct orientation of oil control valve pipe and tighten banjo bolts (x2). Torque 45 Nm.
  7. Install M6x30 bolt securing oil control valve pipe to engine. Torque 10 Nm.
  8. Check oil level and top up if necessary.
