Antenna - GNSS

Print

Operation Code: 17.27.36-02

    Removal

  1. Remove instrument panel assembly. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector from instrument panel harness.
  3. Disengage harness from defroster outlet.
  4. Pull harness and connector through top of fascia panel.
  5. Disengage adhesive securing GNSS antenna to fascia panel.
  6. Remove GNSS antenna.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
  2. Clean surface and remove any excess adhesive.
