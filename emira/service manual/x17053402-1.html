Overhead Console

Print

Operation Code: 17.05.34-02

    Removal

  1. Open door.
  2. Disconnect battery. Refer to procedure.
  3. Disengage clips (x4) securing overhead console to headlining.
  4. Remove overhead console to access connectors.
  5. Disconnect harness connectors (x2) from overhead console.
  6. Remove overhead console.

    7. Do not carry out further disassembly if component is removed for access only.

  7. Disengage clips (x6) securing harness to overhead console.
  8. Disengage clips (x4) securing motion sensors (x3) to overhead console.
  9. Remove motion sensors(x3).
  10. Disengage clips (x2) securing internal motion sensor module to overhead console.
  11. Remove internal motion sensor module.
  12. Disconnect ribbon cable.
  13. Disengage clips (x2) securing warning cluster to overhead console.
  14. Remove warning cluster.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure.
