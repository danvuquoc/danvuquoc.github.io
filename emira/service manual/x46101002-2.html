Solenoid Valve - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 46.10.10-02

    Removal

  1. Remove engine cover. Refer to procedure.
  2. Disconnect harness connector from solenoid valve.
  3. Remove M6 bolts (1) (x3) securing solenoid valve (Y101) to air intake pipe. Torque 9 Nm.
  4. Remove solenoid valve.

    5. NOTE: Plug air intake pipe to prevent ingress of dirt.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. After installation perform a diagnostic read and clear error memory using Lotus Insight tool.
