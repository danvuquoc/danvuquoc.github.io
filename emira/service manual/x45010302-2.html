Silencer Assembly - 4 Cylinder

Print

Operation Code: 45.01.03-02

    Removal

  1. Remove rear diffuser. Refer to procedure.

    2. WARNING: Allow the engine to cool completely before proceeding.

  2. Loosen clamp securing silencer assembly to catalyst. Torque 60 Nm.
  3. Disconnect harness connector from exhaust valve actuator and move aside.
  4. Remove M8x25 bolts (x2) securing left side exhaust hangers to vehicle. Torque 24 Nm.
  5. Remove M8x25 bolts (x2) securing right side exhaust hangers to vehicle. Torque 24 Nm.
  6. Remove nuts (x2) securing exhaust silencer to silencer flexible mount.

    7. NOTE: Parts shown removed for clarity.

  7. Remove exhaust hanger straps (x2) from vehicle.
  8. Remove silencer assembly.

    9. Do not carry out further disassembly if removing for access only.

  9. Remove exhaust hanger rubbers (x2) from silencer assembly.
  10. Remove and discard torca clamp from silencer assembly.

    11. NOTE: It may be necessary to loosen the nut further in order to remove the clamp.

  11. Remove nuts (x3) and remove valve actuator.

    Installation

  1. Installation is the reverse of removal procedure except for the following:
  2. Renew discarded torca clamp. Torque 60 Nm.
